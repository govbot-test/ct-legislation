Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 96
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT FOR PREMIUM PAYMENTS
FOR CERTAIN LONG-TERM CARE INSURANCE POLICIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 12 of the general statutes be amended to establish a tax
2 credit for the amount of premiums paid to purchase an individual or
3 group long-term care insurance policy that provides benefits for health
4 care provided in an insured's home.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a tax credit for the amount of premiums paid to purchase
an individual or group long-term care insurance policy that provides
benefits for health care provided in an insured's home.
LCO No. 96 1 of 1
