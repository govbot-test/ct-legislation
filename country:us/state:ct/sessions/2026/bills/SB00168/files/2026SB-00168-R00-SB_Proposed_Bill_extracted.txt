General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 168
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 895
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
SEN. MARX, 20th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A SEWER
EXPANSION PROJECT IN THE FITCHVILLE SECTION OF THE TOWN
OF BOZRAH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Public Health for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid
6 to the town of Bozrah to extend sewer lines in the Fitchville section of
7 said town to connect to Norwich Public Utilities.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a sewer expansion project in the Fitchville
section of the town of Bozrah.
