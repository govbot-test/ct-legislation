Statement of Purpose:
To: (1) Revise processes related to applications for, and issuance of,
trade name certificates, (2) provide the Secretary of the State with
additional authority to take actions with respect to fraudulent filings,
deceptive business solicitations and the dissolution of corporations that
fail to comply with state law, (3) revise provisions relating to the
issuance of an apostille, and (4) increase permissible fees that may be
charged by a notary.
