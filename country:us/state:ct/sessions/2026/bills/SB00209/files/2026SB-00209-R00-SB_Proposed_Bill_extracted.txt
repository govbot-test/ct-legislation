General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 209
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 29
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING MEDICAID REIMBURSEMENT RATES FOR
NEPHROLOGY SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 17b of the general statutes be amended to require the
2 Commissioner of Social Services to (1) amend the Medicaid state plan to
3 increase rates of reimbursement for nephrology services to not less than
4 eighty per cent of Medicare rates for the same types of services, and (2)
5 file a report on the impact of the rate increases on provider participation
6 in the Medicaid program, access to care and the state budget.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase Medicaid rates of reimbursement for nephrology services.
