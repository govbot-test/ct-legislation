AN ACT CONCERNING VISITATION POLICIES OF THE DEPARTMENT
OF CORRECTION AND THE TRANSFER OF FUNDS HELD IN TRUST
BY THE DEPARTMENT OF CORRECTION TO PERSONS WHO WERE
FORMERLY INCARCERATED.

Assembly convened:

Statement of Purpose:
To prescribe (1) minimal in-person visitation thresholds for persons
who are incarcerated, and (2) the process for the transfer of funds held
in trust by the Department of Correction to persons who were formerly
incarcerated.
