AN ACT ESTABLISHING A WORKING GROUP TO ADDRESS
ISLAMOPHOBIA IN SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a
2 working group to address Islamophobia in public schools. The working
3 group shall provide assistance and resources to the Department of
4 Education. The Commissioner of Education may provide such
5 assistance and resources to local and regional boards of education to
6 address issues relating to Islamophobia that affect students, families,
7 educators and school personnel. Such assistance and resources may
8 include, but need not be limited to, (1) proposing amendments to school
9 district policies to ensure that all students, educators and school
10 personnel feel safe inside and outside of the school setting, and (2)
11 offering training relating to Islamophobia for educators and
12 administrators.
13 (b) The working group shall consist of the following members:
14 (1) Two appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives,
15 one of whom shall be a representative from a national civil rights
