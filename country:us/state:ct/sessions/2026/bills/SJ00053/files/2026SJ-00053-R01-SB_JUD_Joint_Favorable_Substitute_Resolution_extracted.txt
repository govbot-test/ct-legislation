General Assembly Substitute Senate Joint
Resolution No. 53
February Session, 2026
RESOLUTION VACATING THE DECISION OF THE CLAIMS
COMMISSIONER TO DISMISS THE CLAIM AGAINST THE STATE OF
CAROL FALCONE AND GRANTING THE CLAIMANT PERMISSION TO
SUE THE STATE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number 26384 of
2 said commissioner, ordering the dismissal of the claim against the state
3 of Carol Falcone, is vacated. In accordance with the provisions of
4 subsections (b) and (c) of section 4-159 of the general statutes, the
5 General Assembly grants the claimant permission to sue the state. The
6 claimant's action shall be brought not later than one year from the date
7 of the final adoption by the General Assembly of this resolution.
JUD Joint Favorable Subst.
