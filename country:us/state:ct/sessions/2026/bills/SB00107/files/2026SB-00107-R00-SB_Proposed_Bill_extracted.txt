General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 107
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 789
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
IMPROVEMENTS TO THE ATHLETIC FIELDS FOR REGIONAL
SCHOOL DISTRICT 8.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three million five hundred dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used
5 by the Department of Education for the purpose of providing a grant-
6 in-aid to Regional School District 8 for improvements to the athletic
7 fields.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to Regional School District 8 for improvements to
the athletic fields.
