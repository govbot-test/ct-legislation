AN ACT CONCERNING CHILDREN'S PROGRAMS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Commissioner of Children and
2 Families shall conduct a study to evaluate the efficacy of the programs
3 administered by the Department of Children and Families. The
4 commissioner shall report, in accordance with the provisions of section
5 11-4a of the general statutes, the findings of such study to the joint
6 standing committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of
7 matters relating to children on or before January 1, 2027.
