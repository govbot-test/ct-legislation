2 including a federal or state officer or employee who, under color of any

10 section, if the defendant is held liable, the court may award damages,
11 including, but not limited to, nominal damages, actual damages,
12 compensatory damages, punitive damages, injunctive relief and other
13 appropriate equitable relief, to protect the peaceable exercise or

15 Constitution.

17 if the defendant is held liable, the court shall award the plaintiff
18 reasonable attorney's fees and costs in the court's discretion.
