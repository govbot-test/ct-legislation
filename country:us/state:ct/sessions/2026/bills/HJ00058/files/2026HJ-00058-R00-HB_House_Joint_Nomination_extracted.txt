General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 58
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2207
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE
BARRY F. ARMATA OF SUFFIELD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE
JUDICIAL REVIEW COUNCIL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Barry F. Armata of Suffield to be a member of the Judicial
3 Review Council, as a judge of the Superior Court who is not also a judge
4 of the Supreme Court, in succession to the Honorable Vernon Oliver, to
5 serve a term ending November 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed
6 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
7 confirmed.
