General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 70
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3245
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
KEMP, ESQUIRE, OF HAMDEN TO BE A JUDGE OF THE SUPERIOR
COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Felice N.
2 Gray-Kemp, Esquire, of Hamden to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to
3 serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both
4 Houses, in succession to the Honorable Elpedio N. Vitale, is approved
5 and confirmed, and she is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for
6 said term.
