AN ACT CONVEYING A PARCEL OF STATE LAND TO THE
NORTHWEST RESOURCE RECOVERY AUTHORITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding any provision
2 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Administrative Services
3 shall convey to the Northwest Resource Recovery Authority a parcel of
4 land located in the city of Torrington, and any improvements on such
5 parcel, at a cost equal to the administrative costs of making such
6 conveyance. Said parcel of land has an area of approximately 4 acres
7 and is identified as Lot 002 in Block 001 on city of Torrington Tax
8 Assessor's Map 114 and contains the Torrington Transfer Station. Said
9 parcel was transferred to the Department of Administrative Services
10 pursuant to sections 8 to 15, inclusive, of public act 23-170. The
11 conveyance shall be subject to the approval of the State Properties
12 Review Board.
13 (b) The Northwest Resource Recovery Authority shall use said parcel
14 of land for purposes of operating a public transfer station for municipal
15 solid waste and recycling materials. If the authority:
