RESOLUTION VACATING THE DECISIONS OF THE CLAIMS
COMMISSIONER TO DISMISS THE CLAIMS AGAINST THE STATE OF
KEVIN BERUBE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 Section 1. That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number
2 27029 of said commissioner, ordering the dismissal of the claim against
3 the state of Kevin Berube, is vacated and the claim is remanded to the
4 Claims Commissioner for a hearing on the merits.
5 Sec. 2. That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number
6 26618 of said commissioner, ordering the dismissal of the claim against
7 the state of Kevin Berube, is vacated and the claim is remanded to the
8 Claims Commissioner for a hearing on the merits.
