EMPLOYEES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) Not later than July 1, 2027,
2 the state shall provide uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage
3 under the State Fleet Insurance Policy, with limits for bodily injury or
4 death not less than one million dollars, to employees of the Department
5 of Transportation for the protection of such employees who are legally
6 entitled to recover damages from owners or operators of uninsured
7 motor vehicles and underinsured motor vehicles because of a bodily
8 injury, including death resulting therefrom, arising out of or in the
9 course of such employee's employment when such employee is required
10 to be outside of a motor vehicle.

Explanation
The bill requires the state to provide uninsured and underinsured
motorist coverage for Department of Transportation employees
beginning July 1, 2027. This results in a cost between $700,000 and
$1,700,000 per year depending on the quantity and severity of relevant
accidents.

continue into the future subject to inflation.

TRANSPORTATION EMPLOYEES.
SUMMARY
This bill requires the state, by July 1, 2027, to give Department of
Transportation employees uninsured and underinsured motorist
coverage under the State Fleet Insurance Policy. The coverage must
have limits for bodily injury or death of at least $1 million and cover
employees who are legally entitled to recover damages from owners or
operators of uninsured or underinsured vehicles due to bodily injury or
death arising out of, or in the course of, their employment when the
employee must be outside of a motor vehicle.
