General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 74
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 112
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. HARDING, 30th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT FOR DAIRY FARMERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a tax credit in the
2 amount of twenty million dollars in the aggregate for dairy farmers, to
3 offset cyclical downturns in milk prices paid to such farmers.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a tax credit in the amount of twenty million dollars in the
aggregate for dairy farmers, to offset cyclical downturns in milk prices
paid to such farmers.
