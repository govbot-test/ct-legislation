General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 73
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3247
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
ESQUIRE, OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE A JUDGE OF THE
SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Sean O.
2 Kehoe, Esquire, of West Hartford to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to
3 serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both
4 Houses, in succession to the Honorable Robin L. Wilson, is approved
5 and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for
6 said term.
