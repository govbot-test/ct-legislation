General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 153
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 893
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT EXPANDING MEDICAID ELIGIBILITY FOR OLDER ADULTS
WITH DISABILITIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 17b-261 of the 2026 supplement to the general statutes
2 be amended to disregard certain income for a person identified as an
3 adult disabled child under Title II of the Social Security Act in
4 accordance with an amendment sponsored by former United States
5 Representative James Pickle and incorporated into federal Medicaid
6 law.
Statement of Purpose:
To expand Medicaid eligibility for older adults with disabilities by
disregarding certain income.
