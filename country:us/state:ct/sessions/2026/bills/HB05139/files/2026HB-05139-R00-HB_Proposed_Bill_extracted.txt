Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:

2 estate tax recapture for estates that exceed the fifteen-million-dollar

3 limit on estate tax liability, and (2) an alternative minimum estate tax to
4 prevent the effective net tax rate from falling below two per cent.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish (1) an estate tax recapture for certain estates, and (2) an
alternative minimum estate tax to prevent the effective net tax rate from
falling below two per cent.
