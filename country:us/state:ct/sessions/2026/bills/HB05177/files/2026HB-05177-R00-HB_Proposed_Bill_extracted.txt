General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5177
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 715
Referred to Committee on COMMERCE
Introduced by:
REP. ROCHELLE, 104th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT FOR MANUFACTURERS
THAT DONATE EQUIPMENT OR SUPPLIES TO MANUFACTURING
TRAINING PROGRAMS AT PUBLIC SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 12 of the general statutes be amended to create a tax credit
2 for manufacturers that donate equipment or supplies to manufacturing
3 training programs at public middle schools or public high schools in the
4 state.
Statement of Purpose:
To encourage the donation of equipment and supplies to public middle
schools and public high schools in the state that have manufacturing
training programs by allowing manufacturers to receive a tax credit for
such donations.
