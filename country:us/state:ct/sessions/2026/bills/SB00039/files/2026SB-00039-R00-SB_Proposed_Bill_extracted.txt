Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 281
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. FAZIO, 36th Dist.
AN ACT REQUIRING THE INDEXING OF INCOME THRESHOLDS FOR
THE PERSONAL INCOME TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to require that
2 income thresholds for the personal income tax be adjusted in accordance
3 with any change in the consumer price index.
Statement of Purpose:
To require that income thresholds for the personal income tax be
adjusted in accordance with any change in the consumer price index.
LCO No. 281 1 of 1
