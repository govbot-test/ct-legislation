AN ACT CONCERNING THE ANGEL INVESTOR TAX CREDIT
PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) The chairpersons of the joint
2 standing committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of
3 matters relating to commerce shall convene a working group to study
4 and make recommendations concerning the angel investor tax credit
5 program established pursuant to section 12-704d of the general statutes.
6 Such study shall include, but need not be limited to, a review of the audit
7 summary completed by the Auditors of Public Accounts on Connecticut
8 Innovations, Incorporated's administration of the angel investor tax
9 credit program for the fiscal years ending June 30, 2022, and June 20,
10 2023.
11 (b) The working group shall consist of, but need not be limited to, the
12 following members:
13 (1) The chairpersons and ranking members of the joint standing
14 committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of matters
15 relating to commerce, or their designees;

18 (3) The Commissioner of Economic and Community Development,

28 cochairpersons from among the members of the working group.
29 (e) The administrative staff of the joint standing committee of the
30 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to commerce
31 shall serve as administrative staff of the working group.
32 (f) Not later than January 1, 2027, the working group shall submit a
33 report on its findings and recommendations to the joint standing
34 committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of matters
35 relating to commerce, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a
36 of the general statutes. The working group shall terminate on the date
37 that it submits such report or January 1, 2027, whichever is later.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a working group to study the angel investor tax credit
program established pursuant to section 12-704d of the general statutes.
