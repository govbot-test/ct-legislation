NOMINATIONS

RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF DR. RYAN G.
FLANAGAN OF RIDGEFIELD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE
PSYCHIATRIC SECURITY REVIEW BOARD.
Resolved by the Senate:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Dr. Ryan G.
2 Flanagan of Ridgefield, to be a member of the Psychiatric Security
3 Review Board, as a psychiatrist experienced with the criminal justice
4 system and not otherwise employed on a permanent basis by the state,
5 in succession to John Bonetti, to serve a term ending June 30, 2029, or
6 until a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever is longer, is
7 hereby approved and confirmed.
