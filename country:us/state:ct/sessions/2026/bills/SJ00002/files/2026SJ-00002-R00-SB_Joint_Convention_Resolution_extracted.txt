General Assembly Joint Convention
Resolution No. 2
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 411
Referred to Committee on NO COMMITTEE
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
THAT THE SENATE AND HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES ARE MET
IN JOINT CONVENTION TO RECEIVE HIS BUDGET MESSAGE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That a committee of two Senators and two Representatives be
2 appointed to inform His Excellency, the Governor, that the Senate and
3 the House of Representatives are met in Joint Convention for the
4 purpose of receiving his message concerning the budget.
