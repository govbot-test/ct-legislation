February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1090
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
(HS)
AN ACT CONCERNING MEDICAID PHARMACY AUDITS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section, (1)
2 "extrapolation" has the same meaning as provided in subsection (d) of
3 section 17b-99 of the general statutes, and (2) "pharmacy" means a
4 pharmacy participating in the state Medicaid program. The
5 Commissioner of Social Services shall not, as a basis for denying
6 Medicaid reimbursements to a pharmacy, use extrapolation of data
7 stemming from clerical errors or minor technical errors in any audit of
8 such pharmacy conducted by the Department of Social Services
9 pursuant to subsection (d) of section 17b-99 of the general statutes.
10 (b) The commissioner shall (1) provide a process to confirm receipt
11 by a pharmacy of any notices from the department, including notices to
12 a pharmacy regarding pharmacy billing, and (2) maintain a reliable
13 database accessible to a pharmacy of the preferred drug list approved
14 pursuant to section 17b-274d of the general statutes. The commissioner
15 shall update such database with real-time information regarding denials
16 and approvals of drugs for inclusion on such preferred drug list.

17 (c) Not later than October 1, 2026, the commissioner shall implement
18 a grievance process in accordance with chapter 54 of the general statutes

22 a pharmacy to present evidence of the pharmacy's cost in dispensing
