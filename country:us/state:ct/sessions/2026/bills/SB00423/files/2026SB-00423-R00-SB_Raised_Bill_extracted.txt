Statement of Purpose:
To require the Board of Regents for Higher Education to include
additional data and information in its annual report concerning the
consolidation of the regional community-technical colleges into the
Connecticut State Community College and to require that a copy and
any updates to the higher education strategic plan be provided to the
legislative committee with cognizance of government oversight.
