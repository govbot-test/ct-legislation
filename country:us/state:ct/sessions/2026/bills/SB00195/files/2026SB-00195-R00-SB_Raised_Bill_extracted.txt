AN ACT CONCERNING THE PREVENTION OF ACCIDENTAL
OVERDOSE DEATHS AND IMPROVING ACCESS TO TREATMENT
AND RECOVERY SERVICES FOR SUBSTANCE USE DISORDER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "Overdose prevention center" means a community-based facility

13 a separate location within the facility, safely consume controlled
14 substances under the observation of licensed health care providers who
