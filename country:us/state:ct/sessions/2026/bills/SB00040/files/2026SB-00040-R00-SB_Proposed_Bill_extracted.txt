AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT FOR EDUCATIONAL
ACCESS AND OPPORTUNITY SCHOLARSHIPS.

Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a tax credit for

3 and opportunity scholarships.

To establish a tax credit for donations made to nonprofit entities that
