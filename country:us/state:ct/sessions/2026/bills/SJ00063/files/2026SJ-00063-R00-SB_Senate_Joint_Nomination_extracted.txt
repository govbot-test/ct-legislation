RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF EVERETT SMITH
III OF GREENWICH TO BE A UTILITY COMMISSIONER OF THE
PUBLIC UTILITIES REGULATORY AUTHORITY.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Everett
2 Smith III of Greenwich to be appointed a utility commissioner of the
3 Public Utilities Regulatory Authority, in succession to Michael A. Caron,
4 to serve a term ending June 30, 2030, or until a successor is appointed
5 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
6 confirmed.
