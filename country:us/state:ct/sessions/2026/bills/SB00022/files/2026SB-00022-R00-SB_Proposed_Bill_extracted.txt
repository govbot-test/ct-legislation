Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 121
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE YOUTH SERVICE
BUREAUS ENHANCEMENT GRANT PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of three hundred thousand dollars be appropriated to
2 the Department of Children and Families, from the General Fund, for
3 the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for the youth service bureaus
4 enhancement grant program established under section 10-19q of the
5 general statutes.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide additional grant funding for youth service bureaus.
LCO No. 121 1 of 1
