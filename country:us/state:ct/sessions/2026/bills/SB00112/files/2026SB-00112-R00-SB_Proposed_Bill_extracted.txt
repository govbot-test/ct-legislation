General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 112
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 802
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
IMPROVEMENTS TO LISBON CENTRAL SCHOOL IN THE TOWN OF
LISBON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 eight hundred twenty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be
5 used by the Department of Education for the purpose of providing a
6 grant-in-aid to the school district in the town of Lisbon for upgrades to
7 the gym and associated areas and the heating, ventilation and air
8 conditioning system at Lisbon Central School.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for upgrades to the gym and associated areas and
the heating, ventilation and air conditioning system at Lisbon Central
School in the town of Lisbon.
