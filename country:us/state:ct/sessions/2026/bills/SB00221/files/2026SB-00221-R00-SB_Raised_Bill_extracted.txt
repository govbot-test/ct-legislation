2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 (a) There is established an aspiring educators [diversity] scholarship
4 program administered by the Department of Education. The program
5 shall provide an annual scholarship to [diverse students] aspiring
6 educators who (1) graduated from a public high school in [an alliance
7 district, as defined in section 10-262u] this state, and (2) are enrolled in

15 identified by the Commissioner of Education pursuant to section 10-8b,
16 as amended by this act.
