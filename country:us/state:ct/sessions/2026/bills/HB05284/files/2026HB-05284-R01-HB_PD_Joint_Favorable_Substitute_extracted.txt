General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5284
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING PROPERTY TAX ABATEMENT FOR CERTAIN
FIRST-TIME HOMEBUYERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026, and applicable to assessment
2 years commencing on or after October 1, 2026) Any municipality may, upon
3 approval by its legislative body or, in any town in which the legislative
4 body is a town meeting, by the board of selectmen, adopt an ordinance
5 allowing for the abatement of up to five hundred dollars, for a period
6 not exceeding five assessment years, of the real property taxes due upon
7 any residential property owned by a person who purchased such
8 property with funds provided to qualified first-time homebuyers by the
9 Connecticut Housing Finance Authority and which property is
10 encumbered by a mortgage financed, directly or indirectly, by the
11 Connecticut Housing Finance Authority. For the purposes of this
12 section, "residential property" means a single-family residential
13 dwelling that is the principal residence of the owner of such property.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026, and New section
applicable to assessment
years commencing on or
after October 1, 2026
