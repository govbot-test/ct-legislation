General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5178
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 970
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
REP. BUMGARDNER, 41st Dist.
SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
REP. GAIEWSKI, 40th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A CULINARY
ARTS CENTER AT ROBERT E. FITCH HIGH SCHOOL IN THE TOWN
OF GROTON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 seven hundred fifty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be
5 used by the Department of Education for the purpose of providing a
6 grant-in-aid to the town of Groton for a culinary arts center at Robert E.
7 Fitch High School.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a culinary arts center at Robert E. Fitch High
School in the town of Groton.
