AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE TO STUDY UNDUE DELAY
IN WORKERS' COMPENSATION CLAIMS BY POLICE OFFICERS AND
FIREFIGHTERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a task force
2 to study causes of undue delay in workers' compensation claims made
3 by police officers and firefighters. Such study shall include, but need not
4 be limited to, an examination of delays caused by (1) administrative
5 processing of such claims, (2) medical provider availability and
6 schedule limitations, and (3) insurer authorization requirements.
7 (b) The task force shall consist of the following members:
8 (1) Two appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives;
9 (2) Two appointed by the president pro tempore of the Senate;
10 (3) One appointed by the majority leader of the House of

30 (f) The administrative staff of the joint standing committee of the
31 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor and
32 public employees shall serve as administrative staff of the task force.
33 (g) Not later than January 1, 2027, the task force shall submit a report
34 on its findings and recommendations to the joint standing committee of
35 the General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor
36 and public employees, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-
37 4a of the general statutes. The task force shall terminate on the date that
38 it submits such report or January 1, 2027, whichever is later.
