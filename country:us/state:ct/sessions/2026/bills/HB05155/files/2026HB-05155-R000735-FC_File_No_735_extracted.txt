Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a working
2 group within the legislative branch to examine the creation of a
3 searchable database that is capable of receiving, organizing and storing
4 data associated with the sales and use of pesticides that the
5 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection may require or
6 request pursuant to chapter 441 of the general statutes. In undertaking
7 such examination, such working group shall review the searchable
8 pesticide databases of not less than two other states, including, but not
9 limited to: (1) Reviewing the costs of creating, licensing and maintaining
10 such databases and the costs associated with complying with the use of
11 such databases for any applicator, farmer, retailer or other person who
12 reports any such sales or use information, and (2) learning the
13 parameters, uses, outcomes and end users of such databases, including,
14 but not limited to, any security or proprietary information issues related

Explanation
The bill creates a working group to examine the creation of a
searchable database associated with the sales and use of pesticides
resulting in no fiscal impact to the state. The working group has the
resources and expertise to meet the requirements of the bill.
