General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 66
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3556
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
OF NAUGATUCK TO BE A MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS
OF THE CONNECTICUT PORT AUTHORITY.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by Matthew Ritter, the speaker of the House of
2 Representatives, of Robert Mezzo of Naugatuck, to be a member of the
3 Board of Directors of the Connecticut Port Authority, to serve a term
4 ending June 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified,
5 whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
