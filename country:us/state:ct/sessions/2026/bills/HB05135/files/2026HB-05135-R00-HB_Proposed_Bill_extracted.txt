General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5135
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 747
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. CANDELARIA J., 95th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A YOUTH
CENTER IN THE FAIR HAVEN COMMUNITY OF NEW HAVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two million five hundred dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used
5 by the Department of Economic and Community Development for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the city of New Haven for the
7 development of a youth center in the Fair Haven community of said city.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the development of a youth center in the Fair
Haven community of New Haven.
