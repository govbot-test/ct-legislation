AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF MICROTRANSIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Transportation shall conduct a study relating to microtransit. For
3 purposes of this section, "microtransit" means a technology-enabled
4 transportation service that uses multipassenger vehicles to provide on-
5 demand services with dynamically generated routing. Such study shall
6 include, but need not be limited to: (1) An analysis of designated service
7 areas appropriate for microtransit; (2) the estimated utilization of
8 microtransit in identified service areas; (3) a timeline for
9 implementation of a microtransit program; and (4) the potential costs of
10 and sources of funding for such program. Not later than January 1, 2027,
11 the commissioner shall submit a report of the results of such study to
12 the joint standing committees of the General Assembly having
13 cognizance of matters relating to transportation and appropriations and
14 the budgets of state agencies, in accordance with the provisions of
15 section 11-4a of the general statutes.
