General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5114
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 593
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REFUNDABLE CREDIT AGAINST THE
PERSONAL INCOME TAX FOR A PORTION OF RENT PAYMENTS
MADE FOR A PRIMARY RESIDENCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a refundable credit
2 against the personal income tax for the portion of rent payments made
3 for a primary residence in the state by a taxpayer for a taxable year that
4 is attributable to the property tax assessed on the rental property.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a refundable credit against the personal income tax for the
portion of rent payments made for a primary residence in the state by a
taxpayer for a taxable year that is attributable to the property tax
assessed on the rental property.
