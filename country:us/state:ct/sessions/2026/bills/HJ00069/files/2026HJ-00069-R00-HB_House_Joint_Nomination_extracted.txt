2 Fahey, Esquire, of South Glastonbury to be a Judge of the Superior
3 Court, to serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation
4 by both Houses, in succession to the Honorable Susan Q. Cobb, is
5 approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior
6 Court for said term.
