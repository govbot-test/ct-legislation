Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 New section
Sec. 3 October 1, 2026 53-289a
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) specify the circumstances in which a ticket reseller may enter into
a contract with, or accept consideration from, a purchaser for the resale
of a ticket to an entertainment event, (2) establish and modify various
requirements concerning the sale or resale of a ticket to an entertainment
event by way of an Internet web site or online technology platform, (3)
define various terms concerning tickets to entertainment events, and (4)
require a ticket seller or reseller to provide a refund for a cancelled live
entertainment event.
