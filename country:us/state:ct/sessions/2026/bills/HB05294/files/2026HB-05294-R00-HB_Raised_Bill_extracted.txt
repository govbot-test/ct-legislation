Statement of Purpose:
To (1) specify that all citizens and state residents intending to become
United States citizens, between the ages of eighteen and forty-five, are
subject to state military duty, (2) provide for greater discretion in the
appointment of the Governor's military staff, and (3) rename the state
military training facility in Niantic as Camp Nett.
