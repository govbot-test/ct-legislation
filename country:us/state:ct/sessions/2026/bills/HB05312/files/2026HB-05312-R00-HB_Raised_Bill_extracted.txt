AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CIVIL ACTION FOR THE OFFICE OF THE
ATTORNEY GENERAL AND A PRIVATE RIGHT OF ACTION FOR
VICTIMS OF UNLAWFUL DISSEMINATION OF A SYNTHETICALLY
CREATED INTIMATE IMAGE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:

11 initiating the action.

Statement of Purpose:
To establish a private right of action and a civil action for the office of
the Attorney General to pursue damages from an operator of an
electronic digital platform who fails to take down a synthetically created
intimate image when the operator knew or should have known that
dissemination of such image was unlawful.
