Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 274
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. BUCKBEE, 67th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING PERSONAL INCOME TAX DEDUCTIONS FOR
SENIORS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 12-701 of the general statutes be amended to require that,
2 for seniors, the qualifying income threshold for any personal income tax
3 deduction be indexed to reflect the rate of inflation.
Statement of Purpose:
To require that, for seniors, the qualifying income threshold for any
personal income tax deduction be indexed to reflect the rate of inflation.
LCO No. 274 1 of 1
