General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 42
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 211
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. PERILLO J., 21st Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A MEDICARE PREMIUMS TAX CREDIT
AGAINST THE PERSONAL INCOME TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to establish a
2 credit against the personal income tax for premiums paid by a taxpayer
3 for a taxable year for Medicare and Medicare Advantage plans.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a credit against the personal income tax for premiums paid
by a taxpayer for a taxable year for Medicare and Medicare Advantage
plans.
