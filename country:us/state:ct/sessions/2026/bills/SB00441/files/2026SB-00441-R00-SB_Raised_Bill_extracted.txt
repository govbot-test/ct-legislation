General Assembly Raised Bill No. 441
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2757
Referred to Committee on LABOR AND PUBLIC EMPLOYEES
Introduced by:
(LAB)
AN ACT REQUIRING A STUDY OF LABOR LAW VIOLATIONS BY
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES AND OTHER BUSINESS ENTITIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Labor Commissioner shall, in
2 consultation with the Secretary of the State, Commissioner of Revenue
3 Services and Commissioner of Administrative Services, study the
4 potential for members of a limited liability company or other business
5 entity to circumvent the provisions of chapter 557 or 558 of the general
6 statutes by establishing other business entities. Not later than January 1,
7 2027, the commissioner shall submit a report along with any
8 recommendations for legislation, in accordance with the provisions of
9 section 11-4a of the general statutes, to the joint standing committee of
10 the General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor
11 and public employees on the results of such study.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section

Raised Bill No. 441
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Labor Commissioner, in consultation with the Secretary
of the State, Commissioner of Revenue Services and Commissioner of
Administrative Services, to study the potential for members of a limited
liability company or other business entity to circumvent certain labor
laws by establishing a new business entity.
