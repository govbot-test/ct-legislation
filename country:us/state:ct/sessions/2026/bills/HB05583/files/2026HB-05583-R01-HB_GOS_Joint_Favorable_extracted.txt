AN ACT CONCERNING CONVEYANCE OF PARCELS OF STATE
LAND IN THE TOWN OF DERBY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding any provision
2 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Transportation shall convey
3 to the Connecticut Municipal Development Authority parcels of land
4 located in the town of Derby, at a cost equal to the administrative costs
5 of making such conveyance. Said parcels of land have an area of
6 approximately 1.5 acres and are identified as Lots 178, 181, 182, 183, 184,
7 185, 190, 191, 193 and 211 on town of Derby Tax Assessor's Map 8-5 and
8 Lot 7 on town of Derby Tax Assessor's Map 8-3. The precise size and
9 area of the parcels to be conveyed shall be determined by the
10 Commissioner of Transportation. The conveyance shall be subject to the
11 approval of the State Properties Review Board.
12 (b) The Connecticut Municipal Development Authority shall issue a
13 request for proposals to develop said parcels and the adjacent parcel
14 owned by the town of Derby for mixed-use redevelopment, which may
15 include the sale or lease of said parcels for such purposes. If the
