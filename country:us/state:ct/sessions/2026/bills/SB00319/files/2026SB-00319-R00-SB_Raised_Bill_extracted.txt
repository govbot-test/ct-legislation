Introduced by:

AN ACT CONCERNING THE USE OF BATTERY-POWERED LEAF
BLOWERS AND SIMILAR EQUIPMENT BY THE STATE AND
MUNICIPALITIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) On and after September
2 1, 2027, the Departments of Administrative Services, Energy and
3 Environmental Protection and Transportation shall, within available
4 appropriations, purchase, lease or acquire electric hand-held or push
5 lawn mowers, leaf blowers and lawn trimmers for state agencies for
6 landscaping services on state-owned property whenever such existing
7 gas-powered equipment reaches the end of its useful life.
8 (b) On and after September 1, 2029, the Departments of
9 Administrative Services, Energy and Environmental Protection and
10 Transportation shall, within available appropriations, prepare bidding
11 documents for contractors who seek to perform maintenance or
12 landscaping work on state properties in order to require such bidders to
13 use electric hand-held or push lawn mowers, leaf blowers and lawn
14 trimmers in the performance of such work.
