Statement of Purpose:
To (1) require support for paid internship programs at small businesses,
(2) provide grants to students who receive federal Pell grants to assist
such students in obtaining internships, (3) require institutions of higher
education to report about internship opportunities offered through such
institutions, (4) establish a state quality seal for quality internships, (5)
extend the human capital investment tax credit for salaries paid to
interns, and (6) establish a grant program for nonprofit organizations to
offset the cost of salaries paid to interns.
