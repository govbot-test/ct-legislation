AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE TO STUDY MEANS OF
ENCOURAGING MORE NURSING HOMES IN THE STATE TO
CONTRACT WITH THE UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF
VETERANS AFFAIRS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) There is established a task force to
2 study available means of encouraging nursing homes in the state to
3 become federally contracted veterans nursing homes and increase the
4 availability of nursing home care to veterans eligible for and in need of
5 such care. The task force shall consider financial incentives, including,
6 but not limited to, assistance to supplement reimbursement for such
7 care and tax credits, and other manners of promoting such nursing
8 homes to veterans eligible for care that is covered by the United States
9 Department of Veterans Affairs. As used in this section, "nursing home"
10 and "federally contracted veterans nursing home" have the same
11 meanings as provided in section 19a-533 of the general statutes.
12 (b) The task force shall consist of the following members:
