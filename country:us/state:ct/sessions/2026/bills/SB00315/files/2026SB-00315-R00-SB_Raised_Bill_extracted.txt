General Assembly Raised Bill No. 315
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1442
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING THE RELEASE OF STERILE CARP IN
PACHAUG POND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) Notwithstanding the provisions of
2 title 26 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Energy and
3 Environmental Protection shall provide for the release of two sterile
4 carp in Pachaug Pond in the town of Griswold.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 July 1, 2026 New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the release of sterile carp in Pachaug Pond for the control of
invasive vegetation.
