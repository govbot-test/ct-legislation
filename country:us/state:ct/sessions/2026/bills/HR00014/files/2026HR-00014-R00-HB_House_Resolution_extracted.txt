RESOLUTION CONGRATULATING SENATOR MARTIN M. LOONEY
ON HIS DISTINGUISHED LEGISLATIVE CAREER.
Resolved by this House:
1 WHEREAS, Martin M. Looney has devoted decades of exemplary
2 public service with integrity, diligence and unwavering commitment,
3 not only to the constituents he represented in the Connecticut General
4 Assembly but to all people of the State of Connecticut; and
5 WHEREAS, Martin M. Looney has served with distinction as a
6 member of both the House of Representatives and the Senate, including
7 in his role as President Pro Tempore of the Senate where he has
8 provided thoughtful leadership and guidance; and
9 WHEREAS, Martin M. Looney's dedication to public service reflects
10 the highest ideals of civic responsibility and has earned him the respect
11 and admiration of colleagues, constituents and community leaders
12 alike.
