General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5480
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING PEER SUPPORT SERVICES UNDER THE
MEDICAID PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section, (1)
2 "peer support services" means recovery-focused behavioral health
3 services that allow an individual the opportunity to learn to manage
4 such individual's recovery with help from a peer support specialist; and
5 (2) "peer support specialist" means an individual who (A) has
6 experience recovering from mental illness or substance use disorder,
7 and (B) is certified to provide peer recovery support under a program
8 administered by the Department of Mental Health and Addiction
9 Services.
10 (b) The Commissioner of Social Services shall amend the Medicaid
11 state plan to integrate peer support services into care teams funded
12 under the medical assistance program and provide Medicaid
13 reimbursement to peer support specialists for such services.
14 (c) Not later than August 1, 2027, the Commissioner of Social Services
15 shall file a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of
16 the general statutes, with the joint standing committees of the General
17 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to human services and
18 public health on (1) the number of peer support specialists provided
