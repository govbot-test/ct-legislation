General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5475
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING OVERSIGHT OF PERMITTING PROCESSES
OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENTAL
PROTECTION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Not later than January 1, 2027, the
2 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection shall assess and
3 report on potential methods to facilitate the permitting process of
4 general permits, including (1) the use of artificial intelligence tools to do
5 the preliminary review of all applications, and (2) the feasibility of
6 creating an expedited review process with increased fees for the
7 expedited service for certain permits and identifying any permits
8 appropriate for such service and any statutory changes required to
9 effectuate such expedited review process.
10 (b) Not later than January 1, 2027, the Commissioner of Energy and
11 Environmental Protection shall assess and report on methods to
12 facilitate the permitting process of individual permits, including, but not
13 limited to, (1) permits for solid waste, thermal loop and fuel cell power
14 generation and coastal land management, whether there are increased
15 automation tools that businesses and municipalities could use to reform
16 the timeline and costs of permitting, and (2) the feasibility of creating an
17 expedited review process with increased fees for the expedited service
18 for certain permits and identifying any permits appropriate for such
