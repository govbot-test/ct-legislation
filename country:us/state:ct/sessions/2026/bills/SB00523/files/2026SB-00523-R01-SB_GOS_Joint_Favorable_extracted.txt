AN ACT CONVEYING A PARCEL OF STATE LAND TO THE TOWN OF
NORTH CANAAN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding any provision
2 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Transportation shall convey
3 to the town of North Canaan a parcel of land located in the town of
4 North Canaan, at a cost equal to the administrative costs of making such
5 conveyance. Said parcel of land has an area of approximately 9.45 acres
6 and is identified as the property depicted on a map of the Department
7 of Transportation attached to a lease of said parcel to the town of North
8 Canaan dated July 16, 1991, that is recorded in the town of North
9 Canaan Land Records in Volume 70 at pages 524 to 530, inclusive, and
10 is further described in deeds recorded in the town of North Canaan
11 Land Records in Volume 29 at page 613 and Volume 30 at page 114, as
12 parcel #3. The precise size and area of the parcel to be conveyed shall be
13 determined by the Commissioner of Transportation. If a survey of said
14 parcel of land for the purpose of the conveyance is deemed necessary by
15 the Commissioner of Transportation, the town of North Canaan shall
16 pay the costs of any such survey. The conveyance shall be subject to the

18 (b) The town of North Canaan shall use said parcel of land for open
19 space or low impact nonmotorized public recreation purposes,
20 including, but not limited to, an existing greenway trail, except for a
21 portion of said parcel to be used for an emergency services facility as
22 provided in subsection (c) of this section. If the town of North Canaan:
23 (1) Does not use said parcel for any of said purposes;
24 (2) Does not retain ownership of all of said parcel, other than a
25 transfer of the management of a portion of the parcel as described in
26 subsection (c) of this section or a transfer to a nonprofit entity to facilitate
27 or manage any of said purposes; or
28 (3) Leases all or any portion of said parcel, other than a lease to a
29 nonprofit entity to facilitate or manage any of said purposes,
30 the parcel shall revert to the state of Connecticut. Any sale or lease of
31 said parcel to a nonprofit entity under this subsection shall be for fair
32 market value and any funds received by the town of North Canaan from
33 such sale or lease shall be transferred to the State Treasurer for deposit
34 in the Special Transportation Fund.
35 (c) The town may use a portion not exceeding four acres of said parcel
36 for the construction of a nonprofit fire and emergency services facility
37 to be used and managed by the North Canaan Fire Company, provided
38 the town retains ownership of said portion of the parcel.
39 (d) Said parcel shall be subject to an easement in favor of the
40 Housatonic Railroad Company for purposes of maintaining a railroad
41 spur abutting the southerly boundary of the parcel.
42 (e) The State Properties Review Board shall complete its review of the
43 conveyance of said parcel of land not later than thirty days after it
44 receives a proposed agreement from the Department of Transportation.
45 The land shall remain under the care and control of said department
46 until a conveyance is made in accordance with the provisions of this
