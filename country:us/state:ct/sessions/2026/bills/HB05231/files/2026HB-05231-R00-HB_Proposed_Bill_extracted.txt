General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5231
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 241
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. DEFRONZO, 26th Dist.
AN ACT EXPANDING A PERSONAL INCOME TAX DEDUCTION TO
INCLUDE CERTAIN MEMBERS OF THE UNIFORMED SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That subparagraph (B)(xvi) of subdivision (20) of subsection (a) of
2 section 12-701 of the general statutes be amended to expand the personal
3 income tax deduction for any income received from the United States
4 government as retirement pay to include retired members of the
5 commissioned corps of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric
6 Administration and the commissioned corps of the Public Health
7 Service.
Statement of Purpose:
To expand a personal income tax deduction for certain retirement pay
to include retirees of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric
Administration and the Public Health Service.
