February Session, 2026 LCO No. 65

AN ACT REDUCING FEES FOR SMALL BUSINESS FILINGS WITH
THE SECRETARY OF THE STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 34 of the general statutes be amended to reduce fees for
2 small business filings with the Secretary of the State.
Statement of Purpose:
To reduce fees for small business filings with the Secretary of the State.
