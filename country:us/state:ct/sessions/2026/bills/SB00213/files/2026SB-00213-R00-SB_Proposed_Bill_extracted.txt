General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 213
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 41
Referred to Committee on PUBLIC HEALTH
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE 9-8-8 SUICIDE
PREVENTION AND MENTAL HEALTH CRISIS LIFELINE AND THE 2-1-
1 INFOLINE PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to require the imposition of a
2 surcharge of twenty-five cents on the monthly fee paid by residents of
3 the state for telecommunication services and cellular mobile telephone
4 services provided in the state that are capable of accessing the 9-8-8
5 Suicide Prevention and Mental Health Crisis Lifeline for (1) deposit into
6 the 9-8-8 Suicide Prevention and Mental Health Crisis Lifeline Fund
7 established pursuant to section 17a-674a of the general statutes, and (2)
8 administration of the 2-1-1 Infoline program in the state operated by the
9 United Way of Connecticut.
Statement of Purpose:
To require dedicated funding for the 9-8-8 Suicide Prevention and
Mental Health Crisis Lifeline and the 2-1-1 Infoline program.
