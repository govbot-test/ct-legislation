Section 1 October 1, 2026 19a-2a
Statement of Purpose:
To allow the Commissioner of Public Health to (1) impose conditions on
the issuance or renewal of any permit, approval, registration, certificate
or license, and (2) resolve enforcement actions with an agreed settlement
or consent order without need for a formal hearing.
