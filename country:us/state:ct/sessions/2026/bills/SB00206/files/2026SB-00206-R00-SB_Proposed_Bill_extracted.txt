February Session, 2026 LCO No. 82
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. RAHMAN, 4th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING THE COST OF HEADSTONES UP TO A
CERTAIN AMOUNT FROM THE SALES AND USE TAXES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 219 of the general statutes be amended to exempt up to
2 two thousand five hundred dollars of the cost of a headstone from the
3 sales and use taxes.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt up to two thousand five hundred dollars of the cost of a
headstone from the sales and use taxes.
LCO No. 82 1 of 1
