NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF HOLLY
PUBLIC UTILITIES REGULATORY AUTHORITY.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Holly
2 Cheeseman of Niantic to be appointed a utility commissioner of the
3 Public Utilities Regulatory Authority, in succession to John W. Betkoski
4 III, to serve a term ending June 30, 2030, or until a successor is appointed
5 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
6 confirmed.
