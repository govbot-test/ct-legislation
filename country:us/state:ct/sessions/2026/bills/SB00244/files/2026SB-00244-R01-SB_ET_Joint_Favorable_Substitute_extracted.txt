General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 244
February Session, 2026
AN ACT REQUIRING A STUDY OF THE RENEWABLE ENERGY
CERTIFICATE MARKET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) The Commissioner of Energy and
2 Environmental Protection shall conduct a study concerning the market
3 for certificates issued by the New England Power Pool Generation
4 Information System. The study shall include, but need not be limited to,
5 (1) the role of such certificates in supporting the development, financing
6 and continued operation of renewable energy resources in the state and
7 the region, (2) the current supply, demand and pricing trends for such
8 certificates in the state and in regional markets, including markets
9 administered by the regional independent system operator, as defined
10 in section 16-1 of the general statutes, and (3) the extent to which such
11 certificates, when used for compliance with state renewable energy
12 requirements, demonstrate benefits in environmental protection,
13 electric-grid resiliency or any other identified benefits to the state. Not
14 later than January 1, 2027, the commissioner shall submit a report on the
15 results of such study, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a
16 of the general statutes, to the joint standing committees of the General
17 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to energy and
18 technology and the environment.
