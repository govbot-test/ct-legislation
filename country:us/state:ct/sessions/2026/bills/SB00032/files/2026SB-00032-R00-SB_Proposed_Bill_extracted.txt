General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 32
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 143
Referred to Committee on COMMERCE
Introduced by:
SEN. HWANG, 28th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING THE LEARN HERE, LIVE HERE PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 32-4i of the general statutes be amended to increase the
2 annual expenditure on the Learn Here, Live Here program to not more
3 than five million dollars and to make such program available only to
4 individuals whose income is seventy-five thousand dollars or less.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase the annual expenditure on the Learn Here, Live Here
program to not more than five million dollars and to make such
program available only to individuals whose income is seventy-five
thousand dollars or less.
