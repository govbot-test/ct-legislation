General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5232
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 479
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. LUXENBERG, 12th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A YOUTH
BASEBALL AND SOFTBALL INDOOR TRAINING FACILITY IN THE
TOWN OF MANCHESTER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Economic and Community Development for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Manchester for the
7 construction of a youth baseball and softball indoor training facility.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for an indoor training facility and increase the after-
school opportunities for youth in the town of Manchester.
