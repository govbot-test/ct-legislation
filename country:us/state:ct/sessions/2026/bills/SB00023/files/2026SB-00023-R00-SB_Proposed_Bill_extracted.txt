February Session, 2026 LCO No. 120
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR YOUTH SERVICE BUREAU
DATA REPORTING.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of three hundred ten thousand dollars be appropriated
2 to the Department of Children and Families, from the General Fund, for
3 the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for annual data collection, analysis
4 and reporting concerning youth service bureau programs.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide data on youth service bureaus.
LCO No. 120 1 of 1
