Section 1 October 1, 2026 47a-42
Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 52-261(b)
Statement of Purpose:
To require that personal property left behind after eviction be stored for
fifteen days by the plaintiff and to permit the plaintiff to dispose of such
property as such plaintiff sees fit following the expiration of fifteen days.
