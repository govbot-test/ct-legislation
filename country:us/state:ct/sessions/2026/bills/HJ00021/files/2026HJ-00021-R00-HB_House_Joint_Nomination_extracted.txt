General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 21
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1480
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY

REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
SUSAN QUINN COBB OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE A SENIOR
JUDGE OF THE SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Susan Quinn Cobb of West Hartford to be a Senior Judge of
3 the Superior Court, to serve for a term of eight years from May 5, 2026,
4 is approved and confirmed, and she is appointed a Senior Judge of the
5 Superior Court for said term.
