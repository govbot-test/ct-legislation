AN ACT CONVEYING A PARCEL OF STATE LAND TO THE TOWN OF
WOODBURY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding any provision
2 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Transportation shall convey
3 to the town of Woodbury a parcel of land located in the town of
4 Woodbury, at a cost equal to the administrative costs of making such
5 conveyance. Said parcel of land (1) has an area of approximately 1 acre,
6 (2) is identified as the portion of a state highway right-of-way located
7 on the easterly side of Route 6 between Hollow Road to the south and
8 Dolittle Hill Road to the north, which portion is known as the town's
9 South Green, and (3) contains memorial veterans monuments and a
10 paved roadway known as Veterans Lane. The precise size and area of
11 the parcel to be conveyed shall be determined by the Commissioner of
12 Transportation. The conveyance shall be subject to the approval of the
13 State Properties Review Board.

Asset
Explanation
The bill conveys a specified parcel of land, totaling one acre, from the
Department of Transportation to the town of Woodbury. The state
would lose use of the asset. The town of Woodbury would gain use of
an asset and would pay the administrative costs of the conveyance. The
asset would revert to state control if the recipient does not use the land
for specified purposes.
The land conveyance is subject to the review and approval of the State
Properties Review Board.
House “A” makes minor changes and has no fiscal impact.
The Out Years
The conveyance action is one time in nature. Control of the asset
would continue into the future as described above, subject to future
municipal decisions.
