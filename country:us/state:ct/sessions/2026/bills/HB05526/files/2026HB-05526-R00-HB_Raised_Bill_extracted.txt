General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5526
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2502
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING RECIPROCITY FOR CERTAIN INLAND
WATERS FISHING LICENSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) If and when the state of
2 New York, the state of Massachusetts or the state of Rhode Island enacts
3 a similar law granting reciprocal privileges to residents of this state, any
4 person who holds a license to fish in inland waters in the state of New
5 York, the state of Massachusetts or the state of Rhode Island may fish in
6 inland waters in this state, or in such waters as negotiated by the
7 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection of this state and
8 any similar authority in such adjoining state, without a nonresident
9 inland waters license to fish as required by title 26 of the general
10 statutes, provided such nonresidents shall be subject to all other
11 provisions of the general statutes and the regulations of the
12 commissioner relating to fishing in lakes and ponds.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
