General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5581
February Session, 2026
AN ACT AMENDING A PRIOR CONVEYANCE OF A PARCEL OF
STATE LAND TO THE TOWN OF ROCKY HILL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Special act 22-16 is amended to read as follows (Effective
2 from passage):
3 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the
4 Commissioner of Transportation shall convey to the town of Rocky Hill
5 a parcel of land located in the town of Rocky Hill, at a cost equal to [the
6 fair market value of the property, as determined by the average of the
7 appraisals of two independent appraisers selected by the commissioner,
8 plus] the administrative costs of making such conveyance. Said parcel
9 of land has an area of approximately 5.3 acres, [and] is identified as the
10 parcel of land located at 1090 Maple Street [,] and is further identified as
11 "RELEASE AREA" on a map entitled "Compilation Plan, Town of Rocky
12 Hill, Map Showing Land Released to ____ by the State of Connecticut
13 Department of Transportation, Interstate Route 291, scale 1"=40',
14 August 2021, Scott A. Hill, P.E., Chief Engineer-Bureau of Engineering
15 and Construction". The precise area of the parcel to be conveyed shall
16 be determined by the Department of Transportation. The conveyance
17 shall be subject to the approval of the State Properties Review Board.
18 (b) The town of Rocky Hill shall use said parcel of land for open space

19 or passive recreational purposes. If the town of Rocky Hill:
20 (1) Does not use said parcel for either of said purposes;
21 (2) Does not retain ownership of all of said parcel; or
22 (3) Leases all or any portion of said parcel,
23 the parcel shall revert to the state of Connecticut.
24 [(b)] (c) The State Properties Review Board shall complete its review
25 of the conveyance of said parcel of land not later than thirty days after
26 it receives a proposed agreement from the Department of
27 Transportation. The land shall remain under the care and control of said
28 department until a conveyance is made in accordance with the
29 provisions of this section. The State Treasurer shall execute and deliver
30 any deed or instrument necessary for a conveyance under this section,
31 which deed or instrument shall include provisions to carry out the
32 purposes of subsection (b) of this section. The Commissioner of
33 Transportation shall have the sole responsibility for all other incidents
34 of such conveyance. Any funds received by said department from a
35 conveyance of said parcel in accordance with this section shall be
36 transferred to the State Treasurer for deposit in the Special
37 Transportation Fund.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage SA 22-16
Statement of Legislative Commissioners:
In Subsec. (b), "space and passive" was changed to "space or passive" for
accuracy and consistency with standard drafting conventions.
