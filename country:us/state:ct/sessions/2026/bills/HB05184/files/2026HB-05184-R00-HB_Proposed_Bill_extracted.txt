General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5184
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 871
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. WILSON, 46th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
IMPROVEMENTS AND RENOVATIONS TO CASTLE CHURCH IN THE
CITY OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Economic and Community Development for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to Castle Church in the city of
7 Norwich to complete improvements and renovations.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to complete improvements and renovations to
Castle Church in the city of Norwich.
