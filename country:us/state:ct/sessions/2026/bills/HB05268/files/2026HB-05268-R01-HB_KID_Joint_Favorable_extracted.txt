AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC HEALTH TO
RELEASE INFORMATION RELATING TO AN ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF
PARENTAGE TO THE DEPARTMENT OF CHILDREN AND FAMILIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 46b-486 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 The Department of Public Health may release information relating to
4 an acknowledgment of parentage to (1) a signatory of the
5 acknowledgment, (2) the child if such child is eighteen years of age or
6 older, (3) a guardian of the person whose parentage is acknowledged,
7 (4) an attorney representing a person to whom such information may be
8 released, (5) a court, (6) a federal agency, (7) an authorized
9 representative of the Department of Social Services, (8) an authorized
10 representative of the Department of Children and Families, (9) the child
11 support agency of this state, (10) any agency acting under a cooperative
12 or purchase of service agreement with the child support agency of this
13 state, and (11) the child support agency of another state.
