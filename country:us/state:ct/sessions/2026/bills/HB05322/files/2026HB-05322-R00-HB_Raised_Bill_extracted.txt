AN ACT CONCERNING A WORKING GROUP REGARDING
ENDOMETRIOSIS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) There is established an
2 endometriosis working group. The working group shall (1) evaluate
3 methods of diagnosing and treating endometriosis and educating health
4 care providers who diagnose and treat patients with endometriosis, and
5 (2) make recommendations regarding such methods. The working
6 group shall be within the Legislative Department.
7 (b) The working group shall consist of members appointed by the
8 House chairperson of the joint standing committee of the General
9 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to human services that
10 such chairperson deems relevant and necessary to carry out the duties
11 of the working group.
12 (c) The administrative staff of the joint standing committee of the
13 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to human
14 services shall serve as administrative staff of the working group.
