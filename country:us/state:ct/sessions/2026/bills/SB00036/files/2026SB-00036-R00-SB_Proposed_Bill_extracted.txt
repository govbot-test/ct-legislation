General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 36
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 127
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT DECREASING THE PASSPORT TO THE PARKS FEE AND
INCREASING THE NONRESIDENT PARKING PASS FEE AT ANY
STATE PARK.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That (1) subsection (b) of section 14-49b of the general statutes be
2 amended to decrease the "Passport to the Parks Fee" imposed on the
3 registration of a motor vehicle from twenty-four dollars to twenty
4 dollars, and (2) subsection (c) of section 23-26 of the general statutes be
5 amended to increase the fee for a nonresident calendar year parking
6 pass at any state park, forest, boat launch or other state recreational
7 facility from one hundred twelve dollars to one hundred seventy-five
8 dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To reduce motor vehicle registration fees for Connecticut residents and
increase the nonresident parking pass at any state park, forest, boat
launch or other state recreational facility.
