AN ACT CONCERNING STUDENT LITERACY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) For the school year
2 commencing July 1, 2026, and each school year thereafter, the Center for
3 Literacy Research and Reading Success, established pursuant to section
4 10-14gg of the general statutes, shall establish and administer the
5 middle grades student support grant program. The center shall award
6 grants to local and regional boards of education seeking to support
7 reading development for students enrolled in grades four to six,

13 portion of the mastery examination for the prior school year, as
14 determined by the State Board of Education.
