General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5112
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 569
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM THE PERSONAL INCOME TAX THE
AMOUNT OF STATE AND FEDERAL DEBT RELIEF RECEIVED BY A
TAXPAYER FOR STUDENT LOANS AND MEDICAL DEBT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to exempt from
2 the personal income tax the amount of any state and federal debt relief
3 received by a taxpayer for student loans and medical debt.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt from the personal income tax the amount of any state and
federal debt relief received by a taxpayer for student loans and medical
debt.
