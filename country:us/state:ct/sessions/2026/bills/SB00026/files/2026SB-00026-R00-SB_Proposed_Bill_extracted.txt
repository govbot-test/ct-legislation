February Session, 2026 LCO No. 113
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR MUNICIPALITIES FOR EARLY
VOTING COSTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of ____ dollars be appropriated to the Office of Policy
2 and Management, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June
3 30, 2027, for reimbursement to each municipality for any increase in
4 election administration costs because of early voting.
Statement of Purpose:
To make municipalities whole for the increased cost of administering
early voting.
