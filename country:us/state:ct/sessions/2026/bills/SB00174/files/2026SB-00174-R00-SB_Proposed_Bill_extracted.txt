General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 174
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 882
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A PLANNING
GRANT TO EXPAND AND INSTALL SEWER LINES IN THE TOWN OF
LEDYARD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection for the
6 purpose of a providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Ledyard for a
7 planning grant to expand and install sewer lines on Connecticut Route
8 12 in said town.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a planning grant to expand and install sewer
lines in the town of Ledyard.
