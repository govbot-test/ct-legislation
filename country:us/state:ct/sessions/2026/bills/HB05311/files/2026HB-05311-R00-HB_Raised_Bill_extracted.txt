This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 53a-40e(a)
Statement of Purpose:
To require that in the case of a court declining to issue a standing
criminal protective order in the case of a family violence crime, the court
shall enter into the record the reasons for not issuing such order.
