Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 90
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
(By Request)
AN ACT PROHIBITING STATE AGENCIES FROM CHARGING
SERVICE FEES FOR PAYMENTS MADE BY CERTAIN MEANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to prohibit state agencies from
2 charging payors a service fee for payment by means of a credit card,
3 charge card or debit card.
Statement of Purpose:
To prohibit state agencies from charging payors a service fee for
payment by means of a credit card, charge card or debit card.
LCO No. 90 1 of 1
