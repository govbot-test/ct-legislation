(By Request)
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A PERSONAL INCOME TAX DEDUCTION
FOR CERTAIN LOSSES INCURRED AS A RESULT OF
CRYPTOCURRENCY INVESTMENT FRAUD OR WIRE FRAUD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to establish a
2 personal income tax deduction for the amount properly deductible, for
3 federal income tax purposes, as a loss resulting from cryptocurrency
4 investment fraud or wire fraud.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a personal income tax deduction for the amount properly
deductible, for federal income tax purposes, as a loss resulting from
cryptocurrency investment fraud or wire fraud.
