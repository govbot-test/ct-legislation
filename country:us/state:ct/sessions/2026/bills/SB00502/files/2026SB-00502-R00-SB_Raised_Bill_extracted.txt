Section 1 October 1, 2026 46b-127
Statement of Purpose:
To include all serious firearm offenses in the list of offenses eligible for
transfer from the juvenile court to the adult court, require a motion to
transfer a hearing on such motion for a case involving such offense to be
within fifteen days of the child's arraignment and require reporting on
transfer motions.
