OLR Bill Analysis
sSB 444
AN ACT AUTHORIZING CERTAIN MUNICIPAL EMPLOYEES TO
SERVE ON MUNICIPAL BOARDS OF FINANCE.
SUMMARY
This bill creates an additional exception to a statutory prohibition on
municipal employees serving on the board of finance (BOF) in the
municipality in which they work and live. The bill allows these
employees to serve on the BOF if they earn less than $10,000 per year.
Specifically, to fall under the exception, the salary and wages from the
municipality cannot exceed $10,000 in any rolling 12-month period.
Additionally, as under existing law, the prohibition on municipal
employees serving on the BOF in the municipality does not apply if:
1. a local charter or home rule ordinance explicitly allows the
service or
2. employees serve only in their capacity as a member of the
municipality’s legislative body (ex officio membership).
EFFECTIVE DATE: October 1, 2026
