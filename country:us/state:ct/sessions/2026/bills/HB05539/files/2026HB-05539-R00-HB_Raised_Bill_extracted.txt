Statement of Purpose:
To (1) require applicants for cigarette and tobacco products distributor
and dealer licenses and renewals to provide certain information with
such applications, and (2) establish additional penalties for violations of
state law regarding the sale, offer for sale or marketing of electronic
nicotine delivery systems and vapor products.
