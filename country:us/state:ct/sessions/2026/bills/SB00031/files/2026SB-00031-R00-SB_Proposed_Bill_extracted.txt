Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 144
Referred to Committee on COMMERCE
Introduced by:
SEN. HWANG, 28th Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING THE BOND AUTHORIZATION FOR THE
CONNECTICUT BIOSCIENCE INNOVATION FUND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 32-41dd of the general statutes be amended to increase
2 the bond authorization for the Connecticut Bioscience Innovation Fund
3 by twenty-five million dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase funding for bioscience innovation.
LCO No. 144 1 of 1
