Statement of Purpose:
To (1) allow unit owners of common interest communities to petition
the Superior Court for an accounting of such association's financial
records, (2) revise the disclosure requirements relating to common
interest communities, and (3) impose a cap on the fee imposed for the
preparation and recordation of a lender questionnaire.
