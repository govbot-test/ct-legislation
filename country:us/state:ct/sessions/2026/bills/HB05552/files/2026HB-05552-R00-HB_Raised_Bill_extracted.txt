Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
Statement of Purpose:
To impose requirements for contracts entered into by a public agency
with an entity that concern automated license plate reader information
to govern how the entity may store or use such information, require
such information be kept confidential and concerning jurisdiction and
choice of law in the event of a dispute.
