February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING PHONE-FREE SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 10-233j of the general statutes is repealed and the

16 (A) "Mobile electronic device" means any hand-held or other portable
17 electronic equipment capable of providing data communication
