AN ACT CONCERNING LIMITATIONS ON THE PROVISION OF GIFTS
TO THE EXECUTIVE BOARD MEMBERS OF THE ASSOCIATION OF A
COMMON INTEREST COMMUNITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 47-245 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (a) Except as provided in the declaration, the bylaws, subsection (b)
4 of this section, or other provisions of this chapter, the executive board
5 may act in all instances on behalf of the association. In the performance
6 of their duties, officers and members of the executive board appointed
7 by the declarant shall exercise the degree of care and loyalty to the
8 association required of a trustee and officers and members of the
9 executive board not appointed by a declarant shall exercise the degree
10 of care and loyalty to the association required of an officer or director of
11 a corporation organized under chapter 602, and are subject to the
12 conflict of interest rules governing directors and officers under chapter
13 602. The standards of care and loyalty described in this section apply
14 regardless of the form in which the association is organized.
