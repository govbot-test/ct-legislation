AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF THE REMEDIATION AND
DEVELOPMENT OF BROWNFIELDS IN THIS STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) The brownfields working group
2 established pursuant to section 32-770 of the general statutes, in
3 consultation with the Commissioner of Economic and Community
4 Development and the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental
5 Protection, shall conduct a study of issues relating to the remediation
6 and development of brownfields in this state, including, but not limited
7 to, any such issues caused by the implementation of the release-based
8 cleanup program established pursuant to chapter 445b of the general
9 statutes.
10 (b) The working group may request any data or other information
11 required to conduct such study from the Commissioner of Economic
12 and Community Development and the Commissioner of Energy and
13 Environmental Protection.

OFA Fiscal Note
State Impact: None
Municipal Impact: None
Explanation
The bill has no fiscal impact by requiring the brownfields working
group, in consultation with the Department of Economic and
Community Development and the Department of Energy and
Environmental Protection, to conduct a study of remediation and
development of brownfields in the state, including any issues caused by
the implementation of the released-based clean-up program.
It is anticipated that DECD and DEEP both have the necessary
expertise to assist the brownfields working group with this study.

State Impact: None
Municipal Impact: None
