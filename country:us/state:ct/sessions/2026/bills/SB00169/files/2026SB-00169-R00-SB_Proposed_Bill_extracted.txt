General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 169
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 891
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO PURCHASE
CAMP LAUREL AND REHABILITATE THE DAM IN THE TOWN OF
LEBANON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 four hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection for the
6 purpose of purchasing Camp Laurel and rehabilitating the dam in the
7 town of Lebanon.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to purchase Camp Laurel and rehabilitate the dam
in the town of Lebanon.
