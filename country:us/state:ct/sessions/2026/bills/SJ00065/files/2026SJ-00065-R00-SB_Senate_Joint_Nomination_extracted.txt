General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 65
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3053
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
OF RIDGEFIELD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE STATE BOARD OF
EDUCATION.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Evan Pitkoff
2 of Ridgefield, to be appointed a member of the State Board of Education,
3 in succession to Allan B. Taylor, to serve a term ending February 28,
4 2029, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever is
5 longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
