AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE CONNECTICUT-GROWN
FOR CONNECTICUT KIDS PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of ___ dollars be appropriated to the Department of
2 Agriculture, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30,
3 2027, for the Connecticut-Grown for Connecticut Kids program.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to support the Connecticut-Grown for Connecticut
Kids program and offer incentives to local farms and food growers.
