AN ACT CONCERNING MEDICAID RATE INCREASES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That Medicaid provider rates be increased in accordance with phase
2 one of a Medicaid rate study required pursuant to section 1 of public act
3 23-186 to (1) ensure such rates are competitive with provider rates
4 offered in neighboring states, and (2) improve access to quality health
5 care.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase Medicaid provider rates and improve access to quality
health care.
