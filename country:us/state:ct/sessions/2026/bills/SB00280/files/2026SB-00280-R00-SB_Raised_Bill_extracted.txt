AN ACT CONCERNING THE LEGISLATIVE COMMISSIONERS'
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR MINOR AND TECHNICAL REVISIONS TO
STATUTES RELATING TO VETERANS' AND MILITARY AFFAIRS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subdivision (1) of subsection (b) of section 27-102l of the
2 2026 supplement to the general statutes is repealed and the following is
3 substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
4 (b) (1) The commissioner may appoint a manager to administer an
5 Office of Advocacy and Assistance for the aid and benefit of veterans
6 and their spouses, eligible dependents and family members. The office
7 shall have a staff of not [less] fewer than nineteen men and women,
8 including not [less] fewer than fourteen veterans' service officers, and
9 not [less] fewer than three clerical personnel. The manager and veterans'
10 service officers shall be veterans, as defined in subsection (a) of section
11 27-103, or veterans who were awarded the armed forces expeditionary
12 medal for service by the armed forces.
13 Sec. 2. Subsection (c) of section 14-21cc of the 2026 supplement to the
