64 Sec. 2. Section 42-338 of the general statutes is repealed and the

66 (a) An authorized wheelchair dealer shall timely repair a wheelchair,
67 including a complex rehabilitation technology wheelchair, sold or

71 home upon request.

75 messages. The authorized wheelchair dealer shall (1) respond to a
76 request for wheelchair repair not later than one business day after the
77 date of request, and (2) order parts for a repair not later than three
