RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF NISA J. KHAN,
ESQUIRE, OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE A JUDGE OF THE
SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Nisa J. Khan,
2 Esquire, of West Hartford to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to serve
3 for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both Houses,
4 in succession to the Honorable Carl E. Taylor, is approved and
5 confirmed, and she is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for said
6 term.
