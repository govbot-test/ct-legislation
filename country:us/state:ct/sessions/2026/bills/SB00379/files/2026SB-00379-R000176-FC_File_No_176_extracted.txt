AN ACT CONCERNING WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT IN THE
STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Board of Regents for Higher
2 Education shall conduct a study relating to workforce development
3 initiatives in the state. Not later than January 1, 2027, the board shall
4 submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the
5 general statutes, of the results of such study to the joint standing
6 committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of matters
7 relating to higher education and employment advancement.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:

OFA Fiscal Note
State Impact: None
Municipal Impact: None
Explanation
The bill has no fiscal impact. It requires the Connecticut State
Colleges and Universities (CSCU) to conduct a study regarding
workforce development initiatives in the state. It is anticipated that
CSCU has the resources to complete the study.
