Statement of Purpose:
To establish the Supplemental Graduate Student Loan Program, allow
up to ten million dollars in state bonds for said program, allocate not
less than sixty million dollars in annual private activity bonds for the
Connecticut Higher Education Supplemental Loan Authority and
increase the authority's bonding authorization cap.
