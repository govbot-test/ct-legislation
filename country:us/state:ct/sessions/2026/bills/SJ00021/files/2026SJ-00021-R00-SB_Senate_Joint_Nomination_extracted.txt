RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE
INGRID L. MOLL OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE A JUDGE OF THE
APPELLATE COURT AND A JUDGE OF THE SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Ingrid L. Moll of West Hartford to be a Judge of the
3 Appellate Court and a Judge of the Superior Court, to serve for a term
4 of eight years from May 3, 2026, is approved and confirmed, and she is
5 appointed a Judge of the Appellate Court and the Superior Court for
6 said term.
