General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 29
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 85
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
AN ACT ELIMINATING CERTAIN FUNDING FOR PEOPLESBANK
ARENA AND THE CITY OF HARTFORD AND INCREASING FUNDING
FOR SCHOOL RESOURCE OFFICERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to require that any state funds
2 related to the renovation of or improvements to PeoplesBank Arena, or
3 provided to the city of Hartford in connection with the Municipal
4 Accountability Review Board, be diverted to municipalities in order to
5 provide funding for school resource officers.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for school safety programs in all towns and cities in
the state by reducing certain state funding for PeoplesBank Arena and
the city of Hartford.
