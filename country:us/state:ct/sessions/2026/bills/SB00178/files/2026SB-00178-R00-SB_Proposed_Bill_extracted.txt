General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 178
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 979
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. LESSER, 9th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
CONSTRUCTION OF A FIRE STATION IN THE CITY OF
MIDDLETOWN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 ___ dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the Department of
5 Emergency Services and Public Protection for the purpose of providing
6 a grant-in-aid to the city of Middletown for the construction of a fire
7 station on Connecticut Route 3 in said city.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the construction of a fire station to ensure
adequate fire protection in the Newfield Street corridor in the city of
Middletown.
