General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5426
February Session, 2026
AN ACT REQUIRING THE AVAILABILITY OF AN ADVISOR DURING
DISCIPLINARY PROCEEDINGS AT INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER
EDUCATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Not later than August 1, 2026,
2 each institution of higher education in the state shall adopt a policy
3 allowing each student to designate an advisor or support person of such
4 student's choice to assist and support such student during a disciplinary
5 proceeding that may result in such student being suspended from such
6 institution, placed on probation, expelled from such institution or
7 prohibited from participating in an intercollegiate athletic program at
8 such institution. Such advisor or support person shall receive copies of
9 any communication relating to such disciplinary proceeding and
10 accompany such student to any meeting relating to such disciplinary
11 proceeding, provided (1) the involvement of such advisor or support
12 person does not result in the postponement or delay of such meeting as
13 scheduled, and (2) for disciplinary proceedings concerning sexual
14 assault, stalking and intimate partner violence, the policies adopted
15 pursuant to the provisions of section 10a-55m of the general statutes
16 concerning a student's entitlement to be accompanied by an advisor or
17 support person shall take precedence over the implementation of the
18 policy adopted under this section to the extent such policies differ. Each
