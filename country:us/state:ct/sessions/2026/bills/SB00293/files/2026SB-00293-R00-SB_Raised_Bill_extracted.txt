General Assembly Raised Bill No. 293
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1887
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
(JUD)
AN ACT CONCERNING THE STATUTE OF LIMITATION FOR INJURY
CAUSED BY FRAUD IN THE PROVISION OF FERTILITY CARE AND
TREATMENT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026, and applicable to any cause
2 of action arising from an incident committed on or after said date)
3 Notwithstanding the provisions of section 52-577 of the general statutes,
4 an action to recover damages for personal injury caused by fraud in the
5 provision of fertility care and treatment may be brought not later than
6 three years from the date on which the minor child affected by fraud in
7 the provision of fertility care and treatment reaches the age of majority,
8 or three years after the date on which the fraud is discovered, whichever
9 is later.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:

Statement of Purpose:
To provide that an action to recover damages for personal injury to a
person caused by fraud in the provision of fertility care and treatment
may be brought not later than three years from the date the minor child
affected by such fraud reaches the age of majority, or three years after
the date on which the fraud is discovered, whichever is later.
