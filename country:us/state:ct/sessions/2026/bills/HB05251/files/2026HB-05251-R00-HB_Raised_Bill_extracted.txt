February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1427
Referred to Committee on GOVERNMENT OVERSIGHT
Introduced by:
(GOS)
AN ACT CONCERNING REPORTING ON IMPLEMENTATION OF
CERTAIN CONSULTANT REPORT RECOMMENDATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "Consultant" means any professional who (A) is registered or
3 licensed to practice such profession in accordance with the applicable
4 provisions of the general statutes or any planner or any environmental,
5 management or financial specialist, and (B) provides consultant services
6 to a state agency pursuant to a contract with such state agency,
7 including, but not limited to, any architect, professional engineer,
8 accountant, planner or environmental, management or financial
9 specialist;
10 (2) "Consultant services" includes administrative, planning, analysis,
11 statistical or research services rendered by any architect, professional
12 engineer, accountant, planner or environmental, management or
13 financial specialist, as well as incidental services that members of such
14 professions and those in their employ are authorized to perform, for
15 purposes of recommending a state agency course of action;
LCO No. 1427 1 of 2
Raised Bill No. 5251
16 (3) "Firm" means any individual, partnership, corporation, joint
17 venture, association or other legal entity authorized by law to offer
18 consultant services; and
19 (4) "State agency" means any office, department, board, council,
20 commission, institution, constituent unit of the state system of higher
21 education, technical education and career school or other agency in the
22 executive branch of state government.
23 (b) Any state agency that contracts with a firm to provide consultant
24 services at a cost of one hundred thousand dollars or more for purposes
25 of producing a study or other report with recommendations for future
26 actions for the state agency to undertake that is required to be
27 transmitted to a committee of the General Assembly pursuant to the
28 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes shall, not later than
29 one year after receipt of the results of such study or a final report from
30 such firm, also submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of
31 section 11-4a of the general statutes, to the joint standing committee of
32 the General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to
33 government oversight, summarizing the findings of the entity's report,
34 whether any recommendations have been implemented by the agency
35 and whether the state agency intends to implement any such
36 recommendations in the future and, if applicable, by what date.
