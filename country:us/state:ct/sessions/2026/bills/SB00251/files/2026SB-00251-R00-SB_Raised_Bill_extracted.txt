Section 1 October 1, 2026 2-90
Statement of Purpose:
To authorize the Auditors of Public Accounts to (1) require additional
review in audits where only a sampling of transactions was obtained,
(2) require physical site visits of certain housing and human services
providers, (3) establish agency and quasi-public agency performance
and accountability standards, and (4) issue subpoenas pursuant to an
investigation.
