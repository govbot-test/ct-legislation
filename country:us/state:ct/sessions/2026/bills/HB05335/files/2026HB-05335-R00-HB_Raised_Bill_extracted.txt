AN ACT CONCERNING THE SAFETY CAPACITY OF BURR POND
STATE PARK IN TORRINGTON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Energy and Environmental Protection shall develop and post for
3 members of the public, on or before January 31, 2027, the capacity limit
4 of Burr Pond State Park. Such capacity limit shall be determined
5 utilizing the following factors: (1) The number of visitors that can be
6 safely supervised, taking into account the staffing at said state park; (2)
7 the number of swimmers that can be safely supervised, taking into
8 account the lifeguard staffing in said state park; (3) the parking capacity
9 at said state park; (4) the public restroom facility capacity at such state
10 park; (5) the pedestrian and vehicular traffic on any state highway
11 without a sidewalk that leads to said state park that threatens, or is
12 reasonably likely to threaten, public safety by pedestrians walking on
13 the side of the highway or by impeding the travel of emergency vehicles
14 because of the high volume of vehicles parked or moving on the
15 highway; and (6) the authority of the commissioner to utilize the
16 number of vehicles entering said state park as a proxy for the number of
