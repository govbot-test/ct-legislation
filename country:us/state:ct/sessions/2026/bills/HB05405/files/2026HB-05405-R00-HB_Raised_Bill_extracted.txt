Statement of Purpose:
To (1) modify the membership of the Examining Board for Crane
Operators, (2) establish that crane owners, crane operators and hoisting
equipment operators may be required to produce such owners' and
operators' licenses or registrations for verification, (3) establish a
procedure regarding the issuance of stop work orders to such owners
and operators, (4) modify and establish certain penalty provisions
applicable to such owners and operators, and (5) establish the
"investigations and inspections account".
