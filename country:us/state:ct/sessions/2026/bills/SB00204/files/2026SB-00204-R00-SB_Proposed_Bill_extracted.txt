General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 204
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 801
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A SKILLED
TRADES CENTER AT JUNE NORCROSS WEBSTER SCOUT
RESERVATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 one million five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall
5 be used by the Department of Economic and Community Development
6 for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Ashford to
7 establish a skilled trades center located at June Norcross Webster Scout
8 Reservation.
Statement of Purpose:
To authorize bonds of the state to establish a skilled trades center at June
Norcross Webster Scout Reservation.
