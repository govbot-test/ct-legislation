AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE MILITARY DEPARTMENT EMERGENCY
RESPONSE ACCOUNT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) There is established an
2 account to be known as the "Military Department emergency response
3 account", which shall be a separate, nonlapsing account. The account
4 shall contain any moneys required or permitted by law to be deposited
5 in the account. Any balance remaining in the account at the end of any
6 fiscal year shall be carried forward in the account for the fiscal year next
7 succeeding.
8 (b) The Military Department shall administer the account. Moneys in
9 the account shall be used for the purpose of covering costs incurred by
10 the Military Department, or by other state personnel and resources, in
11 responding to an emergency, including, but not limited to, any natural
12 disaster, civil emergency or other event requiring a state response
13 thereto, whenever federal moneys for such purpose are not immediately
14 available, provided any such use shall be approved by the Governor in
