General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 208
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 80
Referred to Committee on GENERAL LAW
Introduced by:
SEN. RAHMAN, 4th Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING THE MINIMUM AVERAGE COMMISSION
PAYABLE TO LOTTERY SALES AGENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229a of the general statutes be amended to increase the
2 minimum average commission payable to lottery sales agents to six and
3 one-half per cent.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase the minimum average commission payable to lottery sales
agents.
