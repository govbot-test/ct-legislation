General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 198
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 32
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
DEVELOPMENT OF A WATER STORAGE FACILITY IN THE TOWN OF
SOUTH WINDSOR.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 one million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Energy and Environmental Protection for the purpose of
6 providing a grant-in-aid to the town of South Windsor for the
7 development of a water storage facility.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the development of a water storage facility in
the town of South Windsor to support emergency responses to
residential fires and other disasters, ensure a reliable water supply for
farms and livestock, prepare for droughts and extreme weather events
and address infrastructure gaps in rural parts of said town.
