Introduced by:
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.

AN ACT AUTHORIZING STATE BONDS FOR MUNICIPAL
COMMERCIAL FACADE IMPROVEMENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 twenty million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing grants-in-aid to municipalities for commercial facade
7 improvements.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to municipalities for commercial facade
improvements, encourage beautification programs in downtown
corridors and promote foot traffic and economic activity.
