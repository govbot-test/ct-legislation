Statement of Purpose:
To implement the Department of Economic and Community
Development's recommendations for revisions to the commerce
statutes, including (1) removing references to certain Department of
Economic and Community Development offices, (2) repealing or
modifying certain reporting requirements, (3) repealing defunct
programs, (4) addressing changes to federal funding, (5) revising the
Manufacturing Innovation Fund Advisory Board membership and
establishing a board absentee policy, (6) establishing a council absentee
policy for the Tourism Council, (7) revising the name, charge and
membership of the Minority Business Initiative Advisory Board and
permitting the board to establish bylaws, (8) implementing certain
revisions to the concentrated poverty statutes, and (9) rename the Small
Business Express program and the small business express assistance
account.
