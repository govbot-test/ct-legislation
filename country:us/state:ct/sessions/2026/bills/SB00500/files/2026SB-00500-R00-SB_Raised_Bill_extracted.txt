General Assembly Raised Bill No. 500
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2358
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
(HS)
AN ACT CONCERNING A STATE-FUNDED CAREGIVER PROGRAM
TO ASSIST PARENTS OF CHILDREN WITH AUTISM SPECTRUM
DISORDER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (d) of section 17a-215c of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (d) The Commissioner of Social Services may seek approval of an
5 amendment to the Medicaid state plan or a waiver from federal law,
6 whichever is sufficient and most expeditious, to establish and
7 implement a Medicaid-financed home and community-based program
8 to provide community-based services and, if necessary, housing
9 assistance, to adults diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder but not
10 with intellectual disability. Within available appropriations, the
11 commissioner shall establish a caregiver respite program to assist
12 parents of children enrolled in the Medicaid home and community-
13 based waiver program by providing state-financed care of such
14 children.
