General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5406

Referred to Committee on VETERANS' AND MILITARY

Introduced by:

AN ACT CONCERNING AN AWARD HONORING THE HEROIC
WARTIME SERVICE OF CERTAIN VETERANS AND RESERVE
MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES AND MINOR AND TECHNICAL
REVISIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 27-73e of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 The Commissioner of Veterans Affairs, in conjunction with the
4 Adjutant General, shall award a ribbon and medal to each (1) veteran
5 who either (A) was a resident of this state at the time he or she was called
6 to active duty for service in time of war, or (B) is domiciled in this state
7 on the date of such award, and (2) former member of any reserve
8 component of the armed forces who was honorably discharged [who]
9 and either (A) was a resident of this state at the time he or she was
10 serving in such reserve component during a period of war, or (B) is
11 domiciled in this state on the date of such award. The commissioner, in
12 conjunction with the Adjutant General, shall adopt regulations, in
13 accordance with chapter 54, setting forth the process for designing the

15 are eligible for the ribbon and medal under this section and establishing

This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 27-73e
Statement of Purpose:
To make minor and technical revisions to a statute concerning ribbons
and medals awarded to certain veterans and armed forces reservists
with wartime service.
