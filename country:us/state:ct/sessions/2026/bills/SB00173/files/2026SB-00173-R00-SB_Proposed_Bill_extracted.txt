General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 173
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 883
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A MULTIMODAL
TRACK ON A PORTION OF CONNECTICUT ROUTE 117.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Ledyard for a multimodal
7 track on Connecticut Route 117 to the Groton town line.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to the town of Ledyard for a multimodal track on
Connecticut Route 117 to the Groton town line.
