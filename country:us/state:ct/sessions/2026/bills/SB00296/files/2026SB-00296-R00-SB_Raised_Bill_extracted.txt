Statement of Purpose:
To (1) increase the maximum amount of restitution available under the
Connecticut Unfair Trade Practices Act, (2) make consistent statutory
provisions concerning record retention under said act, and (3) update
the state's cheating statute to include provisions relating to sports
wagering.
