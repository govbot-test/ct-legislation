Explanation
The bill expands allowable uses and extends the deadline for sale for
parcels of land conveyed in special act 19-4.
To the extent the sale is completed under the original terms or does
not occur prior to July 1, 2031, there is no fiscal impact from the bill.
To the extent that the sale happens under the revised terms, either for
the expanded uses or within the extended deadline, the bill precludes
reversion of control of the property to the state and there would be a
revenue gain to the Special Transportation Fund equal to the selling
price of the parcels before July 1, 2031.
The Out Years
The conveyance action is one time in nature. Control of the asset
would continue into the future as described above, subject to future
municipal decisions.
