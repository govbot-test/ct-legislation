Explanation
The bill modifies a conveyance approved in special act 22-16 by
removing the requirement that the town of Rocky Hill pay the fair
market value of the asset, which precludes a revenue gain to Special
Transportation Fund and reduces the cost to the town of Rocky Hill in
the amount of the fair market value of the property (most recently
valued at approximately $668,800).
Additionally, the bill adds a requirement that the asset would revert
to state control if the recipient does not use the land for specified
purposes, which results in a potential loss of asset to the town of Rocky
Hill and potential gain of asset for the state.
