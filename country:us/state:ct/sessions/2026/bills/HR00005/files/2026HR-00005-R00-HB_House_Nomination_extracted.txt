RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF GEORGE
BARRIOS OF FAIRFIELD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF
TRUSTEES FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF CONNECTICUT.
Resolved by this House:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of George
2 Barrios of Fairfield, to be a member of the Board of Trustees for The
3 University of Connecticut, in succession to Marilda L. Gandara, to serve
4 a term ending June 30, 2029, or until a successor is appointed and has
5 qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
