General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5075
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 668
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
REP. JOHNSON, 49th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING THE SCHOOL BUILDING PROJECT AT
WINDHAM HIGH SCHOOL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That notwithstanding the provisions of section 10-285a of the general
2 statutes, or any regulation adopted by the State Board of Education or
3 the Department of Administrative Services pursuant to said section
4 concerning the reimbursement percentage that a local board of
5 education may be eligible to receive for a school building project, the
6 town of Windham may use the reimbursement rate of ninety-five per
7 cent for the renovation project at Windham High School (Project
8 Number 163-0079 RNV).
Statement of Purpose:
To provide the town of Windham with a ninety-five per cent
reimbursement rate for its school building project at Windham High
School.
