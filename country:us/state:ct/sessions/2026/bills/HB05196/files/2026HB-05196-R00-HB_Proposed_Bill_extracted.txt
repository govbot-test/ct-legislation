General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5196
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1036

BONDING

REP. BUMGARDNER, 41st Dist.
REP. GAIEWSKI, 40th Dist.
SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE GROTON
COMMUNITY CENTER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Economic and Community Development for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Groton for planning
7 and engineering the Groton Community Center.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for planning and engineering the Groton
Community Center.
