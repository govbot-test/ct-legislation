General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5129
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 912
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. SIMMS, 140th Dist.
REP. DOUCETTE, 13th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A SURCHARGE ON HIGH-VALUE
RECREATIONAL VESSELS AND CONCERNING THE USE OF THE
REVENUE GENERATED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 12 of the general statutes be amended to establish a
2 surcharge on high-value recreational vessels and dedicate the revenue
3 generated from such surcharge to reducing and eliminating the
4 property tax on motor vehicles.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a surcharge on high-value recreational vessels and dedicate
the revenue generated from such surcharge to reducing and eliminating
the property tax on motor vehicles.
