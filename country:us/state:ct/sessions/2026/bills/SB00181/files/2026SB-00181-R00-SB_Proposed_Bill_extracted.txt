AN ACT CONCERNING PERSONAL INCOME TAX REFUNDS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That $330,811,954 be transferred from the Budget Reserve Fund to the
2 General Fund and said amount be used to provide refunds, as calculated
3 by the Department of Revenue Services, of personal income tax actually
4 paid by taxpayers who filed a federal income tax return for the taxable
5 year commencing on or after January 1, 2025.
Statement of Purpose:
To require moneys to be transferred from the Budget Reserve Fund to
the General Fund and used to provide personal income tax refunds to
taxpayers.
