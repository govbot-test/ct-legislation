Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 380
Referred to Committee on AGING
Introduced by:
SEN. HOCHADEL, 13th Dist.
AN ACT PROVIDING A FAMILY CAREGIVER TAX CREDIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to provide a tax
2 credit to family caregivers for certain expenditures incurred for the care
3 and support of elderly family members.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide a tax credit to family caregivers for certain expenditures
incurred for the care and support of elderly family members.
