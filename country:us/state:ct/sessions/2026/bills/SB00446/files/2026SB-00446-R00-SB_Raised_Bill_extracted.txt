General Assembly Raised Bill No. 446
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2368
Referred to Committee on PLANNING AND DEVELOPMENT
Introduced by:
(PD)
AN ACT REQUIRING THE OFFICE OF POLICY AND MANAGEMENT
TO CONDUCT A STUDY CONCERNING THE FEASIBILITY OF
AUTHORIZING MUNICIPALITIES THAT HAVE ENTERED INTO
REVENUE SHARING AGREEMENTS TO ADOPT DIFFERENTIAL MILL
RATES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Office of Policy and
2 Management shall conduct a study concerning the feasibility of
3 authorizing municipalities that have entered into revenue sharing
4 agreements pursuant to section 7-148bb of the general statutes to adopt
5 differential mill rates applicable to taxable real property for which the
6 tax revenues are shared pursuant to such agreements. Not later than
7 January 1, 2027, the Secretary of the Office of Policy and Management
8 shall submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a
9 of the general statutes, to the joint standing committee of the General
10 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to planning and
11 development. Such report shall include the findings of such study and
12 any legislative recommendations to authorize such municipalities to
13 adopt such differential mill rates.
