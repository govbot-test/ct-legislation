Explanation
The bill expands a prior conveyance to allow sale, transfer, or lease of
the property. To the extent revenue is generated by the sale, transfer, or
lease of the property, there would be a revenue gain to the Special
Transportation Fund. The town of Stratford would lose the asset if it
chooses to sell or transfer the property.
The Out Years
Control of the asset would continue into the future as described
above, subject to future municipal decisions.
