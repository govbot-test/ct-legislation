AN ACT CONCERNING ANIMAL WELFARE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The State Veterinarian
2 shall identify one or more contact persons for victims of domestic
3 violence in the state who need to place their companion animal in the
4 care of another person due to such circumstance. Any such contact
5 person shall be accessible by telephone, text message or electronic mail
6 at any point during any calendar day and shall be a resource for
7 informing any such victim of locations of animal rescue organizations,
8 dog pounds or regional animal control facilities who, at such point
9 during such calendar day, can serve as a location for placement of such
10 victim's companion animal on a temporary or permanent basis. The
11 State Veterinarian shall cause the name and contact information of any
12 such contact person to be regularly updated and disseminated to the
13 Connecticut Coalition Against Domestic Violence, any other domestic
14 violence organization in the state, and each police department, dog
15 pound, regional animal control facility and animal rescue organization
16 in the state. The State Veterinarian shall work with any such animal
17 rescue organization, dog pound and regional animal control facility to

40 represents animal control officers, and (6) any additional members, as
41 deemed necessary by the commissioner. The commissioner, on or before
42 January 1, 2027, shall submit the recommendations of such working
43 group to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
44 cognizance of matters related to the environment, in accordance with

Statement of Purpose:
To identify resources for persons needing to place their companion
animal in another person's care due to a domestic violence situation and
to clarify the authority of the State Veterinarian to combat disease
among companion animals and establish a working group to study
ways to improve the funding of the animal population control program.
