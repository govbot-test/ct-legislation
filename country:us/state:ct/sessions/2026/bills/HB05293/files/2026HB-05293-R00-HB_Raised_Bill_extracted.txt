51 Sec. 2. Section 27-122b of the general statutes is repealed and the
52 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2027):
53 (a) As used in this section, "veteran" means any person who is a
54 veteran, as defined in section 27-103, who (1) has completed at least
55 twenty years of qualifying service, as described in and computed under
56 10 USC Chapter 1223, as amended from time to time, in the Connecticut
57 National Guard; or (2) was killed in action, or who died as a result of
58 accident or illness sustained while performing active service, in the
59 United States Army, Navy, Marine Corps, Air Force, Coast Guard or
60 Space Force or any women's auxiliary branch thereof, organized
61 pursuant to an Act of Congress, or in the Connecticut National Guard.
62 (b) (1) Any veteran may, by letter or other communication addressed
63 to the Commissioner of Veterans Affairs, or by will, request that upon

66 amended by this act, or (2) the spouse or other next of kin of any such
