AN ACT CONCERNING PESTICIDE REPORTING MODERNIZATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) The Department of Energy
2 and Environmental Protection shall establish an online pesticide sales
3 and use database that enables the searching and tracking of data on the
4 pesticides that are sold or used in the state in accordance with the
5 reporting requirements of the department for such sales or use
6 authorized pursuant to chapter 441 of the general statutes while
7 additionally enabling the provision of any information previously
8 tracked or collected by the department pursuant to chapter 441 of the
9 general statutes, prior to the effective date of this section, to the general
10 public and other governmental users. The department shall collaborate
11 with the Department of Agriculture to collect and make available
12 through such online database information on the sale of pesticide-
13 treated seeds in the state. Such online database shall be implemented
14 not later than December 1, 2028.

Statement of Purpose:
To require the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection to
institute the use of modern technology and methodologies for the
reporting of pesticide applications in the state and enable ready
knowledge of where pesticides are being used, what pesticides are being
used and the quantities of pesticides that are being used.
