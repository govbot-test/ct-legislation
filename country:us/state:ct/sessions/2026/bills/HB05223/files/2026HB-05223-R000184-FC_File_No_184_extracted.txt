AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE TO STUDY ISSUES
CONCERNING GROCERY STORE BEER PERMITS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a task force
2 to study issues concerning grocery store beer permits. Such study shall
3 include, but need not be limited to, an examination of (1) the feasibility
4 of establishing a per capita cap on the number of grocery store beer
5 permits that may be issued within a municipality, and (2) the criteria
6 applied to assess eligibility for a grocery store beer permit.
7 (b) The task force shall consist of the following members:
8 (1) Two appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives;
9 (2) Two appointed by the president pro tempore of the Senate;
10 (3) One appointed by the majority leader of the House of
11 Representatives;
