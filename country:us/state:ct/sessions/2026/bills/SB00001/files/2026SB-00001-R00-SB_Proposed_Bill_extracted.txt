AN ACT CONCERNING AFFORDABILITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to: (1) Exempt from the sales
2 and use taxes clothing costing under one hundred dollars, school
3 supplies and appliances; (2) eliminate the additional one per cent sales
4 and use taxes imposed on meals sold by grocery stores; (3) establish a
5 voluntary employer payroll tax and a personal income tax credit for

7 the maximum allowable credit against the personal income tax for a
