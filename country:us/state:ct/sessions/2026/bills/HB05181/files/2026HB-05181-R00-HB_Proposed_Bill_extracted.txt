AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR CAPITAL
IMPROVEMENTS TO VARIOUS COMMUNITY ORGANIZATIONS IN
THE CITY OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three million seven hundred thirty thousand dollars, the proceeds of
5 which shall be used by the Department of Economic and Community
6 Development for the purpose of providing (1) a grant-in-aid in the
7 amount of one hundred thousand dollars to the First Haitian Baptist
8 Church of Norwich in the city of Norwich for capital improvements; (2)
9 a grant-in-aid in the amount of five hundred thousand dollars to the
10 Chestnut Street Playhouse in the city of Norwich for capital
11 improvements; (3) a grant-in-aid in the amount of seven hundred
12 twenty thousand dollars to the Norwich Arts Center in the city of
