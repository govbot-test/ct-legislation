The Committee on Veterans' and Military Affairs reported
through REP. FOSTER of the 57th Dist., Chairperson of the

AN ACT NAMING THE CONNECTICUT NATIONAL GUARD
READINESS CENTER IN THE TOWN OF PUTNAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The state military
2 readiness center in Putnam shall be named the Governor John Dempsey
3 Connecticut National Guard Readiness Center in honor of John
4 Dempsey, who served as mayor of and state representative for the town
5 of Putnam and as the eighty-first governor of Connecticut.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:

HB 5295
AN ACT NAMING THE CONNECTICUT NATIONAL GUARD
READINESS CENTER IN THE TOWN OF PUTNAM.
SUMMARY
This bill names the Connecticut National Guard readiness center in
Putnam the “Governor John Dempsey Connecticut National Guard
Readiness Center.” John Dempsey was the 81st governor of Connecticut
and served as both mayor of and state representative for Putnam.
