General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 55
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 317
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
AN ACT DEDICATING THE ADDITIONAL SALES TAX ON MEALS TO
CERTAIN PURPOSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to dedicate the revenue
2 generated from the additional one per cent of sales tax on meals as
3 follows: (1) Fifty per cent of the amount collected to be deposited in the
4 Tourism Fund; (2) twenty-five per cent of the amount collected to be
5 paid to the municipalities to which the purchases of the meals are
6 sourced; and (3) twenty-five per cent of the amount collected to be
7 deposited in the General Fund and dedicated to funding free school
8 lunches.
Statement of Purpose:
