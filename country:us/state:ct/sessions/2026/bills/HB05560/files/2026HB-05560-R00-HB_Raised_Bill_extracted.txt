General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5560
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2732
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
(HS)
AN ACT CONCERNING COSTS AND RATES OF REIMBURSEMENT
FOR PROGRAMS ADMINISTERED BY THE DEPARTMENTS OF
DEVELOPMENTAL AND SOCIAL SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) The Commissioners of
2 Developmental Services and Social Services shall evaluate and share
3 with each other rates of payment each pay to nonprofit provider
4 organizations for the same services. To the extent such rates are lower
5 for the same services at the Department of Developmental Services or
6 the Department of Social Services, the commissioner paying such lower
7 rates shall increase such rates to achieve payment parity.
8 (b) Not later than October 1, 2026, the commissioners shall file a joint
9 report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general
10 statutes, with the joint standing committees of the General Assembly
11 having cognizance of matters relating to human services and public
12 health. The report shall include, but need not be limited to: (1) A list of
13 services provided by nonprofit organizations by contract with each
14 department, (2) rates of payment by each department for each such
