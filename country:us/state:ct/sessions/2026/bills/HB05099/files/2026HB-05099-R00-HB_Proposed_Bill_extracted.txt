February Session, 2026 LCO No. 776
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. GAIEWSKI, 40th Dist.
REP. NOLAN, 39th Dist.
SEN. MARX, 20th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR CERTAIN TRANSPORTATION
SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of one million dollars be appropriated to the
2 Department of Transportation, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year
3 ending June 30, 2027, for micro-transit services in the city of New
4 London.
Statement of Purpose:
To support transportation services in the city of New London.
