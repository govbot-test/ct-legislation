General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 14
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 306
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. CICARELLA, 34th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE STATE-WIDE
NARCOTICS TASK FORCE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of five hundred thousand dollars be appropriated to the
2 Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection, from the
3 General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for the state-wide
4 narcotics task force.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to combat the illegal manufacturing and sale of
fentanyl in Hartford, New Haven, Bridgeport and Waterbury.
