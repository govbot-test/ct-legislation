General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 29
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1630
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
OF NEW BRITAIN TO BE REAPPOINTED A MEMBER OF THE STATE
DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION ARBITRATION PANEL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Janis C.
2 Jerman of New Britain to be reappointed a member of the State
3 Department of Education Arbitration Panel, as a neutral arbitrator, to
4 serve a term ending June 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed and
5 has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
LCO No. 1630 1 of 1
