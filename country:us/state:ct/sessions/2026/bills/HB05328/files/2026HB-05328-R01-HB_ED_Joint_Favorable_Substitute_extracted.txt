General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5328
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A WORKING GROUP TO ADDRESS ANTI-
MUSLIM AND ANTI-ARAB RACISM IN SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a
2 working group to address anti-Muslim and anti-Arab racism in public
3 schools. The working group shall develop guidance and resources to
4 address issues relating to anti-Muslim and anti-Arab racism that affect
5 students, families, educators and school personnel. Such guidance and
6 resources may include, but need not be limited to, (1) suggested
7 amendments to school district policies to ensure that all students,
8 educators and school personnel feel safe inside and outside of the school
9 setting, (2) recommended training relating to anti-Muslim and anti-
10 Arab racism for educators and administrators, and (3) guidance in the
11 creation or provision of curriculum materials and resources relating to
12 Islamic and Arab studies, pursuant to the provisions of section 10-16b
13 of the general statutes, and genocide education and awareness,
14 pursuant to the provisions of section 10-18f of the general statutes,
15 including, but not limited to, the history, heritage, contributions and
16 persecution of Muslim communities.
17 (b) The working group shall consist of the following members:
18 (1) Two appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives,
