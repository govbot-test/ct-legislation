General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 161
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 985
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
MARLBOROUGH FIRE DEPARTMENT IN THE TOWN OF
MARLBOROUGH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 thirty five thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the Marlborough Fire
7 Department in the town of Marlborough to complete purchases and
8 projects.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to complete purchases and projects at the
Marlborough Fire Department in the town of Marlborough.
