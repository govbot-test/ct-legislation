AN ACT CONCERNING EXPENDITURES OF THE GENERAL FUND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) The Office of Fiscal Analysis shall
2 conduct a review of appropriations authorized from the General Fund
3 in the state budget for the biennium ending June 30, 2027, and shall
4 identify general areas of spending for which appropriations increased
5 by ten per cent or more over appropriations authorized from the
6 General Fund in the state budget for the immediately preceding
7 biennium. On or before February 1, 2027, said office shall submit, in
8 accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes,
9 the results of its review to the joint standing committee of the General
10 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to appropriations and
11 the budgets of state agencies.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
