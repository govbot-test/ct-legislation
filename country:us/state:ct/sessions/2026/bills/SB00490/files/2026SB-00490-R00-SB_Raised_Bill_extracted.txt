Statement of Purpose:
To (1) exclude certain voluntary payments given by contributors to
online political donation platforms from the definition of "contribution",
and the use of such online platforms to request such voluntary
payments from the definition of "expenditure", and (2) prohibit such
platforms from taking automatically recurring contributions without
the affirmative consent of contributors.
