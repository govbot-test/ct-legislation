General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 61
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2840
Referred to Committee on Executive and Legislative Nominations
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF PATRICIA M.
FEENEY OF PLAINFIELD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE TECHNICAL
EDUCATION AND CAREER SYSTEM BOARD.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Patricia M.
2 Feeney of Plainfield to be appointed a member of the Technical
3 Education and Career System Board, as a person who has experience in
4 manufacturing or a trade offered by the Technical Education and Career
5 System or who is an alumna of the system, in succession to Christine
6 Benz, to serve at the pleasure of the Governor, for a term coterminous
7 with his term or until a successor is appointed and has qualified,
8 whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
