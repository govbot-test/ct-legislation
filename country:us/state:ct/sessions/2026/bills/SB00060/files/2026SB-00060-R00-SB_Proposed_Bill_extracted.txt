Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 206
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING THE SALES AND USE TAXES RATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 219 of the general statutes be amended to (1) decrease
2 the sales and use taxes rate to six per cent, and (2) eliminate the
3 additional one per cent sales and use taxes imposed on meals sold by
4 eating establishments, caterers or grocery stores.
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) decrease the sales and use taxes rate to six per cent, and (2)
eliminate the additional one per cent sales and use taxes imposed on
meals sold by eating establishments, caterers or grocery stores.
LCO No. 206 1 of 1
