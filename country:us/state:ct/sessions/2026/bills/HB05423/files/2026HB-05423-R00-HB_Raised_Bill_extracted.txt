AN ACT CONCERNING JUVENILE JUSTICE INITIATIVES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) Not later than July 1, 2026, the
2 executive director of the Court Support Services Division of the Judicial
3 Branch shall report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a
4 of the general statutes, to the joint standing committees of the General
5 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to the judiciary and
6 appropriations and the budgets of state agencies on the usage of court
7 support services for the previous fiscal year and recommendations for
8 expansion of such services.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require a report by the executive director of the Court Support
Services Division of the Judicial Branch on the expansion of community
support services.
