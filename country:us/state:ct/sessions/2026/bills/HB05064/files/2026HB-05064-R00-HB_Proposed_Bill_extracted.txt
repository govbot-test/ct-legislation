General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5064
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 524
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. SCOTT, 112th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT FOR FOOD DONATED BY
FARMERS TO CHARITABLE ORGANIZATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a tax credit of five
2 thousand dollars, adjusted annually in accordance with any change in
3 the consumer price index, for food donated by farmers to charitable
4 organizations.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a tax credit for food donated by farmers to charitable
organizations.
