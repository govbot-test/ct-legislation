General Assembly File No. 623
Substitute Senate Joint Resolution No. 48
February Session, 2026
Senate, April 14, 2026
The Committee on Judiciary reported through SEN. WINFIELD
of the 10th Dist., Chairperson of the Committee on the part of
the Senate, that the substitute joint resolution ought to be
adopted.
RESOLUTION VACATING THE DECISION OF THE CLAIMS
COMMISSIONER TO DENY THE CLAIM AGAINST THE STATE OF
MILAGROS POLLOCK HESS, ADMINISTRATRIX OF THE ESTATE OF
JHECARI HESS AND GRANTING THE CLAIMANT PERMISSION TO
SUE THE STATE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number 26437 of
2 said commissioner, ordering the denial of the claim against the state of
3 Milagros Pollock Hess, Administratrix of the Estate of Jhecari Hess, is
4 vacated. In accordance with the provisions of subsections (b) and (c) of
5 section 4-159 of the general statutes, the General Assembly grants the
6 claimant permission to sue the state. The claimant's action shall be
7 brought not later than one year from the date of the final adoption by
8 the General Assembly of this resolution.
JUD Joint Favorable Subst.
