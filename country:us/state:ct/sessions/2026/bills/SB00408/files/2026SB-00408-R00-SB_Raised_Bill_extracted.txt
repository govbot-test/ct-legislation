Statement of Purpose:
To (1) delete a provision regarding certain inspections not being deemed
to constitute a precondition to renewal of a liquor permit, (2) modify
certain requirements relating to fire safety and prevention inspections,
(3) require certain nonresident owners, project-based housing providers
or agents in charge to register certain information, and (4) establish
various requirements relating to juice bars.
