Statement of Purpose:
To (1) permit health clubs and gyms to offer separate areas for exercise
on the basis of sex, and (2) make statutory changes to commission
processes involving requests for early legal intervention, the reopening
of previously closed complaints and time parameters for pursuing a
complaint in court.
