General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5118
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 794
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. TURCO, 27th Dist.
REP. FARRAR, 20th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR REPAIRS AND
RENOVATIONS FOR CATHOLIC CHARITIES, INC. IN THE TOWN OF
NEWINGTON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two million seven hundred seventy thousand seventy-five dollars, the
5 proceeds of which shall be used by the Department of Economic and
6 Community Development for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid
7 to Catholic Charities, Inc., to repair and renovate the building and
8 grounds at 652 Willard Avenue in the town of Newington.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for repairs and renovations to the building and
grounds owned and used by Catholic Charities, Inc., in the town of
Newington to provide services for persons with intellectual disabilities.
