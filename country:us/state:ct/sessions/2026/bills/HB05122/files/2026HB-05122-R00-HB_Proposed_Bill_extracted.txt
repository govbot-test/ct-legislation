General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5122
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 526
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. HOXHA, 78th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
INFRASTRUCTURE-RELATED TECHNOLOGICAL AND ELECTRIC
UPGRADES TO MUNICIPAL FIREHOUSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two million five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall
5 be used by the Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection
6 for the purpose of providing grants-in-aid to municipal and other local
7 fire departments for the provision of infrastructure-related
8 technological and electric upgrades to firehouses.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to municipal and other local fire departments for
infrastructure-related technological and electric upgrades to firehouses.
