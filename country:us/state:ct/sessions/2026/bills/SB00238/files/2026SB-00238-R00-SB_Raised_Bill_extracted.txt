(PH)
AN ACT CONCERNING EMERGENCY MEDICAL SERVICES
RESPONSE TIME TRANSPARENCY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Public Health shall establish and maintain an emergency medical
3 services response time dashboard. Such dashboard (1) shall be capable
4 of collecting and displaying emergency medical services response time
5 data to the public, disaggregated by geography, call type and time of
6 day, and (2) shall not include any patient identifying information. The
7 Commissioners of Public Health, Social Services and Emergency
8 Services and Public Protection shall use data obtained from the
9 emergency medical services response time dashboard to support any
10 applications for federal emergency medical services response grants
11 and direct such grants to the communities in greatest need for improved
12 emergency medical services response times.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
