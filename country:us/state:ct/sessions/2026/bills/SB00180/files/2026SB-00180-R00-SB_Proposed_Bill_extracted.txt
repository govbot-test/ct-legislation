Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 861
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
REP. WILSON, 46th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
INTEGRATED DAY CHARTER SCHOOL IN THE CITY OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 four million six hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall
5 be used by the Department of Education for the purpose of providing a
6 grant-in-aid to the Integrated Day Charter School in the city of Norwich
7 for capital improvements.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for capital improvements at the Integrated Day
Charter School in the city of Norwich.
LCO No. 861 1 of 1
