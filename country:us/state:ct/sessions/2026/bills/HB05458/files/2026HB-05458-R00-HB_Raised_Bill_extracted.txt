General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5458
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2645
Referred to Committee on PUBLIC SAFETY AND SECURITY
Introduced by:
(PS)
AN ACT PROHIBITING THE DIVISION OF STATE POLICE AND
MUNICIPAL POLICE DEPARTMENTS FROM ACCEPTING CERTAIN
FEDERAL GRANTS RELATED TO CANNABIS GROWN ON PRIVATE
LAND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 21a-420b of the general statutes is amended by
2 adding subsection (f) as follows (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (NEW) (f) The Division of State Police within the Department of
4 Emergency Services and Public Protection and all municipal police
5 departments shall not accept a federal grant to conduct any program
6 related to the surveillance of, search for or destruction of cannabis
7 grown on private land.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 21a-420b(f)

Raised Bill No. 5458
Statement of Purpose:
To prohibit the Division of State Police within the Department of
Emergency Services and Public Protection and all municipal police
departments from accepting federal grants to conduct any program
related to the surveillance of, search for or destruction of cannabis
grown on private land.
