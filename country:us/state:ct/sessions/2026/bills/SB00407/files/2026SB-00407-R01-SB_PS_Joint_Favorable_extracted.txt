General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 407
February Session, 2026
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE COMMISSIONER OF MOTOR VEHICLES
TO ISSUE PERMITS ALLOWING ORGAN TRANSPORT VEHICLES TO
USE CERTAIN LIGHTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (h) of section 14-96q of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
3 1, 2026):
4 (h) The commissioner may issue a permit for emergency vehicles, as
5 defined in subsection (a) of section 14-283, and organ transport vehicles
6 to use a blue, red, yellow [,] or white light or lights, including a flashing
7 light or lights or any combination thereof, except as provided in
8 subsection (k) of this section. For the purposes of this subsection, "organ
9 transport vehicle" means any vehicle operated by an employee of a
10 procurement organization, as defined in section 19a-289a, while
11 transporting parts, as defined in said section.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
