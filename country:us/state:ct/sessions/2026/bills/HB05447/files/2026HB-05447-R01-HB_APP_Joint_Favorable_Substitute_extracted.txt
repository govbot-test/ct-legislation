General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5447
February Session, 2026
AN ACT IMPLEMENTING RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE
TRANSFORMING CHILDREN'S BEHAVIORAL HEALTH POLICY AND
PLANNING COMMITTEE AND CONCERNING TEMPORARY FAMILY
ASSISTANCE BENEFITS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) The executive director of
2 the Commission on Women, Children, Seniors, Equity and Opportunity
3 shall convene two working groups. Each working group shall include
4 one or more members of the Transforming Children's Behavioral Health
5 Policy and Planning Committee and such other members as selected by
6 the executive director.
7 (b) Not later than July 1, 2026, the executive director shall convene a
8 working group concerning the treatment of eating disorders. The
9 working group shall, in consultation with the Commissioners of Public
10 Health and Mental Health and Addiction Services, compile information
11 to be made available to the public concerning (1) providers of eating
12 disorder treatment services in the state, including, but not limited to,
13 facilities that provide such treatment, (2) details concerning the types of
14 services each such provider offers and the ages of persons typically
15 served by such providers, and (3) other resources available in the state
16 for persons with eating disorders. The working group shall also consult
17 with the Commissioner of Public Health to (A) develop, within available
