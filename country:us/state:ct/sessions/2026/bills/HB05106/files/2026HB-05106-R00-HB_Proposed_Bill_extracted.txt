General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5106
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 766
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. WEIR, 55th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING HOSPITAL BEDS FOR HOME HEALTH CARE
USE FROM THE SALES AND USE TAXES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 12-412 of the general statutes be amended to exempt
2 from the sales and use taxes the sale of and the storage, use or other
3 consumption in this state of hospital beds for home health care use.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt from the sales and use taxes the sale of and the storage, use
or other consumption in this state of hospital beds for home health care
use.
