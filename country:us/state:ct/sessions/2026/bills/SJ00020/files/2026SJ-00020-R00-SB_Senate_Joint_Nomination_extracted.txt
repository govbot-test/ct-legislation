General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 20
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1482
Referred to Committee on Judiciary
Introduced by:
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
STEVEN D. ECKER OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE AN ASSOCIATE
JUDGE OF THE SUPREME COURT AND A JUDGE OF THE
SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Steven D. Ecker of West Hartford to be an Associate Judge of
3 the Supreme Court and a Judge of the Superior Court, to serve for a term
4 of eight years from May 3, 2026, is approved and confirmed, and he is
5 appointed an Associate Judge of the Supreme Court and a Judge of the
6 Superior Court for said term.
LCO No. 1482 1 of 1
