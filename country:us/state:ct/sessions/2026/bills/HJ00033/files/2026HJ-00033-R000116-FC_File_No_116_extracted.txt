RESOLUTION RECOGNIZING NATIVE AMERICAN STEWARDSHIP.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 WHEREAS, all land in Connecticut was once Native American
2 territory; and
3 WHEREAS, it is our duty to acknowledge that, as the state's land-
4 grant institution, The University of Connecticut exists on Native
5 American land; and
6 WHEREAS, it is important to understand the long-standing history
7 that has brought all of us to reside on this land and to seek to understand
8 our place within that history; and
9 WHEREAS, colonialism is a continuing practice, and we need to be
10 mindful of our present participation.
11 NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED that the Connecticut General
12 Assembly pause in its deliberations to acknowledge that the land on
13 which we gather was the territory of the Mohegan, Mashantucket

State Impact: None

Explanation
The resolution recognizes that all land in the state of Connecticut was
once Native American territory, including the University of Connecticut
(UConn); acknowledges colonialism; and gives thanks to certain tribes.
As the resolution specifies it has no effect on legal or administrative
proceedings, it results in no fiscal impact to the state or municipalities.
