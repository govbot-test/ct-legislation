General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5069
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 268
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. BUCKBEE, 67th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR ROAD WORK IN
THE TOWN OF NEW MILFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 ____ dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the Department of
5 Transportation for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the town
6 of New Milford for the completion of road work in said town that is
7 recommended in the department's October 2024 New Milford Road
8 Safety Audit report.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for road work recommended in the Department of
Transportation's New Milford Road Safety Audit report dated October
2024.
