AN ACT PROVIDING HOUSING FOR CERTAIN IMPOVERISHED
VETERANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) There is established a
2 veterans' housing program through which the Commissioner of
3 Housing, in coordination with the Commissioner of Veterans Affairs,
4 may provide housing to any qualifying veteran, at no cost to such
5 veteran, in the form of a prefabricated structure upon the grounds of the
6 Department of Veterans Affairs in Rocky Hill.
7 (b) Each prefabricated structure described in subsection (a) of this
8 section shall have an area of not less than five hundred square feet, shall
9 have insulated exterior walls and shall include two bedrooms, a full
10 kitchen and a bathroom.
11 (c) On and after January 1, 2027, a veteran, as defined in section 27-
12 103 of the general statutes, whose income falls below the federal poverty
13 line and who is able to demonstrate having been without a permanent
14 residence for the immediately preceding ninety days, may apply to the

15 Commissioners of Housing and Veterans Affairs, in a form and manner
16 prescribed by said commissioners, to receive housing under the
17 program established in subsection (a) of this section. Not later than ten
18 days after receipt of any such application, said commissioners shall
19 verify whether the applicant qualifies for such housing and shall notify
20 such applicant of the commissioners' determination as to approval or
21 rejection of such application. Any such approval shall be for one year,
22 after which a veteran may reapply under this subsection.
23 (d) Any veteran whose application under subsection (d) of this
24 section has been approved shall be permitted to occupy a prefabricated
25 structure described in subsection (a) of this section, if available, not later
26 than sixty days after such approval, provided either the Commissioner
27 of Housing or Commissioner of Veterans Affairs may revoke such
28 approval or place restrictions or other conditions upon such occupancy
29 in cases where an occupant is posing a risk to the health, safety, security
30 or well-being of other occupants or the integrity of any prefabricated
31 structure.
32 (e) Not later than January 15, 2028, and annually thereafter, the
33 Commissioners of Housing and Veterans Affairs shall jointly submit a
34 report on the performance of the veterans housing program established
35 in subsection (a) of this section, and any recommendations for
36 improvement thereof, to the joint standing committees of the General
37 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to housing and
38 veterans' and military affairs, in accordance with the provisions of
39 section 11-4a of the general statutes.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a program for the provision of prefabricated homes to
certain impoverished veterans.
