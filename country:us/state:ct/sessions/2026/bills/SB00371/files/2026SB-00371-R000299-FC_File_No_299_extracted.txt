Note: GF=General Fund

Explanation
The bill will result in a minimal cost in FY 27, of up to $10,000, to the
Department of Energy and Environmental Protection (DEEP). The cost
is associated with the purchase of additional personal flotation devices,
to support educational programs run by DEEP. Under the bill, personal
floatation devices will be required for all children under 12 years old,
while on any public dock, wharf, or pier in the state. Additionally, DEEP
will require new signage at public docks, wharfs and piers, to inform
the public of the required change.
The Out Years
There will be minimal outyear costs associated with the replacement
of personal flotation devices.

WHILE ON PUBLIC DOCKS, WHARVES AND PIERS.
SUMMARY
This bill requires all children under age 12 to wear a personal
flotation device while on any public dock, wharf, or pier in the state. The
bill specifies that this requirement applies regardless of any existing
special act, municipal charter, or home rule ordinance provisions.
EFFECTIVE DATE: October 1, 2026

identical provisions.
