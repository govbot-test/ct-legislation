General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5277
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1280
Referred to Committee on LABOR AND PUBLIC EMPLOYEES
Introduced by:
(LAB)
AN ACT CONCERNING THE POSTING OF VETERANS' INFORMATION
AND SERVICES FOR DISPLAY IN THE WORKPLACE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The Labor Commissioner
2 and the Chief Manufacturing Officer, in consultation with the
3 Commissioner of Veterans Affairs, shall, within available
4 appropriations, post information on the Internet web sites of the Labor
5 Department and the Office of Manufacturing concerning benefits and
6 services available to veterans, as defined in section 27-103 of the general
7 statutes, in the state. Such information shall be in a form available for
8 downloading by an employer to display at such employer's place of
9 business.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
LCO No. 1280 1 of 2

Raised Bill No. 5277
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Labor Commissioner and the Chief Manufacturing
Officer, in consultation with the Commissioner of Veterans Affairs, to
post information on the Internet web sites of the Labor Department and
the Office of Manufacturing available for download by employers to
display in the workplace.
