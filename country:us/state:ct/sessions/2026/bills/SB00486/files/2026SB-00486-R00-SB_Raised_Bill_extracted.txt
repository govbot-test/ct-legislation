AN ACT IMPLEMENTING THE RECOMMENDATIONS OF THE
DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES CONCERNING
CRIMINAL BACKGROUND CHECKS OF EMPLOYEES WITH ACCESS
TO TAX RECORDS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 5-207a of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective from passage):
3 (a) For each position of employment with the state of Connecticut that
4 involves exposure to federal tax information, the [employing] agency
5 with custody of the federal tax information and [, in the case where the
6 Department of Administrative Services is the provider of human
7 resources services for such employing agency,] the Department of
8 Administrative Services, shall, subject to the provisions of section 31-51i,
9 require each applicant for, each employee applying for transfer to, and,
10 at least every five years, [or more often if required by the United States
11 Department of the Treasury,] each current employee of such a position,
12 to (1) state in writing whether such applicant or employee has been
13 convicted of a crime or whether criminal charges are pending against

This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage 5-207a
Statement of Purpose:
To require criminal background checks of employees with access to
federal tax records be conducted at least every five years.
