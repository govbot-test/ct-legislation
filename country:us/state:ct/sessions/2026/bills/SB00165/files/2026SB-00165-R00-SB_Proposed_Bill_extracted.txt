General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 165
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 904
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
EXPANSION OF BILL LIBRARY IN THE TOWN OF LEDYARD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 one million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Ledyard for the expansion of
7 the Bill Library.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the expansion of the Bill Library in the town of
Ledyard.
