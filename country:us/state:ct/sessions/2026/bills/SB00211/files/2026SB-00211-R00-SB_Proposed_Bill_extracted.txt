General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 211
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 23
Referred to Committee on INSURANCE AND REAL ESTATE
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT APPROPRIATING FUNDS TO INVESTIGATE AND ENFORCE
CONDUCT RELATING TO HEALTH INSURANCE PRACTICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of five million dollars be appropriated to the office of
2 the Attorney General from the General Fund, for the fiscal year ending
3 June 30, 2027, to: (1) Expand said office's capacity to investigate and
4 enforce conduct related to health insurance practices by health carriers
5 that impact access to health care, inflate consumer costs or violate
6 federal or state law; and (2) require that said office submit a report to
7 the joint standing committees of the General Assembly having
8 cognizance of matters relating to insurance and public health, not later
9 than February 1, 2028, summarizing any such investigation or
10 enforcement action taken by said office.
Statement of Purpose:
To: (1) Expand the capacity of the office of the Attorney General to
investigate and enforce conduct related to health insurance practices by
health carriers that impact access to health care, inflate consumer costs
or violate federal or state law; and (2) require that said office submit a
report to the joint standing committees of the General Assembly having
