General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5576
February Session, 2026
AN ACT AMENDING A PRIOR CONVEYANCE OF PARCELS OF
STATE LAND TO THE CITY OF NEW HAVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Special act 23-27 is amended to read as follows (Effective
2 from passage):
3 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the Board
4 of Regents for Higher Education shall convey to the city of New Haven
5 two parcels of land located in the city of New Haven, at a cost equal to
6 the administrative costs of making such conveyance. The first parcel of
7 land has an area of approximately 5.23 acres and is identified as Lot 300
8 in Block 1304 on city of New Haven Tax Assessor's Map 234, in book
9 2548 at page 348 of the city of New Haven Tax Assessor's records, and
10 is further identified on a map entitled "State of Connecticut, Department
11 of Administrative Services, Bureau of Public Works, Site Construction
12 Plan, for South Central Community College, 60 Sargent Drive, New
13 Haven, CT, Project No. BI-RCO-106, Drawing No. 901, William Francis
14 Griffin & Associates, Milford, Connecticut, Scale 1"=30', Date: January
15 8, 1976 – AS BUILT". The second parcel of land has an area of
16 approximately 2 acres and is identified as Lot 403 in Block 314 on city of
17 New Haven Tax Assessor's Map 234, in book 3529 at page 14 of the city
18 of New Haven Tax Assessor's records. Said parcels together shall be
19 referred to in this section as the "subject parcels".

Statement of Legislative Commissioners:
In Subsec. (c)(2), "two years after the effective date of this section" was
changed to "[two years after the effective date of this section] June 28,
2025", and in Subsec. (d), "sixty days after the effective date of this
section" was changed to "[sixty days after the effective date of this
section] August 27, 2023", for clarity; and in Subsec. (e), ", which quit
claim deed or instrument shall include provisions to carry out the
purposes of subsection (b) of this section" was added for accuracy and
consistency with standard drafting conventions.
