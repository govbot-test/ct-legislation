AN ACT ESTABLISHING PERSONAL INCOME TAX DEDUCTIONS
FOR TIPS OR GRATUITIES AND OVERTIME PAY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to establish
2 personal income tax deductions that mirror the federal income tax
3 deductions for tips or gratuities declared by a taxpayer and overtime
4 pay.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish personal income tax deductions that mirror the federal
income tax deductions for tips or gratuities declared by a taxpayer and
overtime pay.
