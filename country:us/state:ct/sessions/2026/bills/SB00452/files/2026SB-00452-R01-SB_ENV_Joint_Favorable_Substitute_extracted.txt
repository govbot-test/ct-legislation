Section 1:
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING THE RELEASE OF LIGHTER-THAN-AIR
BALLOONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 26-25c of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (a) No person, nonprofit organization, firm or corporation, including
4 the state and its political subdivisions, shall knowingly release, organize
5 the release of or intentionally cause to be released into the atmosphere
6 [within a twenty-four-hour period ten or more] any helium or other
7 lighter-than-air gas balloons in the state.
8 (b) Any retailer that sells or offers for sale any helium or other lighter-
9 than-air gas balloon in the state to any consumer shall not provide such
10 balloon to a consumer unless a weight is attached to such balloon that is
11 sufficient to prevent such balloon from rising into the atmosphere.
12 [(b)] (c) Any violation of [subsection (a) of] this section shall be an
13 infraction.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
