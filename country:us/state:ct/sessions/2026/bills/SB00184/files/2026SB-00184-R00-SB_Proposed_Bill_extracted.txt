General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 184
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1038

BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. FLEXER, 29th Dist.
REP. BOYD, 50th Dist.
REP. HADDAD, 54th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING CERTAIN ARTICLES OF CLOTHING FROM THE
SALES AND USE TAXES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 219 of the general statutes be amended to exempt from
2 the sales and use taxes the sale of and the storage, use or other
3 consumption in this state of articles of clothing costing under one
4 hundred dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt articles of clothing costing under one hundred dollars from
the sales and use taxes.
