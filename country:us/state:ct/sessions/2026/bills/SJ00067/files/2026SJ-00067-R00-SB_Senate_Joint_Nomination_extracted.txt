General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 67
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3048
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF SONIA J.
ELECTIONS ENFORCEMENT COMMISSION.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Sonia J.
2 Worrell-Asare of Windsor, to be appointed a member of the State
3 Elections Enforcement Commission, in succession to Andrew Cascudo,
4 to serve a term ending June 30, 2027, or until a successor is appointed
5 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
6 confirmed.
LCO No. 3048 1 of 1
