General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5332
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING CERTAIN BOATING REGULATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) No operator of a
2 motorboat under power shall allow any person in such motorboat to
3 hang any portion of the body over the top of the gunwale while
4 underway. The provisions of this section shall not apply to any person
5 in a motorboat that is engaged in anchoring, mooring or docking
6 activities and that is proceeding at dead slow speed.
7 Sec. 2. Subsection (m) of section 15-140c of the general statutes is
8 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
9 1, 2026):
10 (m) Any person who abandons a vessel shall have committed a
11 violation and shall be fined not less than [three hundred] seven hundred
12 fifty dollars nor more than [five hundred] one thousand dollars, except
13 if such abandonment occurred during a declared emergency and as a
14 result of the conditions that caused such emergency to be declared.
15 Notwithstanding the provisions of this section, the Commissioner of
16 Energy and Environmental Protection may authorize the recovery of
17 reasonable costs against the last owner of record of a vessel for the
18 recovery of an abandoned vessel and any associated environmental
19 remediation by any state or municipal law enforcement agency.
