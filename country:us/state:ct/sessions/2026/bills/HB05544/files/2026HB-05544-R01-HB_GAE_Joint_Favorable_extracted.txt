General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5544
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ALLOWING MUNICIPALITIES TO HOLD MUNICIPAL
ELECTIONS IN CONJUNCTION WITH STATE ELECTIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (a) of section 9-164 of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective January
3 1, 2027):
4 (a) (1) (A) On and after January 1, [2022] 2027, and notwithstanding
5 any contrary provision of law, there shall be held in each municipality,
6 biennially, a municipal election either (i) on the Tuesday after the first
7 Monday of November of the odd-numbered years, except that such
8 municipal election may be held on the first Monday of May of the odd-

Statement of Legislative Commissioners:
In Section 1(a)(1)(A)(ii), "by majority vote" was added for clarity; and in

first Tuesday after the first Monday in November of the even-numbered
years" was added for internal consistency.
