General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 172
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 886
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
N ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR RENOVATIONS
TO ST. MARY OF THE IMMACULATE CONCEPTION IN THE VILLAGE
OF BALTIC.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate six
4 hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to St. Mary of the Immaculate Conception in
7 the village of Baltic in the town of Sprague for renovations to the bell
8 tower and other structures.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to St. Mary of the Immaculate Conception in the
village of Baltic in the town of Sprague for renovations to the bell tower
and other structures.
