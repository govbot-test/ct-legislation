WEBER, ESQUIRE, OF GRANBY TO BE A JUDGE OF THE SUPERIOR
COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Rosemarie
2 T. Weber, Esquire, of Granby to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to
3 serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both
4 Houses, in succession to the Honorable Sheila M. Prats, is approved and
5 confirmed, and she is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for said
6 term.
