General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5527

Introduced by:
(GAE)
AN ACT CONCERNING THE PUBLICATION OF CERTAIN LEGAL
NOTICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 1-2 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 Each provision of the general statutes, the special acts or the charter
4 of any town, city or borough which requires the insertion of an
5 advertisement of a legal notice in a daily newspaper shall be construed
6 to permit such advertisement to be inserted in a weekly newspaper or,
7 if such provision applies to a town, city or borough, both posted on the
8 Internet web site of the town, city or borough and a printed copy posted
9 in the town or city hall and not less than two other conspicuous public
10 municipal locations in a central location in the town, city or borough,
11 including, but not limited to, a library or senior center, in lieu of a
12 newspaper; but this section shall not be construed to reduce or
13 otherwise affect the time required by law for giving such notice.
14 Whenever notice of any action or other proceeding is required to be
