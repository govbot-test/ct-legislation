OLR Bill Analysis
HB 5126
AN ACT CONCERNING THE LEGISLATIVE COMMISSIONERS'
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR MINOR AND TECHNICAL REVISIONS
TO STATUTES CONCERNING CONSUMER PROTECTION.
SUMMARY
This bill makes technical changes to statutes on the home condition
disclosure form, homemaker-companion agencies, and the Connecticut
Data Privacy Act (CTDPA).
EFFECTIVE DATE: Upon passage for the home condition disclosure
form provision; July 1, 2026, for the CTDPA provision; and October 1,
2026, for the homemaker-companion provision.
