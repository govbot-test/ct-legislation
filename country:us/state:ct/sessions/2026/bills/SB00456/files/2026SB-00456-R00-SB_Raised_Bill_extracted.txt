AN ACT CONCERNING A GRANT PROGRAM FOR WILDLIFE
REHABILITATION FACILITIES IN THE STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) The Commissioner of
2 Energy and Environmental Protection may establish a grant program
3 for the issuance of grants to nonprofit wildlife rehabilitation
4 organizations with one or more facilities in the state that register with
5 the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection and that meet
6 any requirements the commissioner establishes for the registration of
7 such wildlife rehabilitation organizations. The commissioner may
8 accept any funds or donations for the purpose of issuing such grants
9 and may establish an account of the General Fund for the receipt of any
10 such funds or donations. Any funds in such account shall not lapse at
11 the conclusion of any fiscal year and shall remain available to the
12 commissioner for the issuance of such grants.
13 (b) Any such registration requirement shall require a nonprofit
14 wildlife organization to submit, on an annual basis, to the commissioner
15 information concerning such organization, including, but not limited to,
