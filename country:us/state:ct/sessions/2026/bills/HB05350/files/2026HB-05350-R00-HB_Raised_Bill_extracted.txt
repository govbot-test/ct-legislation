Statement of Purpose:
To (1) replace "marijuana" with "cannabis", (2) redefine "cannabis", (3)
authorize qualifying out-of-state patients to access the state's palliative
use cannabis market, (4) provide that no producer shall be required to
staff any transport vehicle with more than one employee, (5) establish a
cannabis regulatory working group, (6) prohibit a retailer, hybrid

delinquency for any delinquent retailer, hybrid retailer or dispensary
facility, (8) require the Commissioner of Consumer Protection to adopt
