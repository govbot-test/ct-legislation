AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF UNIDENTIFIED AERIAL
PHENOMENA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) The University of Connecticut
2 shall, within available resources, conduct a study of unidentified aerial
3 phenomena. In conducting such study, university personnel shall
4 consult with personnel from the Department of Emergency Services and
5 Public Protection, the Department of Energy and Environmental
6 Protection, the Office of Military Affairs and the director of the
7 Connecticut chapter of the national nonprofit organization dedicated to
8 the investigation of UFO sightings, abductions, and other unexplained
9 phenomena, as deemed necessary to obtain information. The university
10 shall be given access to data collected by said state agencies. Such study
11 shall include, but need not be limited to: (1) Benefits of establishing a
12 state center for the study of unidentified aerial phenomena; and (2)
13 resources necessary to establish and operate such center.
14 (b) Not later than July 1, 2027, the university shall report the results
15 of the study to the joint standing committees of the General Assembly
