AN ACT CONCERNING PALLIATIVE AND HOSPICE CARE IN
LITCHFIELD COUNTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) The Commissioner of Social
2 Services, in consultation with the Commissioner of Public Health, shall
3 study the need for palliative and hospice care services in Litchfield
4 County. In conducting the study, the commissioner shall take into
5 consideration any findings concerning the need for such services by the
6 Palliative Care Advisory Council established pursuant to section 19a-6o
7 of the general statutes.
8 (b) Not later than January 1, 2027, the Commissioner of Social
9 Services shall file a report on the study, in accordance with the
10 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, with the joint
11 standing committees of the General Assembly having cognizance of
12 matters relating to human services and public health. If the study
13 concludes that there is a need for such services, the commissioner may,
14 within available appropriations, design and implement a pilot program
