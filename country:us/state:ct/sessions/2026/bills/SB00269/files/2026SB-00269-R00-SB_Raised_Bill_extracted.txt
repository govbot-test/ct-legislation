General Assembly Raised Bill No. 269
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1071
Referred to Committee on LABOR AND PUBLIC EMPLOYEES
Introduced by:
(LAB)
AN ACT CONCERNING WORKERS' RIGHTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Labor Commissioner shall
2 conduct a study on the rights of employees in the state. Not later than
3 January 1, 2027, the commissioner shall report the results of the study,
4 in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes,
5 to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
6 cognizance of matters relating to labor and public employees. Such
7 report may include the commissioner's recommendations for legislation
8 regarding the rights of employees in the state.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Labor Commissioner to conduct a study of the rights of
employees in the state.
