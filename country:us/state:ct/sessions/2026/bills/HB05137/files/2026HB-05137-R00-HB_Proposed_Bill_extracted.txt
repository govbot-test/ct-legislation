AN ACT ESTABLISHING A REFUNDABLE TAX CREDIT FOR NEWS
ORGANIZATIONS THAT COVER LOCAL COMMUNITIES IN THE
STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a refundable tax
2 credit to news organizations that cover local communities in the state,
3 in the amount of fifteen thousand dollars per journalist employed in the
4 state by such organization and twenty-five thousand dollars per new
5 journalist hired in the state by such organization, with a cap on the
6 amount a news organization is allowed of one hundred fifty thousand
7 dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a refundable tax credit to news organizations that cover
local communities in the state.
