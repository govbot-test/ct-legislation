AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR AUTISM SPECTRUM
DISORDER SERVICES.

Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of five million dollars be appropriated to the
2 Department of Social Services, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year
3 ending June 30, 2027, for the Autism Spectrum Disorder waiver
4 program.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase funding for the Autism Spectrum Disorder waiver program
to help alleviate the lengthy wait list.
