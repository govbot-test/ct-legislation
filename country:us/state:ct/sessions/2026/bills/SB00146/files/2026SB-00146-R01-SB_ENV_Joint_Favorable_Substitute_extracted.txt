General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 146
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING THE PROTECTION OF CROPS AND
LIVESTOCK FROM CERTAIN WILDLIFE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (e) of section 26-47 of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective from
3 passage):
4 (e) Notwithstanding the provisions of subsection (a) of this section,
5 the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection may issue
6 permits for the taking of wildlife that threatens or causes damage to
7 agricultural crops, livestock or apiaries, or that caused damage to crops
8 during the preceding growing season, as verified by the Department of
9 Energy and Environmental Protection if: (1) The owner or lessee of the
10 subject property utilized reasonable nonlethal efforts to protect such
11 [crops,] apiaries or livestock, including, but not limited to, electric
12 fencing, animal guardians or fortified enclosed structures, and (2) an
13 investigation by Department of Energy and Environmental Protection
14 personnel determines that the taking of such wildlife is necessary to
15 protect agricultural crops, apiaries or livestock from excessive damage
16 and that reasonable nonlethal efforts to protect such [crops,] apiaries or
17 livestock have not been or are not likely to be successful in preventing
18 further damage. Any such permit issued pursuant to this subsection
19 shall specify the means, methods and times for which such take is
