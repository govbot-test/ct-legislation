General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 53
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 322
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
ESTABLISHMENT OF A GARDEN AT YORK CORRECTIONAL
INSTITUTION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 fifty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Correction for the purpose of establishing a garden at the
6 York Correctional Institution to grow produce for the inmates.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a garden at York Correctional Institution to grow
fresh produce for the inmates.
