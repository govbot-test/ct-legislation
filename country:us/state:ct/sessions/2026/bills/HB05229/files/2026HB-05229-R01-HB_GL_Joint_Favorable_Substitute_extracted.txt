Statement of Legislative Commissioners:
In Section 1(c)(4), "request" was added after "initiates a withdrawal" and
", operator, provider or retailer, as applicable" was deleted, for clarity
and consistency, and "because of" was changed to "when" in Subpara.
(D), for clarity; in Section 1(c)(8)(I), "withdrawal" was added before
"request" in clause (ii) and "for such request" was added after
"timeframe" in clause (iii), for clarity; and in Section 3(k), references to
"operator, provider or retailer" or the plural thereof were deleted, for
consistency and clarity, and "recorded" was moved from after
"cancellations" to before "exceptions" in Subdiv. (5), for clarity.
