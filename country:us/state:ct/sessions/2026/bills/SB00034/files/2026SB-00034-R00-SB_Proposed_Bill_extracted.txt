Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 237
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
SEN. GORDON, 35th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING AN INCREASE OF THE FOUNDATION
AMOUNT FOR PURPOSES OF THE EDUCATION COST-SHARING
GRANT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That subdivision (9) of section 10-262f of the general statutes be
2 amended to redefine "foundation" by increasing the amount of the
3 foundation from eleven thousand five hundred twenty-five dollars to
4 sixteen thousand sixty-five dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide property tax relief.
LCO No. 237 1 of 1
