AN ACT CONCERNING THE SEWAGE RIGHT-TO-KNOW ACT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 22a-424a of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective from passage):
3 (a) For the purposes of this section:
4 (1) "Sewage treatment plant or collection system" means any sewage
5 treatment plant, water pollution control facility, related pumping
6 station, collection system or other public sewage works;
7 (2) "Sewage spill" means the diversion of wastes from any portion of
8 a sewage treatment plant or collection system in this state;
9 (3) "Combined sewer" means structures which are designed to
10 convey both sanitary and storm sewage, and allow the overflow of such
11 combined sewage, untreated, to the waters of the state during periods
12 of high flows; and
13 (4) "Electronic report" means a reporting form that uses an electronic
