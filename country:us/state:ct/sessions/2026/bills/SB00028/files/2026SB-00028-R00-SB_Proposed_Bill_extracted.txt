General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 28
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 87
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
AN ACT REQUIRING FUNDING FOR REGIONAL FIRE TRAINING
SCHOOLS THROUGH APPROPRIATIONS IN THE STATE BUDGET.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to require that sufficient funds
2 be appropriated to the Department of Emergency Services and Public
3 Protection, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30,
4 2027, to fund the nine regional fire training schools, as opposed to
5 providing such funds through any specific taxes or increased
6 surcharges, including, but not limited to, fees on insurance premiums.
Statement of Purpose:
To fund the nine regional fire training schools through appropriations
in the state budget and not from any specific taxes or increased
surcharges, including any fees on insurance premiums.
