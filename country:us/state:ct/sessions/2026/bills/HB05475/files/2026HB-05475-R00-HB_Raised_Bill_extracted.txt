Introduced by:
(GOS)
AN ACT CONCERNING OVERSIGHT OF PERMITTING PROCESSES
OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENTAL
PROTECTION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Not later than January 1, 2027, the
2 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection shall assess and
3 report on potential methods to facilitate the permitting process of
4 general permits, including (1) the use of artificial intelligence tools to do
5 the preliminary review of all applications, (2) identifying any permits
6 appropriate for third-party permitting and any statutory changes
7 required to effectuate such third-party permitting, and (3) the feasibility
8 of creating an expedited review process with increased fees for the
9 expedited service for certain permits and identifying any permits
10 appropriate for such service and any statutory changes required to
11 effectuate such expedited review process.
12 (b) Not later than January 1, 2027, the Commissioner of Energy and
13 Environmental Protection shall assess and report on methods to
14 facilitate the permitting process of individual permits, including, but not

sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection
to conduct an assessment of the general and individual permitting
processes and whether certain modifications are feasible or
recommended.
