AN ACT DESIGNATING THE STATE AS THE BIRTHPLACE OF THE
BOYS AND GIRLS CLUBS OF AMERICA, THE STATE AMPHIBIAN,
THE TOWN OF ENFIELD AS THE GATEWAY TO CONNECTICUT AND
THE STATE ROCK.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The state of Connecticut
2 shall be known as the birthplace of the Boys and Girls Clubs of America,
3 the largest youth organization in the country, which originated in
4 Hartford in 1860 as the Dashaway Club, founded by Mary Goodwin,
5 Alice Goodwin, Elizabeth Hamersley and Louisa Bushnell to get boys
6 off the streets and give them productive activities.
7 Sec. 2. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The spring peeper, Pseudacris
8 crucifer, shall be the state amphibian.
9 Sec. 3. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The town of Enfield shall be
10 known as the gateway to Connecticut.
11 Sec. 4. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) Housatonic marble shall be
