General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5048
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 606
Referred to Committee on ENERGY AND TECHNOLOGY
Introduced by:
REP. YACCARINO, 87th Dist.
AN ACT REQUIRING THE REMOVAL OF THE PUBLIC BENEFITS
CHARGE FROM CONSUMER ELECTRIC BILLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to (1) remove the charges
2 collectively called the "combined public benefits charge" from electric
3 bills for end use customers of each electric distribution company, and
4 (2) require that the cost of such charges be paid from the General Fund.
Statement of Purpose:
To remove the combined public benefits charge from electric customer
bills and pay the costs of such charge from the General Fund.
