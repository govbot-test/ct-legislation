AN ACT ESTABLISHING A JOB CLASSIFICATION FOR PART-TIME
FIRE SERVICE INSTRUCTORS.

Assembly convened:

2 Administrative Services, in consultation with the Commissioner of
3 Emergency Services and Public Protection and the State Fire
4 Administrator, shall establish a job classification for part-time fire

6 within the Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection.
