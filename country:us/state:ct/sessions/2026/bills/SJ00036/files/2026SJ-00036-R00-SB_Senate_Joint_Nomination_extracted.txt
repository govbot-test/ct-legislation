RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF DONALD E.
WILLIAMS, JR. OF BROOKLYN TO BE A MEMBER OF THE BOARD
OF REGENTS FOR HIGHER EDUCATION.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Donald E.
2 Williams, Jr. of Brooklyn to be a member of the Board of Regents for
3 Higher Education, in succession to Joann M. Ryan, to serve a term
4 ending June 30, 2030, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified,
5 whichever is longer is hereby approved and confirmed.
