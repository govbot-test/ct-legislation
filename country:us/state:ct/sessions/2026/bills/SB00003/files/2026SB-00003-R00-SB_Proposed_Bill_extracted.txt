AN ACT CONCERNING HEALTH CARE AFFORDABILITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to require an appropriation
2 from the General Fund for the purpose of covering the full cost of
3 increases in health care premiums for persons enrolled in Access Health
4 CT with household income between five hundred and six hundred per
5 cent of the federal poverty level.
Statement of Purpose:
To support affordable health care in the state and mitigate the effects of
federal cuts to health care premium subsidies.
