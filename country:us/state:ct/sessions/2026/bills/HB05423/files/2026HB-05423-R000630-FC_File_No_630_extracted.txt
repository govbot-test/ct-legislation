4 of the general statutes, to the joint standing committees of the General
5 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to the judiciary and
6 appropriations and the budgets of state agencies on the usage of court

OFA Fiscal Note
State Impact: None
Municipal Impact: None
Explanation
The bill requires the executive director of the Court Support Services
Division (CSSD) of the Judicial Branch to report on the expansion of
community support services, resulting in no fiscal impact because CSSD
has the expertise and resources to complete the report.

OFA Bill Analysis
HB 5423
AN ACT CONCERNING JUVENILE JUSTICE INITIATIVES.
SUMMARY
This bill requires the executive director of the Court Support Services
Division of the Judicial Branch, by 7/1/2026, to submit a report to
Judiciary and Appropriations Committees on the usage of court support
services for the previous fiscal year and recommendations for expansion
of these services.
