General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 21
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 129
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. PERILLO J., 21st Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE DISPOSAL OF DEBRIS
FROM THE MOUTH OF THE HOUSATONIC RIVER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of one hundred fifty thousand dollars be appropriated
2 to the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection, from the
3 General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for disposal of
4 surface debris from the mouth of the Housatonic River in the town of
5 Stratford.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the disposal of surface debris from the mouth of
the Housatonic River.
