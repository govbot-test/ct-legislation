General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 479
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING OVERSIGHT OF FEDERAL FUND
APPLICATIONS BY THE DEPARTMENT OF SOCIAL SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) As used in this section, (1)
2 "Rural Health Transformation Program" means a federal program
3 established pursuant to P.L. 119-21 that provides grants to states to
4 improve the health of rural residents; and (2) "technical assistance
5 funding" means federal grants to states to implement system
6 modifications to implement eligibility changes for certain public
7 assistance programs, including, but not limited to, Medicaid required
8 pursuant to P.L. 119-21.
9 (b) Not later than thirty days before submitting an application for
10 Rural Health Transformation Program grants or for technical assistance
11 funding, the Commissioner of Social Services shall forward such
12 application to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly
13 having cognizance of matters relating to human services. The committee
14 may schedule a public hearing on such application and vote on whether
15 to approve, deny or modify the application.
16 (c) If the committee does not vote to approve, deny or modify such
17 application not later than thirty days after receipt of such application
18 from the commissioner, the application shall be deemed approved. If
19 the committee votes to deny the application, the commissioner shall not
