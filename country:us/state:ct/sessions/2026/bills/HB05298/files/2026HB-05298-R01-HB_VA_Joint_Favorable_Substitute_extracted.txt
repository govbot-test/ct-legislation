General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5298
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A GRANT PROGRAM FOR CAPITAL
EXPENDITURE PROJECTS TO PLACE OR MAINTAIN WAR OR
VETERANS' MEMORIALS OR MONUMENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) For the fiscal year
2 ending June 30, 2028, and each fiscal year thereafter, the Department of
3 Veterans Affairs shall administer a program to provide grants to
4 municipalities and nonprofit providers of human or social services for
5 capital expenditure projects to place or maintain war or veterans'
6 memorials or monuments.
7 (b) Not later than January 1, 2027, the department shall develop
8 eligibility criteria to be used in selecting among applicants for such
9 grants, develop application forms and deadlines and post in a
10 conspicuous location on the department's Internet web site a description
11 of the grant program that includes, but is not limited to, such criteria,
12 forms and deadlines.
13 (c) (1) For the purposes described in subdivision (2) of this subsection,
14 the State Bond Commission shall have the power from time to time to
15 authorize the issuance of bonds of the state in one or more series and in
16 principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate two million dollars.
