AN ACT CONCERNING THE COMPOSITION OF FAIR RENT
COMMISSIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 7-148b of the 2026 supplement to the general
2 statutes is repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof
3 (Effective October 1, 2026):
4 (a) For purposes of this section and sections 7-148c to 7-148f,
5 inclusive, "seasonal basis" means housing accommodations rented for a
6 period or periods aggregating not more than one hundred twenty days
7 in any one calendar year, "rental charge" includes any fee or charge in
8 addition to rent that is imposed or sought to be imposed upon a tenant
9 by a landlord, and "municipality" means a town, city or consolidated
10 town and city.
11 (b) Any municipality may, and each municipality with a population
12 of fifteen thousand or more, as determined by the most recent decennial
13 census, shall, through its legislative body, adopt an ordinance that (1)
14 creates a fair rent commission, (2) establishes or joins the municipality
15 in a joint fair rent commission pursuant to subsection (d) of this section,

18 studies and investigations, conduct hearings and receive complaints
19 relative to rental charges on housing accommodations, except those
20 accommodations rented on a seasonal basis, within its jurisdiction,

32 pursuant to subsection (b) of this section shall adopt an ordinance

44 municipality to the commissioner.

46 ordinances adopted by their legislative bodies, establish a joint fair rent
