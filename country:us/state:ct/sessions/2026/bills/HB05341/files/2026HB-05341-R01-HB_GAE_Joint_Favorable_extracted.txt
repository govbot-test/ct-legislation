AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN ELECTRONIC PORTAL FOR
APPLICATIONS FOR STATE GRANT PROGRAMS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 4-68dd of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 (a) [The] Not later than July 1, 2027, the Secretary of the Office of
4 Policy and Management shall, within available appropriations,
5 establish and maintain a single electronic portal available on the Internet
6 and located on the Office of Policy and Management's Internet web site
7 concerning grants administered or provided by state agencies for the
8 purpose of posting all state-funded [municipal] grant applications. Such
9 electronic portal shall be known as the [Municipal] State Grant Portal.
10 (b) The [Municipal] State Grant Portal shall include, but not be
11 limited to: (1) All state-funded [municipal] grant applications, [and
12 municipal reimbursement request forms,] (2) a searchable database for
13 locating information regarding state-funded [municipal] grants, [and]
14 (3) an Internet web page dedicated to state-funded municipal grant
