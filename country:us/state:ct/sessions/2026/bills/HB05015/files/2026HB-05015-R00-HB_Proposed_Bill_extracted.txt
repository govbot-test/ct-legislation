Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 174
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. CARPINO, 32nd Dist.
AN ACT ELIMINATING THE HIGHER SALES AND USE TAXES RATE
FOR CERTAIN MOTOR VEHICLES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 219 of the general statutes be amended to increase to
2 more than one hundred thousand dollars the sales price threshold for a
3 motor vehicle subject to the seven and three-fourths per cent sales and
4 use taxes rate.
Statement of Purpose:
To eliminate the higher sales and use taxes rate for motor vehicles with
a sales price of less than one hundred thousand dollars.
LCO No. 174 1 of 1
