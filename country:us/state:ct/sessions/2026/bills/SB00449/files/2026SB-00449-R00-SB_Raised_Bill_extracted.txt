Statement of Purpose:
To (1) make revisions to statutes relating to the (A) local capital
improvement fund, (B) small town economic assistance program, and
(C) public school district repair and improvement project program, (2)
classify certain property owned or held in trust by the state as farm land,
forest land, open space land or maritime heritage land, and (3) repeal
statutes (A) relating to the intertown capital equipment purchase
incentive program, and (B) prohibiting zoning regulations from treating
family child care homes and group child care homes located in a
residence in a manner different from single or multifamily dwellings.
