OLR Bill Analysis
sSB 123
AN ACT REQUIRING PUBLIC HEARINGS FOR CERTAIN RATE
INCREASES AT ASSISTED LIVING FACILITIES.
SUMMARY
This bill requires assisted living services agencies (ALSAs) to hold an
informational hearing if an existing fee increases by more than 10%. The
hearing must (1) occur at least 30 days before the increase takes effect
and (2) provide residents, their representatives, and family members
with an opportunity to comment.
Under existing law, ALSAs must disclose fee increases to residents or
their representatives at least 60 days before they take effect. But ALSAs
may immediately adjust fees if (1) they are directly related to a change
in the level of care or services necessary to meet a resident’s safety needs
at the time of a scheduled resident care meeting or (2) a resident’s
condition changes so that it requires a change in service.
