General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5206

Introduced by:
REP. MARTINEZ, 22nd Dist.
REP. ROCHELLE, 104th Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TAX CREDIT AGAINST THE PERSONAL
INCOME TAX FOR VOLUNTEER FIREFIGHTERS WHO MEET
CERTAIN SERVICE REQUIREMENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to establish a tax
2 credit of not greater than two thousand five hundred dollars against the
3 personal income tax for volunteer firefighters who meet certain service
4 requirements, as certified by such volunteer firefighters' fire chief.
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a tax credit for volunteer firefighters who meet certain
service requirements.
