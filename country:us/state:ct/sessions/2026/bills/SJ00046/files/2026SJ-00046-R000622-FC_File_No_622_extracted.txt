RESOLUTION ACCEPTING THE RECOMMENDATION OF THE
CLAIMS COMMISSIONER TO MAKE PAYMENT IN EXCESS OF
THIRTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS WITH RESPECT TO THE CLAIM
AGAINST THE STATE OF KALLMAN, MCKINNELL AND WOOD,
ARCHITECTS, INC.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the recommendation of the Claims Commissioner, file number
2 27513 of said commissioner, that an award of $106,568.64, payable from
3 the appropriation to the adjudicated claims account of the General
4 Fund, be granted to Kallman, McKinnell and Wood, Architects, Inc., on
5 such claim against the state, is accepted and that payment of said
6 amount to said claimant is ordered.
