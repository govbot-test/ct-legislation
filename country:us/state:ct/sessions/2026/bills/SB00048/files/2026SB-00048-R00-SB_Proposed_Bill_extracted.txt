General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 48
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 297
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. BERTHEL, 32nd Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE PURCHASE
OF AUTOMATIC EXTERNAL DEFIBRILLATORS AT SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two hundred fifty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used
5 by the Department of Education for the purpose of providing grants-in-
6 aid to schools for purchasing automatic external defibrillators.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to schools for the purchase of automatic external
defibrillators.
