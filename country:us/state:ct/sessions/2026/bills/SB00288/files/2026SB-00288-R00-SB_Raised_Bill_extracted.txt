Statement of Purpose:
To (1) authorize additional nursing home beds in certain geographic
areas of the state under certain conditions, (2) require licensed nursing
homes to submit resident data to the Commissioner of Social Services in
a timely fashion to ensure proper calculation of reimbursement rates,
and (3) authorize the commissioner not to include related parties as
employees when considering costs of a nursing home in rate
determinations.
