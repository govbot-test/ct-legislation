General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5176
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 975
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. MUSHINSKY, 85th Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING FUNDING FOR SPANISH COMMUNITY OF
WALLINGFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of one hundred fifty thousand dollars be appropriated
2 to the Department of Social Services, from the General Fund, for the
3 fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for a grant-in-aid to Spanish
4 Community of Wallingford.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to Spanish Community of Wallingford for
education, public health, social services and job training.
