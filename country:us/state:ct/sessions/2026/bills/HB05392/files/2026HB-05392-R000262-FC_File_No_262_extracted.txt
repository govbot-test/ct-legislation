AN ACT CONCERNING MUNICIPAL AGENCIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 7-148l of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 Any person aggrieved by any order of the board, commission,
4 council, committee or other agency established or designated pursuant
5 to sections 7-148i to 7-148n, inclusive, and subparagraph (B) of
6 subdivision (9) of subsection (c) of section 7-148 may appeal to the State
7 Commission on Human Rights and Opportunities. Any such appeal
8 shall be filed [within] not later than thirty days [of] after the mailing of
9 the written decision.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
