February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1050
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING CERTAIN REVISIONS TO THE DEPARTMENT
OF ENERGY AND ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION'S PERMITTING
AUTHORITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Except as prohibited in
2 subdivision (1) of subsection (i) of section 22a-208a of the general
3 statutes, the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection
4 may, notwithstanding any provision of title 22a of the general statutes,
5 issue a general permit for any activity that the commissioner may
6 authorize by issuance of an individual permit.
7 Sec. 2. Subsection (j) of section 22a-208a of the general statutes is
8 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
9 2026):
10 (j) When a permittee makes a sufficient application for the renewal of
11 an individual permit to operate a solid waste facility, in accordance with
12 the provisions of subsection (b) of this section, the permit shall be
13 deemed renewed if, not less than ninety days after determining the
14 application is sufficient, the commissioner does not sign a notice of

38 renew such approval provided the total period of approval does not
39 exceed five years. The commissioner may order summary suspension of

41 Notwithstanding the renewal process, any person may seek, or the
42 commissioner may require, that the project obtain a general or
