General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 9
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1509
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF WILLIAM D.
THE STATE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION ARBITRATION PANEL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of William D.
2 Logue of West Hartford, to be reappointed a member of the State
3 Department of Education Arbitration Panel, as a neutral arbitrator, to
4 serve a term ending June 30, 2029, or until a successor is appointed and
5 has qualified, whichever is longer is hereby approved and confirmed.
LCO No. 1509 1 of 1
