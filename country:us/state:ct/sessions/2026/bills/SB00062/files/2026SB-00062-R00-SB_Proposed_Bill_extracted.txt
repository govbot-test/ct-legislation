General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 62
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 109
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. HARDING, 30th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
ENHANCEMENT AND EXTENSION OF HIGH-SPEED BROADBAND
INTERNET ACCESS IN RURAL COMMUNITIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 ___ dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the Department of
5 Energy and Environmental Protection, for the purpose of providing
6 grants-in-aid for the enhancement and extension of high-speed
7 broadband Internet access in rural communities.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to improve access to quality Internet and cellular
telephone service in rural communities.
