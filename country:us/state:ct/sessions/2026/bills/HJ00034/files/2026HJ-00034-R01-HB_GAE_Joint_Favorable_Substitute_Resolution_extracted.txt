RESOLUTION COMMEMORATING THE TWENTY-SEVENTH
ANNIVERSARY OF THE CONNECTICUT-TAIWAN SISTER-STATE
RELATIONSHIP.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 WHEREAS, the state of Connecticut and the Republic of China
2 (Taiwan) have enjoyed a long history of friendship and have maintained
3 a fruitful sister-state relationship encompassing cultural, educational
4 and economic exchanges since 1999; and
5 WHEREAS, the state of Connecticut exported 335 million dollars'
6 worth of products to Taiwan in 2025, making Taiwan one of the most
7 important foreign markets for Connecticut, and strengthening a robust
8 bilateral trade relationship as well as fostering a continued collaboration
9 between their respective industries; and
10 WHEREAS, the United States-Taiwan Education Initiative was
11 launched in 2020 to strengthen cooperation on international education
12 and highlight Taiwan's ability to provide instruction in Mandarin, and
13 given Mandarin's designation as a critical language for maintaining
14 national security by the United States, the state of Connecticut welcomes
15 opportunities to advance educational exchanges with Taiwan and
16 signed an Education Exchange Memorandum in January 2025; and
