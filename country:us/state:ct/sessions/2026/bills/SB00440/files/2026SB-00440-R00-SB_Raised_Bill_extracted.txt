AN ACT CONCERNING UNEMPLOYMENT FOR STRIKING WORKERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:

2 general statutes is repealed and the following is substituted in lieu

4 (3) During any week in which the administrator finds that the
5 individual's total or partial unemployment is due to the existence of a

Section 1 October 1, 2026 31-236(a)(3)
Statement of Purpose:
To allow certain striking workers involved in a labor dispute that has
been continuous for fourteen days access to unemployment benefits.
