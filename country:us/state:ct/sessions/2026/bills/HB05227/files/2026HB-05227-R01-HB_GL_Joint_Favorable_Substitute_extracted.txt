AN ACT CONCERNING COMMERCIAL MOLD REMEDIATION AND
FIRE AND CATASTROPHIC RESTORATION BUSINESSES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) As used in this section and
2 sections 2 to 6, inclusive, of this act:
3 (1) "Commercial mold remediation" (A) means mold remediation
4 performed in any building, or any portion thereof, that is not used or
5 designed to be used as (i) a dwelling place, or (ii) a private residence or

24 Sec. 2. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) On and after January 1, 2027,

32 Sec. 3. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) A person seeking to perform
33 commercial mold remediation in this state for compensation shall
34 register with the Department of Consumer Protection in a form and
35 manner prescribed by the Commissioner of Consumer Protection. The
36 department shall not issue a certificate of registration to any person
37 under this section unless the person seeking such certificate of
38 registration submits proof to the department, satisfactory to the
39 commissioner, that such person (1) is certified as a mold remediation
40 specialist by the Institute of Inspection Cleaning and Restoration
41 Certification, (2) is certified as a mold remediator by the National
42 Organization of Remediators and Microbial Inspectors, or (3) is certified
43 to perform mold remediation by any organization approved by the
44 commissioner, provided the commissioner posts notice of such
45 approval and the name of such approved organization on the
46 department's Internet web site.
47 Sec. 4. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) A person who holds a valid
48 certificate of registration issued by the Department of Consumer

Statement of Legislative Commissioners:
In Section 2, "Beginning on" was changed to "On and after" for
consistency with standard drafting conventions; in Section 4, "to said
standard" was added for clarity; in Section 6 "any of the provisions of"
was added for consistency with standard drafting conventions; and in
Section 7(a)(3), (b), (e)(1), (e)(3), (e)(4) and (g), references to
engagement "in the fire and catastrophic restoration business" were
changed to references to engagement "in fire and catastrophic
restoration work" for clarity.
