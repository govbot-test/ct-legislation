AN ACT REVISING THE CALCULATION OF THE HOSPITAL TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 211c of the general statutes be amended to revise the
2 calculation of the hospital tax to be based on the number of Medicaid,
3 Medicare and uninsured patients served.
Statement of Purpose:
To revise the calculation of the hospital tax to be based on the number
of Medicaid, Medicare and uninsured patients served.
