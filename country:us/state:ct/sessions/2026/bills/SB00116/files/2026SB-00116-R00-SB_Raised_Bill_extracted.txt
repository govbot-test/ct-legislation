Statement of Purpose:
To (1) require the Commissioner of Revenue Services to periodically
disclose to the Commissioner of Consumer Protection the name and
address of each operator of a hotel, lodging house or bed and breakfast
establishment who has remitted the use tax, (2) impose penalties on
operators of hotels, motels, inns or similar lodgings who fail to satisfy
various recordkeeping, training, occupancy rate and notice
requirements, and (3) grant additional enforcement powers to the
Commissioner of Consumer Protection.
