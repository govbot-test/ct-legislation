General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5151
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 843
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING PROPOSALS DERIVED FROM THE WASTE
CHARACTERIZATION STUDY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) Not later than January 15, 2027, the
2 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection, in accordance
3 with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, shall submit
4 to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
5 cognizance of matters relating to the environment a report that details
6 any proposal or recommendation of the commissioner that is derived
7 from the results of the most recent waste characterization study
8 undertaken by the Department of Energy and Environmental
9 Protection. Such report shall include, but not be limited to, any
10 recommendation for the establishment of extended producer
11 responsibility programs for components of the state's waste stream,
12 including, but not limited to, consumer packaging or products and
13 construction and demolition debris and any proposed additional
14 designations of recyclable items to be source separated. Such report
15 shall include a draft of any statutory revisions required to implement
16 any such proposal or recommendation.
