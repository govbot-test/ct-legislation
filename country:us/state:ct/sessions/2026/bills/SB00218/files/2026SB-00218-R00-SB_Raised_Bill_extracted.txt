General Assembly Raised Bill No. 218
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1566
Referred to Committee on BANKING
Introduced by:
(BA)
AN ACT REQUIRING A STUDY RELATING TO BANKING ISSUES IN
THE STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Department of Banking shall
2 conduct a study relating to banking issues in the state. Not later than
3 January 15, 2027, the department shall submit a report, in accordance
4 with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, concerning
5 the results of such study to the joint standing committee of the General
6 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to banking.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Department of Banking to study and submit a report
concerning banking issues in the state.
