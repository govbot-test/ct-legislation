General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5233
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 480
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. LUXENBERG, 12th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
IMPROVEMENTS TO WATER AND SEWER INFRASTRUCTURE IN
THE TOWN OF MANCHESTER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Energy and Environmental Protection for the purpose of
6 providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Manchester for improvements
7 to the water and sewer infrastructure in the north end of said town.
Statement of Purpose:
To provided funding for improvements to the water and sewer
infrastructure in the north end of the town of Manchester.
