Explanation
The bill repeals an approved conveyance of a specified parcel of land
from the Department of Transportation to the town of Canton. The
repeal results in the state retaining use of the asset and precludes a
revenue gain up to the fair market value of the property. The town of
Canton would forego gaining use of an asset and paying the fair market
value and administrative costs of the conveyance.
