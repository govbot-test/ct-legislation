General Assembly Raised Bill No. 224

AN ACT EXEMPTING BALLOTS CAST AT ELECTIONS, PRIMARIES
AND REFERENDA FROM DISCLOSURE UNDER THE FREEDOM OF
INFORMATION ACT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Each ballot that has been cast
2 at an election, primary or referendum, including any write-in ballot,
3 shall be exempt from disclosure under the Freedom of Information Act.
4 Nothing in this section shall be construed to impair the ability to
5 conduct any recanvass or audit under chapter 147, 148, 152 or 153 of the
6 general statutes. As used in this section, "ballot", "election",
7 "referendum" and "write-in ballot" have the same meanings as provided
8 in section 9-1 of the general statutes, as amended by this act, "primary"
9 has the same meaning as provided in section 9-372 of the general
10 statutes and "Freedom of Information Act" has the same meaning as
11 provided in section 1-200 of the general statutes.
12 Sec. 2. Subsection (n) of section 9-1 of the general statutes is repealed
13 and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
