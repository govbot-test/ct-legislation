General Assembly Raised Bill No. 454
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2418
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING PARK REPLACEMENT BY MUNICIPALITIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 7-131n of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective from passage):
3 If any municipality takes, converts or repurposes any land, including,
4 but not limited to, any land in such municipality's possession, for
5 highway or other nonpark or nonopen space purposes, which land was
6 purchased or acquired for park or other recreational or open space
7 purposes, or for which bonds were issued for such purposes, or which
8 had been dedicated for such purposes, such municipality shall provide
9 comparable replacement land at least equal in value and per unit area
10 size to the value and per unit area size of the land taken, converted or
11 repurposed; provided before such municipality takes, converts or
12 repurposes such land for highway or other nonpark or nonopen space
13 purposes it shall hold a public hearing in addition to any public hearing
14 required by section 13a-58 or by any other section of the general statutes
15 or by any special act or city charter. At such public hearing and in the
16 notice thereof, the municipality shall set forth the description of the land

29 For purposes of this section "municipality" means any town, city or
30 borough, or other political subdivision of the state.

Statement of Purpose:
To clarify certain provisions of the park and open space replacement
statute for municipalities.
