General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 495
February Session, 2026
AN ACT EXPANDING THE MEMBERSHIP AND STUDY FOCUS OF
THE LONG-TERM CARE PLANNING COMMITTEE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 17b-337 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 (a) There shall be established a Long-Term Care Planning Committee
4 for the purpose of exchanging information on long-term care issues,
5 coordinating policy development and establishing a long-term care plan
6 for all persons in need of long-term care. Such policy and plan shall
7 provide that individuals with long-term care needs have the option to
8 choose and receive long-term care and support in the least restrictive,
9 appropriate setting. Such plan shall integrate the three components of a
10 long-term care system including home and community-based services,
11 supportive housing arrangements and nursing facilities. Such plan shall
12 include: (1) A vision and mission statement for a long-term care system;
13 (2) the current number of persons receiving services; (3) the current
14 number of persons receiving long-term care supports and services in the
15 community and the number receiving such supports and services in
16 institutions; (4) demographic data concerning such persons by service
17 type; (5) the current aggregate cost of such system of services; (6)
18 forecasts of future demand for services; (7) the type of services available
19 and the amount of funds necessary to meet the demand; (8) projected
