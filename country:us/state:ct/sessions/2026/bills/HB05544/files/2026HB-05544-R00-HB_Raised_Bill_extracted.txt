Raised Bill No. 5544
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) allow a municipality, by vote of its legislative body, to change
from holding its municipal election in the odd-numbered years to
holding its municipal election in conjunction with the state election in
the even-numbered years, and (2) modify the laws relating to the
transitional terms of office whenever such a change is made.
