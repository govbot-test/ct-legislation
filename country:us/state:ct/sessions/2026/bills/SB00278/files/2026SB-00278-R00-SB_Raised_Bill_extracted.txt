This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 46b-15(b)
Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 46b-16a(b)
Sec. 3 October 1, 2026 51-5c(a)
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) provide that courts may consider prior or existing military
protective orders that are contained in the state's automated registry of
protective orders when issuing a civil protective order for victims of
domestic violence, sexual assault, sexual violence or stalking, and (2)
specify that such registry contains military protective orders that have
been entered into the National Crime Information Center computerized
index.
