AN ACT ESTABLISHING THE FEDERAL CUTS RESPONSE FUND.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a fund to be
2 known as the "Federal Cuts Response Fund". The fund shall contain any
3 moneys required by law to be deposited in the fund and shall be held
4 by the Treasurer separate and apart from all other moneys, funds and
5 accounts. Amounts in the fund shall be expended only pursuant to
6 appropriation by the General Assembly. Except as provided in
7 subsection (b) of this section, any balance remaining in the fund at the
8 end of a fiscal year shall be carried forward in the fund for the
9 subsequent fiscal year.
10 (b) After the accounts for the Federal Cuts Response Fund have been
11 closed for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, and the Comptroller has
12 determined the amount of the unexpended balance in said fund, the
13 amount of such unexpended balance shall be transferred by the
