Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 132
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. PERILLO J., 21st Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING THE PER DIEM RATE FOR MEMBERS OF THE
STATE BOARD OF LABOR RELATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That subsection (a) of section 31-102 of the general statutes be
2 amended to increase the pay a member or alternate member receives for
3 each day during which such member is engaged in the duties of the
4 board from one hundred fifty dollars to three hundred dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase the per diem rate for regular and alternate members of the
State Board of Labor Relations.
LCO No. 132 1 of 1
