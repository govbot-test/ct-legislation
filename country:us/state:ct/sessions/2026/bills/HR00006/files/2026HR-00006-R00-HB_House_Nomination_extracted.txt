RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF DENIS J. NAYDEN
OF JUPITER, FLORIDA TO BE A MEMBER OF THE BOARD OF
TRUSTEES FOR THE UNIVERSITY OF CONNECTICUT.
Resolved by this House:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Denis J.
2 Nayden of Jupiter, Florida, to be a member of the Board of Trustees for
3 The University of Connecticut, in succession to Rebecca Lobo, to serve a
4 term ending June 30, 2027, or until a successor is appointed and has
5 qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
