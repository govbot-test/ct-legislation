General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5068
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 267
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. BUCKBEE, 67th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING REIMBURSEMENTS TO MUNICIPALITIES
FOR REVENUE LOSS ASSOCIATED WITH A VETERANS PROPERTY
TAX CREDIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to require the state to reimburse
2 each municipality fifty per cent of the revenue loss caused by a property
3 tax exemption for veterans pursuant to subdivision (83) of section 12-81
4 of the general statutes.
Statement of Purpose:
To require the state to partially reimburse municipalities for the cost
associated with administering a veterans property tax credit.
