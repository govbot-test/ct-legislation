OLR Bill Analysis
sSB 452
AN ACT CONCERNING THE RELEASE OF LIGHTER-THAN-AIR
BALLOONS.
SUMMARY
The bill prohibits knowingly releasing, organizing the release of, or
intentionally causing the release of any number of helium or other
lighter-than-air gas balloons into the atmosphere. Under current law,
releases of less than 10 balloons within a 24-hour period are allowed.
The bill also requires retailers that sell these balloons to attach
weights to them so they cannot rise into the atmosphere.
The bill makes violations of both provisions infractions. Under
current law, the release of 10 or more helium or lighter-than-air balloons
in a 24-hour period is an infraction.
EFFECTIVE DATE: October 1, 2026.
BACKGROUND
Fines
Infractions are punishable by fines, usually set by Superior Court
judges, of between $35 and $90, plus a $20 or $35 surcharge and an
additional fee based on the amount of the fine. The total current fine for
the illegal release of balloons is $75.
