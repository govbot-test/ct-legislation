General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 62
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3221
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF CHRISTINA D.
GHIO OF CHESHIRE TO BE THE CHILD ADVOCATE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Christina D.
2 Ghio of Cheshire, to be the Child Advocate, in succession to Sarah Healy
3 Eagan, to serve a term ending five years from the date of confirmation,
4 or until a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever is longer,
5 is hereby approved and confirmed.
