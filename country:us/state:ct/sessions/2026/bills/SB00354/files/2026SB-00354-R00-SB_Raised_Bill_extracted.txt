AN ACT CONCERNING STAFFING IN THE UNEMPLOYMENT
INSURANCE DIVISION IN THE LABOR DEPARTMENT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) For the fiscal year ending June 30,
2 2027, and each fiscal year thereafter, the Labor Commissioner shall
3 employ not fewer than ____ employees at the Unemployment Insurance
4 Benefits Division within the Labor Department for the purpose of
5 processing and administering unemployment benefit claims.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To increase the number of staff at the Unemployment Insurance Benefits
Division in the Labor Department.
