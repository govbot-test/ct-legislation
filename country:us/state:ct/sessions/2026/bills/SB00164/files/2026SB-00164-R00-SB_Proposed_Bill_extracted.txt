Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 905
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE
IMPLEMENTATION AND CONSTRUCTION OF A SEWER AND WATER
LINE IN THE TOWN OF FRANKLIN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 two million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Energy and Environmental Protection for the purpose of
6 providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Franklin for the implementation
7 and construction of a sewer and water line in such town with a
8 connection into the city of Norwich.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the implementation and construction of a sewer
and water line in the town of Franklin.
