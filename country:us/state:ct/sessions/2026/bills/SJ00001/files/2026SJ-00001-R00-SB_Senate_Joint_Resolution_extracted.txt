General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 1
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 410
Referred to Committee on NO COMMITTEE
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONCERNING PUBLICATION OF LEGISLATIVE
BULLETIN, PRINTING OF BILLS AND EXPENSES.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the clerk of the House and the clerk of the Senate shall cause to
2 be published, from time to time, as they find suitable, notices of
3 caucuses, committee hearings, meetings and announcements of interest
4 to members of the General Assembly, following the plan of the
5 Legislative Bulletin published at previous sessions; and
6 That the joint committee on legislative management is authorized to
7 pay the cost of printing of bills for the 2026 session of the General
8 Assembly; and other necessary expenses of the 2026 session of the
9 General Assembly.
