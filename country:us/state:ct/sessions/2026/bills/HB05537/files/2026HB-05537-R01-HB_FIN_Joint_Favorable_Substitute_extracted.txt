General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5537
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A WORKING GROUP TO STUDY WAYS TO
FUND A UNIVERSAL FREE SCHOOL MEALS PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a working
2 group to study ways to fund a universal free school meals program that
3 would assist public schools to provide free breakfasts and lunches to all
4 public school students. The study shall examine (1) the amount of
5 funding projected to be required for such program, (2) possible sources
6 for funding, including, but not limited to, the imposition of a dedicated
7 tax or fee, (3) the parameters for the establishment of such program and
8 for disbursement of funds, and (4) any other matters relevant to the
9 establishment or funding of such program.
10 (b) The working group shall consist of (1) the chairpersons of the joint
11 standing committees of the General Assembly having cognizance of
12 matters relating to finance, revenue and bonding, education, children
13 and public health, (2) representatives of public schools and local or
14 regional boards of education, (3) public health or nutrition experts, and
15 (4) any other individuals or entities the chairpersons deem desirable or
16 necessary to carry out the provisions of this section. The members set
17 forth in subdivisions (2) to (4), inclusive, of this subsection shall be
18 appointed by the chairpersons of the joint standing committee of the
19 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to finance,
