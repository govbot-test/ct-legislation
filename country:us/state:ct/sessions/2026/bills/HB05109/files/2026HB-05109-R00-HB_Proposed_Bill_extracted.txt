February Session, 2026 LCO No. 564
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
AN ACT REPLACING THE CANNABIS TOTAL THC TAX WITH AN
EXCISE TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 214c of the general statutes be amended to replace the
2 cannabis tax based on total THC with an excise tax of ten and seventy-
3 five-hundredths per cent that mirrors Massachusetts' tax rate.
Statement of Purpose:
To replace the cannabis tax based on total THC with an excise tax of ten
and seventy-five-hundredths per cent.
