AN ACT AMENDING A PRIOR CONVEYANCE OF STATE LAND TO
THE CITY OF BRIDGEPORT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 15 of special act 07-11, as amended by section 4 of
2 public act 10-1 of the June special session and section 10 of special act
3 15-1 of the June special session, is amended to read as follows (Effective
4 from passage):
5 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the
6 Commissioner of Transportation shall convey to the Bridgeport Port
7 Authority a parcel of land located in the city of Bridgeport, at a cost
8 equal to the administrative costs of making such conveyance. Said
9 parcel of land has an area of approximately 1.008 acres and is identified
10 as a certain parcel of land situated in the city of Bridgeport, county of
11 Fairfield, and state of Connecticut, being more particularly bounded
12 and described as follows:
13 "Beginning at a point, said point being the southeast corner of the
14 intersection of Seaview Avenue and the eastbound off-ramp of
15 Interchange 29, Interstate 95;

16 Thence running southwesterly along land of the Bridgeport Port
17 Authority, 620', more or less;
18 Thence running northerly along the easterly highway line of

20 less;
21 Thence running easterly along the southerly highway line of said
22 eastbound off-ramp of Interchange 29, Interstate Route 95, 440' more or

24 The conveyance shall be subject to the approval of the State Properties
25 Review Board.
26 (b) (1) The Bridgeport Port Authority shall use said parcel of land for
27 economic development or waterfront related purposes and may sell or
28 lease all or any portion of said parcel for economic development or
29 waterfront related purposes. If the Bridgeport Port Authority:
30 (A) Does not use said parcel for said purposes; or
31 (B) Sells or leases all or any portion of said parcel, except for a sale or
32 lease of all or any portion of said parcel for economic development or
33 waterfront related purposes, in accordance with the provisions of this
34 subsection, the parcel shall revert to the state of Connecticut. Any sale
35 or lease of said parcel in accordance with this section shall be for [the
36 fair market value of the property or lease of said property, as
37 determined by the average of the appraisals of two independent
38 appraisers selected by the commissioner] one hundred nineteen
39 thousand dollars. Any funds received by the Bridgeport Port Authority
40 from a sale or lease of said parcel in accordance with this section shall
41 be transferred to the State Treasurer for deposit in the Special
42 Transportation Fund.
43 (2) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the
44 Department of Transportation shall grant the Bridgeport Port Authority
