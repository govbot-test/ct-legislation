AN ACT STUDYING EFFORTS TO INCREASE VOTER
PARTICIPATION AND AUTHORIZING MUNICIPAL PILOT PROGRAMS
TO PROMOTE SUCH EFFORTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) There is established a task force to
2 study (1) efforts to achieve one hundred per cent voter participation in
3 this state by January 1, 2030, (2) means of enhancing civic engagement
4 generally in this state, (3) experiences of jurisdictions that have enacted
5 policies to require citizens to vote as a civic duty, (4) potential
6 implementation options in pursuit of achieving such one hundred per
7 cent voter participation goal, (5) needs of registrars of voters, town
8 clerks and other municipal officials in administering elections in which
9 one hundred per cent of voters participate, and (6) state and municipal
10 budgetary resources that may be required to achieve such one hundred
11 per cent voter participation goal.
12 (b) The task force shall consist of the following members:
13 (1) One appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives;

Statement of Purpose:
To (1) establish a task force to study increasing voter participation, and
civic engagement generally, in this state, and (2) allow municipalities to
establish pilot programs to promote the goal of achieving one hundred
per cent voter participation.
