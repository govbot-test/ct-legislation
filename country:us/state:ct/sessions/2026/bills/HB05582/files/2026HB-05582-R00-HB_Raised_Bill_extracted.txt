Section 1 from passage PA 18-154, Sec. 4
Statement of Purpose:
To amend a prior conveyance of a parcel of state land to the town of
Stratford to require that a portion of said parcel that was retained by the
state be conveyed to the town of Stratford, subject to an agreement with
the town that the parcel continue to be used by the state.
