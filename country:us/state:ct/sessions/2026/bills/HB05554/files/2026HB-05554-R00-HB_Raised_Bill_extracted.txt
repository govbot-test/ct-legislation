AN ACT CONCERNING THE MEMBERSHIP OF THE LEGISLATIVE
REGULATION REVIEW COMMITTEE AND AGENCY REPORTS
CONCERNING A REVIEW OF REGULATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (a) of section 4-170 of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
3 1, 2026):

5 regulations of the several state departments and agencies following the

9 years, by the [speaker of said House] appointing authority, and six
10 members of the Senate, [three from each major party,] to be appointed
11 on or before said dates by the [president pro tempore of the Senate]
12 appointing authority. The number of major party and minor party
13 members of the Senate shall be in proportion to the number of members

Statement of Purpose:
To modify the membership and leadership of the legislative regulation
review committee, and to require agencies to submit a status report
concerning their progress on reviewing their regulations annually.
