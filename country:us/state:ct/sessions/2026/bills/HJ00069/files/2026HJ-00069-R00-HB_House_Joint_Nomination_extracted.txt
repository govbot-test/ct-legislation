General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 69
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3246
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF PATRICK M.
THE SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Patrick M.
2 Fahey, Esquire, of South Glastonbury to be a Judge of the Superior
3 Court, to serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation
4 by both Houses, in succession to the Honorable Susan Q. Cobb, is
5 approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior
6 Court for said term.
LCO No. 3246 1 of 1
