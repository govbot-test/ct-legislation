General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5182
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 864
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. WILSON, 46th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR CAPITAL
IMPROVEMENTS TO FIRE SERVICE ORGANIZATIONS IN THE CITY
OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 nine million two hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall
5 be used by the Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection
6 for the purpose of providing the city of Norwich (1) a grant-in-aid in the
7 amount of two million dollars to the Laurel Hill Volunteer Fire
8 Company for capital improvements; (2) a grant-in-aid in the amount of
9 one million dollars to the East Great Plain Volunteer Fire Department
10 for capital improvements; and (3) a grant-in-aid in the amount of six
11 million two hundred thousand dollars to the Norwich Fire Department
12 for capital improvements and apparatus acquisition.

Proposed Bill No. 5182
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for capital improvements at Laurel Hill Volunteer
Fire Company, East Great Plain Volunteer Fire Department and
Norwich Fire Department in the city of Norwich.
