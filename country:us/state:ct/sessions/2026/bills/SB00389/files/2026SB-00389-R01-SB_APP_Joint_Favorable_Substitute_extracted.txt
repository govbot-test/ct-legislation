General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 389
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING YOUTH EMPLOYMENT AND TRAINING
FUNDS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 31-3mm of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 (a) The Labor Department, within available appropriations, shall
4 establish a program to distribute youth employment and training funds
5 to regional workforce development boards for services to persons ages
6 fourteen to twenty-four.
7 (b) Funds provided for in this section shall be allocated [as follows:
8 (1) Thirty-two and five-tenths per cent to Capitol Workforce Partners;
9 (2) twenty-two and five-tenths per cent to The Workforce Alliance; (3)
10 twelve and five-tenths per cent to The Workplace, Inc.; (4) twenty-two
11 and five-tenths per cent to the Northwest Regional Workforce
12 Investment Board, Inc.; and (5) ten per cent to the Eastern Connecticut
13 Workforce Investment Board] to the regional workforce development
14 boards by the Labor Commissioner based on a formula established by
15 the Labor Commissioner, in collaboration with the regional workforce
16 development boards, that utilizes available data, including, but not
17 limited to, (1) the number of students in each workforce development
18 region that are eligible for free or reduced-price lunch, (2) the number
