AN ACT REQUIRING HOME HEALTH AIDE AGENCIES TO PROVIDE
CERTAIN PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT TO HOME HEALTH
AIDES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "home health aide agency" has the same meaning as provided in
3 section 19a-490 of the general statutes, (2) "home health aide employee"
4 means a person (A) who is employed as a home health aide by a home
5 health aide agency, or (B) with whom such agency has contracted to
6 provide home health aide services on behalf of such agency, (3) "home
7 health aide services" has the same meaning as provided in section 19a-
8 490 of the general statutes, and (4) "personal protective equipment"
9 includes, but is not limited to, disposable gloves, hand sanitizers,
10 aprons, gowns, foot covers, face shields, N95 masks or higher rated
11 masks certified by the National Institute for Occupational Safety and
12 Health and surgical masks.
13 (b) Each home health aide agency shall provide each home health
14 aide employee, at no cost, personal protective equipment that is
