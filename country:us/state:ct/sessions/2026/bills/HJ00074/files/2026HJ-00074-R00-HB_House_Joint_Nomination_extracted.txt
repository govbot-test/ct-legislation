General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 74
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3339
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
ESQUIRE, OF GLASTONBURY TO BE A JUDGE OF THE SUPERIOR
COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Phillip
2 Miller, Esquire, of Glastonbury to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to
3 serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both
4 Houses, in succession to the Honorable Robert A. D'Andrea, is
5 approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior
6 Court for said term.
LCO No. 3339 1 of 1
