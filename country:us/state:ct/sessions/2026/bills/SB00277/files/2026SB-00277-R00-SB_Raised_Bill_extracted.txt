21 Sec. 2. Section 29-292a of the general statutes is amended by adding
22 subsection (l) as follows (Effective October 1, 2026):
23 (NEW) (l) The State Fire Marshal may issue official interpretations of

28 Sec. 3. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) On or before January 1, 2027,
29 and annually thereafter, the Department of Public Health shall report to
30 the Office of the State Fire Marshal regarding (1) all burn injuries and
31 injuries resulting from fireworks or explosives, (2) any death resulting
32 from burn injuries, smoke inhalation or injuries resulting from
33 fireworks or explosives, and (3) any death to which burn injuries, smoke
34 inhalation or injuries resulting from fireworks or explosives
35 contributed. In providing such report, the department shall utilize death
36 certificate information in accordance with section 7-51a of the general
37 statutes and hospital discharge data provided to the department in
38 accordance with section 19a-2a of the general statutes. The office shall
39 compile the information contained in the report, publish a statistical
40 abstract and annually submit such abstract to each local fire marshal and
41 the General Assembly in accordance with the provisions of section 11-
42 4a of the general statutes.
