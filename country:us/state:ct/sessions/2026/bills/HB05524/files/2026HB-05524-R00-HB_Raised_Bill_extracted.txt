Statement of Purpose:
To expand the number of entities required to source-separate organic
materials from the solid waste stream, prohibit the use of polystyrene
containers by restaurants, prohibit the provision of single-use food
service items unless requested by a consumer, prohibit the distribution
of single-use straws that contain PFAS at restaurants, require certain
entities to prioritize surplus food donation over other means of disposal
and establish eligible costs for material management grants issued by
the state.
