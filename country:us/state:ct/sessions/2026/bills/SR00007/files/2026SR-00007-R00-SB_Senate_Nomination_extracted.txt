General Assembly Senate Resolution No. 7
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2209
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF JOSEPH D.
SECURITY REVIEW BOARD.
Resolved by the Senate:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Joseph D.
2 Marino of Westbrook, to be a member of the Psychiatric Security Review
3 Board, as a member of the general public, in succession to Wakana
4 Hirota, to serve a term ending June 30, 2029, or until a successor is
5 appointed and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved
6 and confirmed.
LCO No. 2209 1 of 1
