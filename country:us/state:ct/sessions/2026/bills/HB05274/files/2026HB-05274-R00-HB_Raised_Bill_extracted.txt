General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5274
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1073
Referred to Committee on LABOR AND PUBLIC EMPLOYEES
Introduced by:
(LAB)
AN ACT CONCERNING THE WORKERS' COMPENSATION SYSTEM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Workers' Compensation
2 Commission shall conduct a study of the workers' compensation system
3 in the state. Not later than January 1, 2027, the commission shall report
4 the results of the study, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-
5 4a of the general statutes, to the joint standing committee of the General
6 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor and public
7 employees. Such report may include the commission's
8 recommendations for legislation regarding the workers' compensation
9 system in the state.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Workers' Compensation Commission to study the
workers' compensation system in the state.
