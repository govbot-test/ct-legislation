Statement of Purpose:
To (1) require the local fire marshal to furnish certain reports to the
Connecticut Siting Council for any fires or explosions that occur at a
solar photovoltaic facility, (2) require said council to determine whether
any solar photovoltaic facility equipment was a contributing factor to
any such fires or explosions and, if so, to initiate an amendment
proceeding to require the owner to adopt a solar photovoltaic facility
fire mitigation plan, (3) require the council to determine if there are any
similarly situated facilities, and (4) authorize the council to initiate
amendment proceedings to require the owners of similarly situated
facilities to adopt the same solar photovoltaic facility fire mitigation
plan.
