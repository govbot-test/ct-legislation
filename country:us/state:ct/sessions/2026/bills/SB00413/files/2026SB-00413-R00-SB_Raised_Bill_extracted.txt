Statement of Purpose:
To revise various motor vehicle statutes regarding removable
windshield placards, firefighter driving reciprocity, motor vehicle
registration upon the death of the owner of the vehicle, the definition of
electric scooter, car rental classifications, electric issuance licensees, the
local bridge program and electric bicycles.
