General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5190
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 996
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. WILSON, 46th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE VETERANS
RALLY POINT IN THE CITY OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three hundred fifty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be
5 used by the Department of Veterans Affairs for the purpose of providing
6 a grant-in-aid to the Veterans Rally Point in the city of Norwich for
7 capital improvements.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for capital improvements to the Veterans Rally
Point in the city of Norwich.
