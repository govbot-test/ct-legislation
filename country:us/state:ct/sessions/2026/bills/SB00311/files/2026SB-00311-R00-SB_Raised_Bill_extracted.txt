Statement of Purpose:
To require P20 WIN to share data with a nonprofit organization that
provides data and data support to other public entities, lower the
maximum number of days for an in-school suspension during one
school year, allow out-of-school suspension for violent behavior, acts of
sexual misconduct or possession of a weapon or controlled substance
and require the Department of Education to establish a Disconnected
Youth Investment Grant program.
