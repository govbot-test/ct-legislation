Statement of Purpose:
To (1) provide that a consumer has a property right in, and retains the
right to exercise exclusive control over, (A) any biological sample that is
derived from the consumer's body and provided to, or used by, a direct-
to-consumer genetic testing company, and (B) the results of any genetic
testing conducted on the consumer's DNA by a direct-to-consumer
genetic testing company, and (2) specify various duties and limitations
applicable to direct-to-consumer genetic testing companies.
