Statement of Purpose:
To provide that (1) the Department of Revenue Services shall not issue
a cigarette dealer's license, or renew any such license, for certain
applicants who exceed certain limitations concerning annual gross sales
and the use of retail sales area, and (2) the Department of Consumer
Protection shall not issue an electronic nicotine delivery system
certificate of dealer registration, or renew any such registration, for
certain applicants who (A) exceed certain limitations concerning annual
gross revenue and the use of retail sales area, or (B) are located in a town

that already includes one dealer for every two thousand five hundred
residents.
