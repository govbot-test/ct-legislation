General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5060
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 510
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. ACKERT, 8th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING THE APPRENTICESHIP TRAINING TAX
CREDIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That subsection (c) of section 12-217g of the general statutes be
2 amended to increase the maximum amount of the corporation business
3 tax credit for wages paid to apprentices in the construction trades from
4 four thousand dollars to seven thousand five hundred dollars per
5 apprentice.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase the maximum amount of the corporation business tax credit
for wages paid to apprentices in the construction trades from four
thousand dollars to seven thousand five hundred dollars per
apprentice.
