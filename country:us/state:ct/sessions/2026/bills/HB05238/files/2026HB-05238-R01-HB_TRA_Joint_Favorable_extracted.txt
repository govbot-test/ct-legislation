General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5238
February Session, 2026
AN ACT STUDYING ACCESS TO PARKING FOR HOME HEALTH
CARE SERVICE PROVIDERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) The Commissioner of Emergency
2 Services and Public Protection shall establish a working group to study
3 and make recommendations regarding parking access challenges faced
4 by home health care service providers while delivering services in
5 residential settings. Such study shall include, but need not be limited to,
6 (1) an assessment of parking restrictions, time limits, permit
7 requirements and enforcement practices affecting home health care
8 service providers, (2) an analysis of geographic areas in the state where
9 parking limitations most significantly impact the delivery of home
10 health care services, and (3) a review of parking accommodation
11 programs in other jurisdictions, including temporary permits and
12 designated home health care service parking and enforcement
13 exemptions.
14 (b) The Commissioner of Emergency Services and Public Protection,
15 or the commissioner's designee, shall be chairperson of the working
16 group and shall schedule the first meeting of the working group not
17 later than September 1, 2026. The commissioner shall appoint to such
18 working group representatives from parking authorities or parking
19 divisions, providers of home health care services, sworn members of
20 municipal police departments and such other members as deemed
