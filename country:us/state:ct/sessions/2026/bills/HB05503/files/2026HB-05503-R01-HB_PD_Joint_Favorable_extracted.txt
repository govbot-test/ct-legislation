AN ACT REQUIRING MUNICIPALITIES TO REPORT EXCESS
WASTEWATER CAPACITY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 7-246 of the general statutes is amended by adding
2 subsection (d) as follows (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (NEW) (d) Not later than January 1, 2027, and annually thereafter, the
4 water pollution control authority for a municipality or, for a
5 municipality that has not established a water pollution control
6 authority, the board of selectmen, shall submit a report to the Secretary
7 of the Office of Policy and Management, in a form and manner
8 prescribed by the secretary, detailing the current wastewater capacity in
9 such municipality at the time such report is filed, and any expected
10 projected excess in such wastewater capacity for the year following such
11 report. If a water pollution control authority or a board of selectmen, as
12 applicable, fails to submit an annual report as required by this section,
13 the secretary may withhold any state funding allocated to such
14 authority or municipality for wastewater infrastructure improvements
