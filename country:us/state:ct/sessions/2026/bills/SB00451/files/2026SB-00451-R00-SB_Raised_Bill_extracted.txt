AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN ADVISORY COUNCIL ON CHIMERIC
ANTIGEN RECEPTOR T-CELL THERAPY AND OTHER GENE
THERAPIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) There is established an
2 advisory council on chimeric antigen receptor T-cell therapy and other
3 gene therapies. The council shall advise and make recommendations to
4 the Department of Public Health and other state agencies, as
5 appropriate, regarding (1) the availability of chimeric antigen receptor
6 T-cell therapy and other gene therapies in the state for the treatment of
7 cancer, (2) safe, equitable and financially sustainable delivery of such
8 therapies, (3) advanced training for clinical providers of such therapies,
9 (4) long-term follow-up and vector safety for patients receiving such
10 therapies, (5) the development of referral and management protocols for
11 such therapies, (6) education for clinicians, patients and patient's
12 relatives and caregivers regarding such therapies and such protocols, (7)
13 advising patients and their relatives and caregivers regarding the cost
14 and availability of insurance coverage for such therapies, (8)
15 opportunities for coordinating with research collaborations,
