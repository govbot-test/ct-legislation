AN ACT CONCERNING USED MOTOR VEHICLE WARRANTIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsections (a) to (c), inclusive, of section 42-221 of the 2026
2 supplement to the general statutes are repealed and the following is
3 substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
4 (a) A dealer selling a used motor vehicle [which has a cash purchase
5 price of three thousand dollars or more] that is less than ten years of age
6 shall not exclude, modify, disclaim or limit implied warranties on the
7 motor vehicle.
8 (b) Each contract entered into by a dealer for the sale to a consumer
9 of a used motor vehicle [which has a cash purchase price of three
10 thousand dollars or more but less than five thousand dollars,] that is less
11 than ten years of age shall include an express warranty, covering the full
12 cost of both parts and labor, that the vehicle is mechanically operational
13 and sound and will remain so for at least [thirty] sixty days or [one
14 thousand five hundred] three thousand miles of operation, whichever
15 period ends first, in the absence of damage resulting from an automobile
16 accident or from misuse of the vehicle by the consumer. [Each contract
