RESOLUTION CONCERNING THE PRINTING OF THE SENATE
JOURNAL.
Resolved by the Senate:
1 That the Clerk of the Senate is authorized and directed to cause to be
2 printed and distributed daily such number of copies of the Journal of
3 the Senate, as the clerk deems necessary, and such number of copies at
4 the close of the session as may be provided by law.
