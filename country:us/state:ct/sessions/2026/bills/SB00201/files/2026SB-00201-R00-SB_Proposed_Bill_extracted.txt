General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 201
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 35
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO ESTABLISH A
WORLD WAR II MUSEUM AND EDUCATION CENTER IN THE TOWN
OF EAST HARTFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 one million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of East Hartford, or a nonprofit
7 organization designated by said town, to establish a World War II
8 museum and education center in said town.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to establish a Word War II museum and education
center in the town of East Hartford to memorialize the contributions and
sacrifices of the veterans of World War II.
