AN ACT CONCERNING MUNICIPAL PARTICIPATION IN THE
CONNECTICUT LAND MOBILE RADIO NETWORK AND RELATED
COSTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) The Department of
2 Emergency Services and Public Protection shall pay all lifecycle costs
3 that a municipality incurs as a condition of participating in the
4 Connecticut Land Mobile Radio Network. For purposes of this section,
5 (1) "Connecticut Land Mobile Radio Network" means the state-wide
6 public safety telecommunications system that (A) is administered by the
7 Division of State-Wide Emergency Telecommunications in accordance
8 with chapter 518a of the general statutes, and (B) provides interoperable
9 radio communications for public safety agencies, and (2) "lifecycle costs"
10 means expenses incurred to ensure the software and hardware
11 necessary for participating in the Connecticut Land Mobile Radio
12 Network remain functional and compatible with said network.
13 Sec. 2. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) The Commissioner of
14 Emergency Services and Public Protection shall develop a plan to

Statement of Purpose:
To require (1) the Department of Emergency Services and Public
Protection to pay all lifecycle costs that municipalities incur as a
condition of participating in the Connecticut Land Mobile Radio
Network, and (2) the Commissioner of Emergency Services and Public
Protection to develop a plan to encourage municipalities to participate
in said network.
