Statement of Purpose:
To revise various transportation-related statutes concerning (1) the type
of vehicles eligible for a rebate or voucher under the Connecticut
Hydrogen and Electric Automobile Purchase Rebate program, (2) the
matching grant program for modernizing existing traffic signal
equipment and operations, (3) the requirements for zero-emission
school buses and alternative fuel school buses and the related grant
program for the purchasing or leasing of zero-emission school buses, (4)
a working group to study incentives to increase the use of freight rail

to transport municipal solid waste by freight rail.
