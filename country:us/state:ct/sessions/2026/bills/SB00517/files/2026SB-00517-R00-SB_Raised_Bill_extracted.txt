AN ACT CONVEYING PARCELS OF STATE LAND TO THE TOWN OF
BRANFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) (1) Notwithstanding any
2 provision of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Transportation
3 shall convey to the town of Branford two parcels of land located in the
4 town of Branford, at a cost equal to the fair market value of the property,
5 as determined by the average of the appraisals of two independent
6 appraisers selected by the commissioner, plus the administrative costs
7 of making such conveyance.
8 (2) (A) The first parcel of land has an area of approximately 7.2 acres
9 and is identified as Lot 1 on a map entitled "Overall Proposed Roadway
10 Improvement Plan and Master Redevelopment Plan (Commercial
11 Pkwy/Exit 53) – Route 1 at SR. 794 (Branford Connector) Commercial
12 Parkway, and Route 146; Branford, Connecticut – Designed J.O.M,
13 Drawn J.M.B, Checked ___, Approved ___, Scale 1"=200', Project No.
14 2500951, Date 03/06/2026, CAD File PB2500951A".
15 (B) The second parcel of land has an area of approximately 11.66 acres

16 and is identified as Lot 5 on the map specified in subparagraph (A) of
17 this subdivision.
18 (3) The conveyance of the parcels described in subdivision (2) of this
19 subsection shall be subject to the approval of the State Properties Review
20 Board.
21 (b) The State Properties Review Board shall complete its review of the
22 conveyance of said parcels of land not later than thirty days after it
