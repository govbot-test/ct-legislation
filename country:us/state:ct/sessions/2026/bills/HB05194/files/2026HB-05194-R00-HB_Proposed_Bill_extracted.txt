General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5194
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1022
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. BUMGARDNER, 41st Dist.
SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE NEW
ENGLAND SCIENCE AND SAILING FOUNDATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three hundred thousand dollars, in the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027,
5 the proceeds of which shall be used by the Department of Education for
6 the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the New England Science and
7 Sailing Foundation to ensure its facilities are compliant with physical
8 accessibility guidelines under the federal Americans with Disabilities
9 Act.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to the New England Science and Sailing Foundation
to ensure its facilities are compliant with physical accessibility
guidelines under the federal Americans with Disabilities Act.
