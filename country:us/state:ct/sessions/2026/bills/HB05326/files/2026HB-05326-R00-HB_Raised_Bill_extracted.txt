February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1869
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
(ED)
AN ACT INCREASING THE RATES FOR THE BIRTH-TO-THREE
PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (d) of section 17a-248e of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (d) The lead agency may provide early intervention services, arrange
5 for the delivery of early intervention services by participating agencies
6 or contract with providers to deliver early intervention services to
7 eligible children and the families of such children. The lead agency in
8 providing, arranging or contracting for early intervention services shall
9 monitor all birth-to-three service providers for quality and
10 accountability in accordance with Section 616 of the Individuals with
11 Disabilities Education Act, 20 USC 1416 and establish state-wide rates
12 for such services that are in accordance with the rates described in phase
13 two of the rate study conducted pursuant to section 1 of public act 23-
14 186.
