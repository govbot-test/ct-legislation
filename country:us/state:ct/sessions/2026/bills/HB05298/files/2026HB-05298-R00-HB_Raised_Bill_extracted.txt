AN ACT ESTABLISHING A GRANT PROGRAM FOR VETERANS-
RELATED CAPITAL EXPENDITURE PROJECTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) For the fiscal year
2 ending June 30, 2028, and each fiscal year thereafter, the Department of
3 Veterans Affairs shall administer a program to provide grants to
4 municipalities and nonprofit veterans' organizations for capital
5 expenditure projects, including, but not limited to, repair or
6 replacement of mechanical systems at the homes of elderly, disabled or
7 means-limited veterans and placement or maintenance of war or
8 veterans' memorials or monuments.
9 (b) Not later than January 1, 2027, the department shall develop
10 eligibility criteria to be used in selecting among applicants for such
11 grants, develop application forms and deadlines and post in a
12 conspicuous location on the department's Internet web site a description
13 of the grant program that includes, but is not limited to, such criteria,
14 forms and deadlines.
