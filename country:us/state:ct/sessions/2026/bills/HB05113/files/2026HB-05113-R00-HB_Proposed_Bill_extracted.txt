General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5113
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 591
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. ROJAS, 9th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING FROM THE PERSONAL INCOME TAX THE
AMOUNT OF STATE AND FEDERAL DEBT SETTLEMENTS AND
DEBT RELIEF RECEIVED BY A TAXPAYER FOR STUDENT LOANS,
MEDICAL DEBT AND CREDIT CARD DEBT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 229 of the general statutes be amended to exempt from
2 the personal income tax the amount of any state and federal debt
3 settlements and debt relief received by a taxpayer for student loans,
4 medical debt and credit card debt.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt from the personal income tax the amount of any state and
federal debt settlements and debt relief received by a taxpayer for
student loans, medical debt and credit card debt.
