General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 210
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 30
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING MEDICAID RATES FOR PEDIATRIC CARE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That title 17b of the general statutes be amended to require the
2 Commissioner of Social Services to (1) amend the Medicaid state plan to
3 increase reimbursement rates for pediatric care services by not less than
4 five per cent higher than such rates in effect on June 30, 2026, and (2) file
5 a report on the impact of such rate increases on provider participation
6 in the Medicaid program, access to care and the state budget.
Statement of Purpose:
To increase Medicaid reimbursement rates for pediatric care services.
