AN ACT CONCERNING THE PROVISION OF NOTICE TO A PARENT
OR GUARDIAN OF A MINOR CHILD WHO HAS RECEIVED
PREGNANCY-RELATED HEALTH CARE SERVICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 19a-601 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (a) Prior to the performance of an abortion upon a minor, a physician
4 or counselor shall provide pregnancy information and counseling in
5 accordance with this section in a manner and language that will be

12 a decision not to have an abortion at any time within the time period
13 during which an abortion may legally be performed;

80 Sec. 2. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) In addition to the provision
81 of information and counseling required under section 19a-601 of the
82 general statutes, as amended by this act, after performing an abortion,
83 the health care provider authorized to perform such abortion under
84 section 19a-602 of the general statutes, or a counselor as defined in
85 section 19a-600 of the general statutes, shall explain and carry out the
86 following notification procedures to parents, guardians or other
87 individuals:
88 (1) Except as provided in subdivision (2) of this subsection, notice
89 shall be provided to at least one parent or guardian of a pregnant
90 unemancipated minor not less than forty-eight hours after the
91 performance of an abortion upon such minor;
92 (2) If a pregnant unemancipated minor declares in a signed written
93 statement that such minor is a victim of sexual abuse, neglect or physical
94 abuse by either a parent or guardian, the notice required pursuant to
95 subdivision (1) of this subsection shall be provided to such minor's
96 sibling who is aged twenty-one years or older or a stepparent or
97 grandparent specified by such minor or, if no such person exists, to
98 some other person who is aged twenty-one years or older specified by
99 such minor; and
100 (3) If the health care provider authorized to perform the abortion

103 statement pursuant to subdivision (2) of this subsection, such provider
