AN ACT AUTHORIZING MUNICIPALITIES TO PROHIBIT THE SALE OF
DOGS, CATS AND RABBITS IN PET SHOPS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subparagraph (D) of subdivision (7) of subsection (c) of
2 section 7-148 of the general statutes is repealed and the following is
3 substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
4 (D) (i) Regulate and prohibit the going at large of dogs and other
5 animals in the streets and public places of the municipality and prevent
6 cruelty to animals and all inhuman sports, except that no municipality
7 shall adopt breed-specific dog ordinances;
8 (ii) Regulate and prohibit the keeping of wild or domestic animals,
9 including reptiles, within the municipal limits or portions thereof;
10 (iii) Notwithstanding the provisions of chapter 435, prohibit the sale
11 of dogs, cats and rabbits in pet shops, as defined in section 22-327;
