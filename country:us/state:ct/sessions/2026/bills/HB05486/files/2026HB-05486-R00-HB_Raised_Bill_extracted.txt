AN ACT CONCERNING A DIAPER PARTNERSHIP GRANT
PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section, (1) "eligible
2 children" means children three years of age or younger from households
3 with family income not exceeding two hundred per cent of the federal
4 poverty level, and (2) "diaper partnership" means a partnership between
5 an organization representing hospitals in the state and a nonprofit
6 organization that distributes diapers to needy families statewide. The
7 Commissioner of Social Services shall establish a grant program for the
8 fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, to fund the provision of diapers at no
9 cost by a diaper partnership to eligible children.
10 (b) The commissioner shall prescribe reporting requirements for any
11 diaper partnership that receives funds under the grant program. Not
12 later than September 1, 2027, the Commissioner of Social Services shall
13 submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the
14 general statutes, to the joint standing committees of the General
15 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to appropriations and
