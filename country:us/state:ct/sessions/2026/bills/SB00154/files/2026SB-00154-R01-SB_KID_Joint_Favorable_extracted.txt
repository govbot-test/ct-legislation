General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 154
February Session, 2026
AN ACT REQUIRING CERTAIN AGENCIES TO REPORT COSTS
ASSOCIATED WITH PROGRAMS SERVING CHILDREN AND
FAMILIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) Not later than January 1, 2027, the
2 Department of Social Services shall submit a report, in accordance with
3 the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, to the joint
4 standing committees of the General Assembly having cognizance of
5 matters relating to children, human services and appropriations. Such
6 report shall (1) describe each program administered by the department
7 that serves children or families, where eligibility for participation in
8 such program or the sliding fee for participation in such program is
9 based on a comparison between annual family income and the federal
10 poverty level, (2) estimate the total cost associated with the
11 administration of each such program, (3) estimate the anticipated total
12 cost associated with the administration of each such program if
13 eligibility for participation was expanded to include children and
14 families with an annual family income above the federal poverty level
15 and below the ALICE Threshold of Financial Survival for the state, as
16 determined by the United Way of Connecticut, (4) estimate how many
17 additional children or families would be eligible for participation in
18 each such program if participation was expanded in the manner
19 described in subdivision (3) of this section, and (5) discuss the feasibility
