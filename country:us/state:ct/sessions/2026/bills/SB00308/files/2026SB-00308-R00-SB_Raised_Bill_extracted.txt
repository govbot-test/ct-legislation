February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1674
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
(ED)
AN ACT REQUIRING BOARDS OF EDUCATION TO POST
CURRICULUM ONLINE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (e) of section 10-220 of the 2026 supplement to
2 the general statutes is repealed and the following is substituted in lieu
3 thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
4 (e) Each local and regional board of education shall establish a school
5 district curriculum committee. The committee shall recommend,
6 develop, review and approve all curriculum for the local or regional
7 school district. Each local and regional board of education shall (1) make
8 available all curriculum approved by the committee and all associated
9 curriculum materials in accordance with the requirements of the
10 Protection of Pupil Rights Amendment, 20 USC 1232h, and (2) for the
11 school year commencing July 1, 2026, and each school year thereafter,
12 post [objectives and scope and sequence of] all approved curriculum,
13 including the objectives and scope and sequence of such approved
14 curriculum, on the Internet web site of such board.
