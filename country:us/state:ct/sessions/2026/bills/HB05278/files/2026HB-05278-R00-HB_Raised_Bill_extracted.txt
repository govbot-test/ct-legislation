General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5278
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1289
Referred to Committee on LABOR AND PUBLIC EMPLOYEES
Introduced by:
(LAB)
AN ACT REQUIRING UNINSURED AND UNDERINSURED MOTORIST
COVERAGE FOR DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION
EMPLOYEES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) Not later than July 1, 2027,
2 the state shall provide uninsured and underinsured motorist coverage
3 under the State Fleet Insurance Policy, with limits for bodily injury or
4 death not less than one million dollars, to employees of the Department
5 of Transportation for the protection of such employees who are legally
6 entitled to recover damages from owners or operators of uninsured
7 motor vehicles and underinsured motor vehicles because of a bodily
8 injury, including death resulting therefrom, arising out of or in the
9 course of such employee's employment when such employee is required
10 to be outside of a motor vehicle.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
LCO No. 1289 1 of 2

Raised Bill No. 5278
Statement of Purpose:
To require the state to provide uninsured and underinsured motorist
coverage to employees of the Department of Transportation.
