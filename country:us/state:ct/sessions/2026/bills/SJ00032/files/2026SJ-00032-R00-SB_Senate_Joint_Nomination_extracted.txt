LAURA F. BALDINI OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE A JUDGE OF THE

1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Laura F. Baldini of West Hartford to be a Judge of the
3 Superior Court, to serve for a term of eight years from May 5, 2026, is
4 approved and confirmed, and she is appointed a Judge of the Superior
5 Court for said term.
