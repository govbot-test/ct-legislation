AN ACT CONCERNING QUALIFIED VETERANS' CHARITABLE

Assembly convened:

4 (b) On and after July 1, 2013, the Commissioner of Veterans Affairs,
5 or the commissioner's designee, shall, within available appropriations,
6 publish a list of qualified veterans' charitable organizations. The

This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 27-100f(b)
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Commissioner of Veterans Affairs to annually solicit
organizations for inclusion on the list of qualified veterans' charitable
organizations published on the Internet web site of the Department of
Veterans Affairs.
