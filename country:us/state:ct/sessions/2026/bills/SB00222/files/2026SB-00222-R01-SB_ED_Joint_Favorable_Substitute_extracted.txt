General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 222
February Session, 2026
AN ACT PERMITTING SCHOOL DISTRICTS TO OFFER AN
EXEMPTION FROM THE PHYSICAL EDUCATION CREDIT
REQUIREMENT TO HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS WHO PARTICIPATE
IN INTERSCHOLASTIC ATHLETICS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (f) of section 10-221a of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (f) (1) Any student who presents a certificate from a physician,
5 physician assistant or advanced practice registered nurse stating that, in
6 the opinion of the physician, physician assistant or advanced practice
7 registered nurse, participation in physical education is medically
8 contraindicated because of the physical condition of such student, shall
9 be excused from the physical education requirement, provided the
10 credit for physical education may be fulfilled by an elective.
11 (2) Any student who participates in interscholastic athletics may be
12 exempt from the physical education and wellness requirement under
13 subdivision (3) of subsection (d) of this section, provided such
14 exemption is permitted pursuant to a policy adopted by the local or
15 regional board of education of the school district in which such student
16 is enrolled. Any student who receives such exemption shall still be
