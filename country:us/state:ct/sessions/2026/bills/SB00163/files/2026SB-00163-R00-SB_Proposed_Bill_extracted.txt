General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 163
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 941
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR
IMPROVEMENTS TO DOUBLEDAY ROAD IN THE TOWN OF
COLUMBIA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate ten
4 million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the Department
5 of Transportation for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the
6 town of Columbia for improvements to Doubleday Road, including, but
7 not limited to, drainage, the bridge and roadbed.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for improvements to Doubleday Road in the town
of Columbia.
