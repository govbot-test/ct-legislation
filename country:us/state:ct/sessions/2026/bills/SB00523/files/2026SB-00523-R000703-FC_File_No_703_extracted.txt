Asset
Explanation
The bill conveys a specified parcel of land, totaling 9.45 acres, from
the Department of Transportation to the town of North Canaan. The
state would lose use of the asset. The town of North Canaan would gain
use of an asset and would pay the administrative costs of the
conveyance, including survey costs if necessary.
Additionally, the bill allows the town to sell or lease a portion of the
parcel, provided the sale or lease of such portion be for fair market value
and the proceeds of which are deposited into the Special Transportation
Fund (STF). This would result in the potential loss of asset for the town
and gain of revenue for the STF.
The asset would revert to state control if the recipient does not use
the land for specified purposes.
The land conveyance is subject to the review and approval of the State
