Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.

RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE
CHRISTINE PERRA RAPILLO OF CHESHIRE TO BE A MEMBER OF
THE JUDICIAL REVIEW COUNCIL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Christine Perra Rapillo of Cheshire to be a member of the
3 Judicial Review Council, as an alternate member who is a judge of the
4 Superior Court and not also a judge of the Supreme Court, in succession
5 to the Honorable Karen A. Goodrow, to serve a term ending November
6 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever
7 is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
