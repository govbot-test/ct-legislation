Introduced by:

RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE
TAMMY T. NGUYEN-O'DOWD OF BLOOMFIELD TO BE A MEMBER
OF THE JUDICIAL REVIEW COUNCIL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Tammy T. Nguyen-O'Dowd of Bloomfield to be a member
3 of the Judicial Review Council, as a judge of the Superior Court who is
4 not also a judge of the Supreme Court, in succession to the Honorable
5 Theodore R. Tyma, to serve a term ending November 30, 2028, or until
6 a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever is longer, is
7 hereby approved and confirmed.
