Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 384
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. CICARELLA, 34th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO RESTORE THE
HISTORIC TRAIN STATION IN THE TOWN OF WALLINGFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three million five hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall
5 be used by the Department of Economic and Community Development
6 for the purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the Economic
7 Development Commission in the town of Wallingford to restore and
8 covert the historic train station for use by the community.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the restoration and conversion of the historic
train station in the town of Wallingford for the benefit of the local
economy and community.
