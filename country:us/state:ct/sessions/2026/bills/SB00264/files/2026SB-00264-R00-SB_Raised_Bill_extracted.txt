AN ACT CONCERNING CHILDREN'S WELL-BEING.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Commissioner of Children and
2 Families shall conduct a study to determine whether policy and
3 procedural changes within the Department of Children and Families
4 may improve the well-being of children in the care and custody of the
5 commissioner. The commissioner shall report, in accordance with the
6 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, the findings of such
7 study to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
8 cognizance of matters relating to children on or before January 1, 2027.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
