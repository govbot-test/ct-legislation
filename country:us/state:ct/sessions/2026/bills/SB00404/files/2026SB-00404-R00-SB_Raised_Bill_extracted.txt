AN ACT REQUIRING A STUDY OF STATUTES AND REGULATIONS
RELATING TO FIREWORKS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Commissioner of Emergency
2 Services and Public Protection shall conduct a study of the general
3 statutes and regulations of Connecticut state agencies relating to
4 fireworks to determine whether any revisions to such statutes or
5 regulations are necessary. Not later than January 1, 2027, the
6 commissioner shall submit a report concerning the results of such study
7 and any recommendations, including recommendations for any
8 legislative action, to the joint standing committee of the General
9 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to public safety and
10 security, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the
11 general statutes.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
