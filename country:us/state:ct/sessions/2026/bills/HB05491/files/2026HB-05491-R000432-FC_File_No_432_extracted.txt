DEVELOPMENT INFORMATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) Not later than January 1,
2 2027, the Labor Commissioner shall create an informational web page,
3 as part of the Internet web site of the Labor Department, to serve as a
4 central repository of information, resources and materials, including
5 links to external sources of such information, resources and materials
6 regarding (1) job training, career counseling and workforce
7 development organizations and opportunities available, (2) regional
8 sector partnerships, and (3) other topics relevant to workforce
9 development. The Labor Commissioner shall, on a quarterly basis,
10 solicit known and reputable providers of information, resources and
11 materials described in this section for items to be included on such
12 informational web page.

OLR Bill Analysis
HB 5491
AN ACT CONCERNING CENTRALIZATION OF WORKFORCE
DEVELOPMENT INFORMATION.
SUMMARY
This bill requires the labor commissioner, by January 1, 2027, to create
a centralized webpage on the Department of Labor’s website with
information, resources, and materials on workforce development
organizations and opportunities. It also requires the labor commissioner
to post links on this webpage to external sources of information,
resources, and materials on:
1. job training, career counseling, workforce development
organizations and opportunities;
2. regional sector partnerships; and
3. other topics related to workforce development.
Lastly, the bill requires the labor commissioner to quarterly contact
reputable providers of related information, resources, and materials for
material to post on the webpage.
EFFECTIVE DATE: October 1, 2026
