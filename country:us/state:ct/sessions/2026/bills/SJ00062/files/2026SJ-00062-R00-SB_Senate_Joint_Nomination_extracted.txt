RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THOMAS WIEHL

UTILITIES REGULATORY AUTHORITY.

1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Thomas
2 Wiehl of Madison to be appointed a utility commissioner of the Public
3 Utilities Regulatory Authority, in succession to Marissa Paslick Gillett,
4 to serve a term ending June 30, 2030, or until a successor is appointed
5 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
6 confirmed.
