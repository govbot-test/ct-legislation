AN ACT CONCERNING THE COMPLEX REHABILITATION
TECHNOLOGY AND WHEELCHAIR REPAIR ADVISORY COUNCIL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 42-339 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):

4 wheelchair repair advisory council to monitor repairs of wheelchairs,
5 including complex rehabilitation technology wheelchairs, as defined in
6 section 42-337, and to make recommendations concerning improving
7 repair times.

13 or repaired under the Medicaid program, and one of whom is a
14 representative of the state advocacy system for persons with disabilities,

66 Sec. 2. Section 42-338 of the general statutes is repealed and the
67 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
68 (a) An authorized wheelchair dealer shall timely repair a wheelchair,
69 including a complex rehabilitation technology wheelchair, sold or
70 leased by such dealer in the state. An authorized wheelchair dealer who
71 sells or leases a complex rehabilitation technology wheelchair in the
72 state shall provide timely repair of such wheelchair at a consumer's

74 (b) An authorized wheelchair dealer shall maintain an electronic mail

78 request for wheelchair repair not later than one business day after the

92 subsection.
