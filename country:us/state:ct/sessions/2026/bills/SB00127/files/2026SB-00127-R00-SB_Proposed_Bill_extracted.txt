General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 127
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1013
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. FLEXER, 29th Dist.
REP. HADDAD, 54th Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING FUNDING FOR HIGHER EDUCATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the following additional sums be appropriated from the General
2 Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027: (1) Ninety-one million
3 dollars to The University of Connecticut; (2) seventy-six million dollars
4 to the Board of Regents for Higher Education; and (3) seventy-two
5 million dollars for Connecticut State Colleges and Universities and
6 Connecticut State Community Colleges.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide more funding for higher education and allow more
Connecticut students to be able to afford college.
