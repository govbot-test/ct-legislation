AN ACT CONCERNING THE HOURLY COMPENSATION FOR
ADJUNCT FIRE INSTRUCTORS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Commencing July 1, 2026, and
2 each fiscal year thereafter, the Commissioner of Administrative Services
3 shall adjust the hourly compensation for adjunct fire instructors
4 employed by the Connecticut Fire Academy at the same percentage rate
5 as any general wage increase and cost-of-living adjustment included
6 within the most recently ratified agreement between the state and the
7 State Employees Bargaining Agent Coalition.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
