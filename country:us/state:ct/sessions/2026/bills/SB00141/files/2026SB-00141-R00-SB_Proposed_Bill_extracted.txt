General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 141
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 902
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO COMPLETE THE
INSTALLATION OF A GENERATOR FOR REGIONAL SCHOOL
DISTRICT NUMBER 8.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 seven hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Education for the purpose of providing a grant-in-
6 aid to Regional School District Number 8 to complete the installation of
7 a generator to create a regional shelter.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for Regional School District Number 8 to complete
the installation of a generator for the creation of a regional shelter.
