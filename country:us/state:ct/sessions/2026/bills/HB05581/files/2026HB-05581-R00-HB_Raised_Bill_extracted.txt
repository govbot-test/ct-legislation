AN ACT AMENDING A PRIOR CONVEYANCE OF A PARCEL OF
STATE LAND TO THE TOWN OF ROCKY HILL TO CHANGE THE
ACREAGE AND IDENTIFICATION OF SAID PARCEL.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Special act 22-16 is amended to read as follows (Effective
2 from passage):
3 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the
4 Commissioner of Transportation shall convey to the town of Rocky Hill
5 a parcel of land located in the town of Rocky Hill, at a cost equal to the
6 fair market value of the property, as determined by the average of the
7 appraisals of two independent appraisers selected by the commissioner,
8 plus the administrative costs of making such conveyance. Said parcel of
9 land has an area of approximately [5.3] 7.5 acres, [and] is identified as
10 the parcel of land located at 1090 Maple Street, and is further identified
11 as ["RELEASE AREA" on a map entitled "Compilation Plan, Town of
12 Rocky Hill, Map Showing Land Released to ____ by the State of
