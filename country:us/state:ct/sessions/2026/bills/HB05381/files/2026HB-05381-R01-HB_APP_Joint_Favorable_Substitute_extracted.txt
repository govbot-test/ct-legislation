General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5381
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING AN ACCOUNT TO COMPENSATE VICTIMS
OF UNLAWFUL FUNERAL SERVICE PRACTICES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) There is established a
2 separate, nonlapsing account to be known as the "funeral service
3 compensation account". The account shall contain (1) any amounts
4 appropriated or otherwise made available by the state for the purposes
5 of this section, (2) any moneys required by law to be deposited in the
6 account, and (3) gifts, grants, donations or bequests made for the
7 purposes of this section. Investment earnings credited to the assets of
8 the account shall become part of the assets of the account. The State
9 Treasurer shall administer the account.
10 (b) All moneys deposited in the account shall be used by the
11 Department of Consumer Protection for the purposes of this section.
12 The Commissioner of Consumer Protection may deduct and retain from
13 the moneys in the account an amount equal to the costs incurred by the
14 department in administering the provisions of this section, except said
15 amount shall not exceed two per cent of the balance in the account in
16 any fiscal year.
17 (c) The Commissioner of Consumer Protection shall use the funeral
18 service compensation account to make grants to eligible persons who
