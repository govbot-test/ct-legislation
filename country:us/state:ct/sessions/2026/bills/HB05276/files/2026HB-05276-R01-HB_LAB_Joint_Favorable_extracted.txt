AN ACT CONCERNING VOLUNTEER FIRE DEPARTMENTS AND THE

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:

2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
3 1, 2026):
4 (d) "Employer" means the state and any political subdivision thereof

8 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
9 1, 2026):

11 employment in the state except the following: (1) Employees of the
12 United States government; [and] (2) working conditions of employees
