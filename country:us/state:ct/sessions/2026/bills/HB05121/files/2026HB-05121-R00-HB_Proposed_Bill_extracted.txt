General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5121
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 748
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. CANDELARIA J., 95th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR BUILDING
FACADES AND SITE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE CITY OF NEW HAVEN.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 five million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the city of New Haven for renovations of
7 building facades and other site improvements in the Hill and Fair Haven
8 neighborhoods of said city.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to the city of New Haven for renovations to building
facades and other site improvements in the Hill and Fair Haven
neighborhoods.
