RESOLUTION ACCEPTING THE RECOMMENDATION OF THE
CLAIMS COMMISSIONER TO MAKE PAYMENT IN EXCESS OF
THIRTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS WITH RESPECT TO THE CLAIM
AGAINST THE STATE OF CLIFFORD BEERS COMMUNITY HEALTH
PARTNERS, LLC O/B/O YOUTH CONTINUUM, LLC.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the recommendation of the Claims Commissioner, file number
2 27412 of said commissioner, that an award of $50,000.00, payable from
3 the appropriation to the adjudicated claims account of the General
4 Fund, be granted to Clifford Beers Community Health Partners, LLC
5 o/b/o Youth Continuum, LLC, on such claim against the state, is
6 accepted and that payment of said amount to said claimant is ordered.

Explanation
The bill confirms the determination of the Claims Commissioner
concerning the claim of Clifford Beers Community Health Partners, LLC
