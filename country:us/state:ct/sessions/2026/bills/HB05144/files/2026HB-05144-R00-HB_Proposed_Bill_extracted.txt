AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR SCHOOL MEALS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of ____ dollars be appropriated to the Department of
2 Education, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year ending June 30,
3 2027, to provide (1) breakfasts to students at no cost at schools
4 participating in the school breakfast program, and (2) lunch to students,
5 who are eligible for reduced-price meals, at no cost at schools
6 participating in the school lunch program.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to reimburse school districts for the difference
between the federal reimbursement rate for reduced-price and fully
paid school meals.
