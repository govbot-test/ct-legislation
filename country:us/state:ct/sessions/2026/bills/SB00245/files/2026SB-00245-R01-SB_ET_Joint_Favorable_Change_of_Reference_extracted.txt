AN ACT ELIMINATING CERTAIN TAX INCENTIVES FOR DATA
CENTERS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (b) of section 32-286 of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (b) Any person that anticipates it will own, operate or be a colocation
5 tenant in a qualified data center in this state may apply to the
6 Commissioner of Economic and Community Development to enter into
7 an agreement in accordance with the provisions of subsection (c) of this
8 section, for exemption from the taxes imposed under chapters 203 and
9 219 as set forth in subsections (d) and (e) of this section, provided no
10 such application may be accepted by the commissioner on or after July
11 1, 2026.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
