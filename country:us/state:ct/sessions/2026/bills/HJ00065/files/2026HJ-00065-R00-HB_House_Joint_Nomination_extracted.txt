General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 65
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3150
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF MICHAEL R.
STATE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION ARBITRATION PANEL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Michael R.
2 Ricci of Guilford, to be reappointed a member of the State Department
3 of Education Arbitration Panel, as a neutral arbitrator, to serve a term
4 ending June 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified,
5 whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
