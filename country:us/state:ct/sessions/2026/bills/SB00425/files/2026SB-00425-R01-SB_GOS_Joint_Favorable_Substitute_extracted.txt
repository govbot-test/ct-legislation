General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 425
February Session, 2026
AN ACT REQUIRING ANNUAL STATE AGENCY PERFORMANCE
PLANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section,
2 "state agency" means any department, board, council, commission,
3 institution or other agency in the executive branch of state government,
4 including, but not limited to, each constituent unit.
5 (b) Not later than July 1, 2027, and annually thereafter, each state
6 agency shall develop and implement an annual performance plan that
7 outlines the state agency's strategic and operational goals. The Office of
8 Policy and Management shall establish guidelines for the performance
9 plans and shall provide training and technical assistance in performance
10 plan development and implementation to state agencies upon request.
11 (c) Not later than January 1, 2028, and annually thereafter, each state
12 agency shall report on the state agency's performance plan and its
13 progress in attaining the strategic and operational goals outlined in the
14 state agency's performance plan from the prior year to the Office of
15 Policy and Management, and shall concomitantly submit a copy of such
16 report to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
17 cognizance of such agency, in accordance with the provisions of section
18 11-4a of the general statutes, except in the case of the Office of State
