AN ACT CONVEYING A PARCEL OF STATE LAND IN THE TOWN OF
EASTON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding any provision
2 of the general statutes, the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental
3 Protection shall convey to The Congregational Church of Easton, Inc., a
4 parcel of land located in the town of Easton, at a cost equal to the
5 administrative costs of making such conveyance. Said parcel of land has
6 an area of approximately 6.85 acres and is identified as Lots 30 and 34
7 in Block 3776B on town of Easton Tax Assessor's Map 1557, and is
8 further described in a deed recorded in the town of Easton Land Records
9 in Volume 0382 at Page 0279. The conveyance shall be subject to the
10 approval of the State Properties Review Board.
11 (b) The Congregational Church of Easton, Inc., shall use said parcel
12 of land for purposes of open space and two small butterfly gardens-
13 pollinator pathways. If the church:
14 (1) Does not use said parcel for either of said purposes;

16 (3) Leases all or any portion of said parcel,
17 the parcel shall revert to the state of Connecticut.

19 conveyance of said parcel of land not later than thirty days after it
20 receives a proposed agreement from the Department of Energy and

24 deliver any deed or instrument necessary for a conveyance under this
25 section, which deed or instrument shall include provisions to carry out
26 the purposes of subsection (b) of this section. The Commissioner of
27 Energy and Environmental Protection shall have the sole responsibility
28 for all other incidents of such conveyance.

sections:
