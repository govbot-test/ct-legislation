General Assembly File No. 648
February Session, 2026
Senate, April 15, 2026
The Committee on Appropriations reported through SEN.
OSTEN of the 19th Dist., Chairperson of the Committee on the
part of the Senate, that the substitute bill ought to pass.
AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF MICROTRANSIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Transportation shall conduct a study relating to the expansion of
3 microtransit. For purposes of this section, "microtransit" means a
4 technology-enabled transportation service that uses multipassenger
5 vehicles to provide on-demand services with dynamically generated
6 routing. Such study shall include, but need not be limited to: (1) An
7 analysis of designated service areas appropriate for microtransit; (2) the
8 estimated utilization of microtransit in identified service areas; (3) a
9 timeline for implementation of a microtransit program; and (4) the
10 potential costs of and sources of funding for such program. Not later
11 than January 1, 2027, the commissioner shall submit a report of the
12 results of such study to the joint standing committees of the General
13 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to transportation and
14 appropriations and the budgets of state agencies, in accordance with the
15 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes.

State Impact: None
Municipal Impact: None
Explanation
The bill, which does not have a fiscal impact, requires the Department
of Transportation to conduct a study relating to the expansion of
microtransit by January 1, 2027. It is expected that the agency has the
necessary expertise to conduct the study.
