AN ACT DISSOLVING THE BROOKLYN FIRE DISTRICT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective October 1, 2026) The Brooklyn Fire District,
2 established pursuant to number 41 of the special acts of 1899, is hereby
3 dissolved. The funds and tangible property held by said fire district
4 shall be transferred to the town of Brooklyn for the benefit of said town.
5 Sec. 2. Number 41 of the special acts of 1899 is repealed. (Effective
6 October 1, 2026)
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 Repealer section

Explanation
The bill dissolves the Brooklyn Fire District which results in a
potential cost and potential revenue gain to Brooklyn beginning in FY
27 to the extent the town must (1) cover services that were previously
provided by the district, and (2) assume any assets, funds, and property
associated with the fire district.
The Out Years
The annualized ongoing fiscal impact identified above would
continue into the future subject to inflation.
