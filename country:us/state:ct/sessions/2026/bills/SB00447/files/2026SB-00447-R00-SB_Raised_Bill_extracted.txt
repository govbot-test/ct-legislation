AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HOMESTEAD PROPERTY TAX
EXEMPTION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) Any municipality may, upon
2 approval of its legislative body or, in a municipality where the
3 legislative body is a town meeting, by vote of the board of selectmen,
4 provide an exemption from property tax applicable to the assessed
5 value of owner-occupied single-family dwellings that are the primary
6 residences of such owners, up to the amount of fifty thousand dollars.
7 Such municipality may require a term of residency for owners to be
8 eligible for an exemption under this section.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To establish a property tax exemption of fifty thousand dollars of the
assessed value of owner-occupied single-family dwellings.
