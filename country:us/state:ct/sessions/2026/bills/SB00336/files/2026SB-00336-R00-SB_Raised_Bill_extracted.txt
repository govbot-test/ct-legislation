AN ACT CONCERNING HOUSING.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:

2 conduct a study on the housing policies of the state. Such study shall
3 examine each housing program, the policy it was designed to address,
4 and the effectiveness of such program. Not later than January 1, 2027,
5 the Commissioner of Housing shall report, in accordance with the
6 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, to the joint standing
7 committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of matters
8 relating to housing.
