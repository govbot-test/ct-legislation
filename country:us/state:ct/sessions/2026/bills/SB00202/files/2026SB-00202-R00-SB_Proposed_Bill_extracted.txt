General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 202
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 40
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. ANWAR, 3rd Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO COMMEMORATE
THE SERVICE AND SACRIFICE OF PHILIPPINE-AMERICAN WAR
VETERANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 three hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by
5 the Department of Veterans Affairs for the purpose of commemorating
6 the service and sacrifice of veterans of the Philippine-American War as
7 part of the Connecticut State Veterans Memorial in Minuteman Park in
8 the city of Hartford.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to commemorate the service and sacrifice of
Philippine-American War veterans as part of the Connecticut State
Veterans Memorial.
