1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Theodore
2 M. Doolittle, Esquire, of West Hartford to be a Judge of the Superior
3 Court, to serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation
4 by both Houses, in succession to the Honorable Mary-Margaret D.
5 Burgdorff, is approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of
6 the Superior Court for said term.
