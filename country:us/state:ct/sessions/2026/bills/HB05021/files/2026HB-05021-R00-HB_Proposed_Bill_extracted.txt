General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5021
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 63
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. BUCHSBAUM, 69th Dist.
AN ACT EXEMPTING CERTAIN ARTICLES OF CHILDREN'S
CLOTHING FROM THE SALES AND USE TAXES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That chapter 219 of the general statutes be amended to exempt from
2 the sales and use taxes the sale of and the storage, use or other
3 consumption in this state of articles of children's clothing costing under
4 one hundred dollars.
Statement of Purpose:
To exempt articles of children's clothing costing under one hundred
dollars from the sales and use taxes.
