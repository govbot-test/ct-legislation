General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5545
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3121
Referred to Committee on GOVERNMENT ADMINISTRATION
AND ELECTIONS
Introduced by:
(GAE)
AN ACT MAKING A TECHNICAL CHANGE CONCERNING A
CAMPAIGN CONTRIBUTION LIMIT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (d) of section 9-611 of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October
3 1, 2026):
4 (d) No individual shall make a contribution to any candidate or
5 committee, other than a contribution in kind, in excess of one hundred
6 dollars except by personal check or credit card of [that] such individual.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 9-611(d)
Statement of Purpose:
To make a technical change to a statute concerning a campaign
contribution limit.
