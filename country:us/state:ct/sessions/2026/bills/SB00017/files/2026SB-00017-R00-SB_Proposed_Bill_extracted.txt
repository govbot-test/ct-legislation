General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 17
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 300
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. KISSEL, 7th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE REMOVAL OF PFAS
FROM FIRE APPARATUS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of one million five hundred thousand dollars be
2 appropriated to the Department of Emergency Services and Public
3 Protection, for Other Expenses, for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027,
4 to provide grants to municipalities, independent fire companies and
5 state agencies to remove PFAS from fire apparatus or provide
6 reimbursement for removal of PFAS from fire apparatus that occurred
7 prior to July 1, 2023.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to municipalities, fire companies and state entities
to remove PFAS from fire apparatus.
