60 Sec. 3. Section 13b-38p of the general statutes is repealed and the
61 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
62 There is [hereby] established a voluntary traffic reduction program in
63 order to achieve the goals of the Clean Air Act. Any [affected] employer
64 which elects to participate in such program shall submit a plan and an
65 annual update to the Commissioner of Transportation. Such plan shall
66 describe the measures to be implemented to (1) reduce single occupancy
67 vehicle trips to and from the work location of such employer, (2)
68 encourage the use of public transportation to and from the work
69 location of such employer, and [to] (3) relieve traffic congestion. Any
70 [such affected] employer which elects to participate in the program shall
71 be eligible for a tax credit pursuant to the provisions of section 12-217s,
72 as amended by this act, and assistance pursuant to section 13b-38v, as
73 amended by this act, provided such plan has been approved by the
74 commissioner.
