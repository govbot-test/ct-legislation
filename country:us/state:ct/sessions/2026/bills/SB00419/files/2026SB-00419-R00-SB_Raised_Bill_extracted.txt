AN ACT CONCERNING SUPPLIERS OF ELECTRICITY.

Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 16-244b of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 [All customers of] On and after July 1, 2000, any customer of an
4 electric distribution [companies] company, as defined in section 16-1,
5 shall have the opportunity to purchase electric generation services from
6 [their] the customer's choice of electric suppliers, as defined in section
7 16-1, in a competitive generation market. [in accordance with the
8 schedule provided in this section. As of July 1, 2000, all customers shall
9 have the opportunity to choose an electric supplier.] On and after
10 January 1, 2000, electric generation services shall be provided in
11 accordance with section 16-244c to any customer who has not chosen an
12 electric supplier or has declined, failed or been unable to enter into or
13 maintain a contract for electric generation services with an electric
14 supplier. The Public Utilities Regulatory Authority may adopt
15 regulations, in accordance with chapter 54, to implement the [phase-in
16 schedule provided in] provisions of this section.
