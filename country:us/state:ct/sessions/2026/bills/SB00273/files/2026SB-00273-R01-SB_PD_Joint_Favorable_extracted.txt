Introduced by:
(PD)
AN ACT CONCERNING LOCAL GOVERNMENT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 7-411 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 [Such] The civil service board shall, [within] not later than ninety
4 days after the initial appointment of such commissioners, classify all the
5 officers and employees in the departments for which the merit system
6 has been adopted, except elective officers, as provided in section 7-407.
7 [; and the] The board may, [also,] in its discretion, exempt from test and
8 competition the officer responsible for the policy of any department, and
9 one deputy, private secretary or other confidential employee of such
10 officer. The officers and employees so classified by such board shall
11 constitute the classified civil service, and no appointment of any such
12 officer or employment of any such employee shall be made except in
13 accordance with the rules hereinafter provided for.
