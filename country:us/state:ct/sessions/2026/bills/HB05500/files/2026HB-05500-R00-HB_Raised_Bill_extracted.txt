AN ACT CONCERNING A WAIVER OF INTEREST ON CERTAIN
DELINQUENT MUNICIPAL PROPERTY TAXES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) Notwithstanding the provisions of
2 chapter 204 of the general statutes, the city of Bridgeport and the town
3 of Stratford shall abate the total amount of interest on any delinquent
4 real property taxes owed to said city and said town as of the effective
5 date of this section by any housing cooperative that is located partly in
6 said city and said town and contains more than nine hundred units.
7 Nothing in this section shall be construed to require the continued
8 abatement of such interest for any real property tax due to said city or
9 said town after the effective date of this section.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
