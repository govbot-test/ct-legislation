General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 447
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A HOMESTEAD PROPERTY TAX
EXEMPTION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 12-81 of the 2026 supplement to the general statutes
2 is amended by adding subdivision (85) as follows (Effective from passage
3 and applicable to assessment years commencing on or after October 1, 2027):
4 (NEW) (85) (A) Any dwelling declared to be the primary residence of
5 (i) the owner or owners of such dwelling, or (ii) any person or persons
6 for whom such dwelling is held in trust, in accordance with the
7 provisions of subparagraph (B) of this subdivision, to the amount of fifty
8 thousand dollars of the assessed value of such dwelling.
9 (B) Any owner or owners of a dwelling, or person or persons for
10 whom such dwelling is held in trust, claiming the exemption provided
11 in this subdivision for any assessment year shall, on or before the first
12 day of November in such assessment year, file with the assessor or
13 board of assessors in the municipality in which such dwelling is located
14 an application claiming such exemption. Such application shall be made
15 in the manner prescribed and on a form prepared for such purpose by
16 the Secretary of the Office of Policy and Management, and shall include,
17 but not be limited to, a declaration that (i) such dwelling is the primary
18 residence of the applicant or applicants, (ii) the applicant or applicants
