Statement of Purpose:
To modernize state revenue collection of payroll expense through an
employer administrative optimization program, establish a structural
efficiency dividend to be applied to the reduction of public benefits
charges on electric bills and provide for the enhancement of employee
retirement assets through qualifying retirement investment plans.
