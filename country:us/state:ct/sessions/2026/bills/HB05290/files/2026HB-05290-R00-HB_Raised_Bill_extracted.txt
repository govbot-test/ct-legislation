AN ACT CONCERNING COASTAL SITE PLAN REVIEWS FOR
CERTAIN SINGLE-FAMILY RESIDENTIAL STRUCTURES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (b) of section 22a-109 of the 2026 supplement to
2 the general statutes is repealed and the following is substituted in lieu
3 thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
4 (b) The zoning commission may by regulation exempt any or all of
5 the following uses from the coastal site plan review requirements of this
6 chapter: (1) Minor additions to or modifications of existing buildings or
7 detached accessory buildings, such as garages and utility sheds; (2)
8 construction of new or modification of existing structures incidental to
9 the enjoyment and maintenance of residential property including but
10 not limited to walks, terraces, elevated decks, driveways, swimming
11 pools, tennis courts, docks and detached accessory buildings; (3)
12 construction of new or modification of existing on-premise structures
13 including fences, walls, pedestrian walks and terraces, underground
14 utility connections, essential electric, gas, telephone, water and sewer
15 service lines, signs and such other minor structures as will not

16 substantially alter the natural character of coastal resources or restrict
17 access along the public beach; (4) activities conducted for the specific
18 purpose of conserving or preserving soil, vegetation, water, fish,
19 shellfish, wildlife and other coastal land and water resources; (5) interior
20 modifications to buildings; [and] (6) minor changes in use of a building,

23 family residential structure except when such structure is (A) located on

28 shall be exempt from the requirements of this chapter. If a zoning
29 commission adopts an exemption pursuant to subdivision (7) of this
30 subsection, such commission shall, not less than quarterly, submit a
31 report to the Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection

33 commission during the period covered by such report. Notwithstanding
34 the provisions of this subsection, shoreline flood and erosion control
35 structures as defined in subsection (c) of this section shall not be exempt
36 from the requirements of this chapter.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 22a-109(b)
Statement of Purpose:
To allow zoning commissions to exempt certain single-family
residential structures from requirements concerning coastal site plan
reviews and instead submit quarterly reports to the Commissioner of
Energy and Environmental Protection concerning the approval of such
structures.
