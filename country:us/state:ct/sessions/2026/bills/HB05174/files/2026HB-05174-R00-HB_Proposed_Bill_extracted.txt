AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE CITY OF NEW LONDON
FOR LOSS OF TAXES ON PRIVATE TAX-EXEMPT PROPERTY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That subdivision (4) of subsection (f) of section 4-66p of the general
2 statutes be amended to provide that four million dollars be paid for
3 reimbursement to the city of New London with respect to the United
4 States Coast Guard Academy annually for loss of taxes on private tax-
5 exempt property.
Statement of Purpose:
To require four million dollars be paid to the city of New London to
better reflect the lost property tax revenue from the United States Coast
Guard Academy.
