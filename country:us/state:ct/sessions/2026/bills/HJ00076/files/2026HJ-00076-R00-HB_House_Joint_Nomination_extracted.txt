RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF PATRICK T. RING,

COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Patrick T.
2 Ring, Esquire, of Windsor to be a Judge of the Superior Court, to serve
3 for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both Houses,
4 in succession to the Honorable David P. Gold, is approved and
5 confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for said
6 term.
