AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR COMMUNITY ACTION
AGENCIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of thirty million dollars be appropriated to the
2 Department of Social Services, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year
3 ending June 30, 2027, for grants to community action agencies.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide critical funding to local community action agencies and
protect the people the agencies serve.
