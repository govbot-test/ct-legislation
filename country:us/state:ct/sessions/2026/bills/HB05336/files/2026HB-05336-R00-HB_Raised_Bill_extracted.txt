AN ACT CONCERNING ADVANCED NUCLEAR ENERGY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 2-138 of the general statutes is amended by adding
2 subsection (f) as follows (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 (f) The council shall, within available resources, conduct a study
4 concerning the demand for skilled labor in the advanced nuclear energy
5 industry, including the development of nuclear power technologies
6 such as small modular reactors, microreactors or reactors that use
7 cooling sources such as molten salt, liquid metal or high temperature
8 gas. In connection with such study, the council may recommend
9 legislation or regulations necessary to support the development,
10 expansion and operations of advanced nuclear energy technologies in
11 the state, including, but not limited to, recommendations concerning: (1)
12 Methods to meet the need for skilled trades workers and other providers
13 of services or labor; (2) the creation of opportunities for state agencies to
14 collaborate with federal agencies, institutions of higher education,
15 businesses, nonprofit organizations and other stakeholders with an
16 interest in the advanced nuclear energy industry; and (3) any other

Statement of Purpose:
To require the Connecticut Council for Advancing Nuclear Energy
