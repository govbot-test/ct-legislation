General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 359
February Session, 2026
AN ACT AUTHORIZING THE DEFERRAL OF A PROPERTY
REVALUATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) Notwithstanding the provisions
2 of section 12-62 of the general statutes or any municipal charter, special
3 act or home rule ordinance, the city of Hartford, which is required to
4 implement a revaluation of real property for the assessment year
5 commencing October 1, 2026, pursuant to section 12-62 of the general
6 statutes, may defer such implementation until the assessment year
7 commencing October 1, 2027, provided such deferral is approved by the
8 legislative body of such city. The rate maker, as defined in section 12-
9 131 of the general statutes, may prepare new rate bills under the
10 provisions of chapter 204 of the general statutes to carry out the
11 provisions of this section.
12 (b) Any required revaluation subsequent to any deferred revaluation
13 implemented pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall be
14 implemented in accordance with the provisions of section 12-62 of the
15 general statutes. Such subsequent revaluation shall recommence at the
16 point in the schedule required pursuant to section 12-62 of the general
17 statutes that such city was following prior to such deferral.
