Explanation
The bill modifies a previous conveyance under section 4 of public act
18-154 in order to convey a specified parcel of land from the Department
of Administrative Services, on behalf of the Department of
Developmental Services (DDS), to the town of Stratford. The land being
conveyed in this bill was separated out from the contiguous property to
remain under state control under the terms of the 2018 conveyance.
The state would lose the asset, but retain access subject to a written
agreement between DDS and the town of Stratford. The town of
Stratford would gain use of an asset and would pay the administrative
costs of the conveyance. The asset would revert to state control if the
recipient does not use the land for specified purposes.
The land conveyance is subject to the review and approval of the State
Properties Review Board.
