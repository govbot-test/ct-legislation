General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 139
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1032
Referred to Committee on EDUCATION
Introduced by:
SEN. FLEXER, 29th Dist.
REP. HADDAD, 54th Dist.
REP. BOYD, 50th Dist.
AN ACT INCREASING THE APPROPRIATION FOR THE SPECIAL
EDUCATION AND EXPANSION DEVELOPMENT GRANT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That section 10-76ggg of the general statutes be amended to increase
2 the appropriation of the special education and expansion development
3 grant by one hundred ninety-one million dollars for the fiscal year
4 ending June 30, 2027.
Statement of Purpose:
To support students with special education needs and protect school
districts from volatility in special education expenditures.
