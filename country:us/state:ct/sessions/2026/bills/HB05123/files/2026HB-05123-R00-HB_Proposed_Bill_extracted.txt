General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5123
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 514
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. HOXHA, 78th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A CLOUD-
BASED FILING SYSTEM FOR THE TOWN OF PLYMOUTH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 twenty thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Office of Policy and Management for the purpose of providing a grant-
6 in-aid to the town of Plymouth for the implementation of a cloud-based
7 filing system.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a cloud-based filing system for the town of
Plymouth.
