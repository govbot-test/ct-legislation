General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5101
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 725
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. ROCHELLE, 104th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING TO INCREASE THE FREQUENCY
OF THE CTTRANSIT NEW HAVEN 255 BUS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That additional funds be appropriated to the Department of
2 Transportation for the fiscal year ending June 30, 2027, for the purposes
3 of increasing the frequency of the CTTransit New Haven 255 bus
4 between the Naugatuck Valley and the city of New Haven at certain
5 times.
Statement of Purpose:
To appropriate additional funds to increase the frequency of the
CTTransit New Haven 255 Bus between the Naugatuck Valley and the
city of New Haven at certain times.
