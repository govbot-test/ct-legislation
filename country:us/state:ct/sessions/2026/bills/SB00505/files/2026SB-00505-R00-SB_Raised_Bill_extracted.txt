AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF THE LAWS OF THIS STATE
CONCERNING ISSUES OF CRIMINAL JUSTICE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) There is established a working group
2 to study the laws of this state concerning issues of criminal justice. Such
3 study shall include, at a minimum, a review of titles 18, 53, 53a and 54
4 of the general statutes for the purpose of recommending legislative
5 changes to said titles. The working group shall consist of the following
6 members who have expertise in criminal law and procedure and the
7 criminal justice system: (1) One appointed by the speaker of the House
8 of Representatives; (2) one appointed by the president pro tempore of
9 the Senate; (3) one appointed by the majority leader of the House of
10 Representatives; (4) one appointed by the majority leader of the Senate;
11 (5) one appointed by the minority leader of the House of
12 Representatives; and (6) one appointed by the minority leader of the
13 Senate. The administrative staff of the joint standing committee of the
14 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to the judiciary
15 shall serve as the administrative staff for the working group. Not later
16 than January 1, 2027, the working group shall submit a report on its
