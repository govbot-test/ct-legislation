AN ACT CONCERNING SPECIAL EDUCATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (d) of section 10-236b of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (d) (1) No school employee shall place a student in seclusion except
5 as an emergency intervention to prevent immediate or imminent injury
6 to the student or to others, provided the seclusion is not used for
7 discipline or convenience and is not used as a substitute for a less
8 restrictive alternative. (2) No student shall be placed in seclusion unless
9 (A) such student is monitored by a school employee during the period
10 of such student's seclusion pursuant to subsection (m) of this section,
11 and (B) the area in which such student is secluded is equipped with a
12 camera and a window or other fixture allowing such student a clear line
13 of sight beyond the area of seclusion. (3) Seclusion shall not be utilized
14 as a planned intervention in a student's behavioral intervention plan,
15 individualized education program or plan pursuant to Section 504 of the
16 Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended from time to time.
