AN ACT REQUIRING ANNUAL STATE AGENCY PERFORMANCE
PLANS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section,
2 "state agency" means any department, board, council, commission,
3 institution or other agency in the executive branch of state government,
4 including, but not limited to, each constituent unit.
5 (b) Not later than July 1, 2027, and annually thereafter, each state
6 agency shall develop and implement a performance plan that outlines
7 the state agency's strategic and operational goals. The Office of Policy
8 and Management shall establish guidelines for such plans and shall
9 provide training and technical assistance in plan development and
10 implementation to state agencies upon request.
11 (c) Not later than January 1, 2028, and annually thereafter, each state
12 agency shall report on the state agency's performance plan and its
13 progress in attaining the strategic and operational goals established in
14 the state agency's plan developed pursuant to subsection (b) of this
15 section during the prior year to the Office of Policy and Management,

This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require executive branch state agencies to develop and implement
annual performance plans.
