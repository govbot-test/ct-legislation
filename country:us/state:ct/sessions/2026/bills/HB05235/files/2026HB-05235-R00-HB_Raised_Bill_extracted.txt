General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5235

Referred to Committee on TRANSPORTATION
Introduced by:
(TRA)
AN ACT CONCERNING A PLAN AND COORDINATED RESPONSE TO
PERSONS EXPERIENCING HOMELESSNESS ON PROPERTY UNDER
THE CONTROL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Transportation, in consultation with the interagency council on
3 homelessness established pursuant to section 8-37vvv of the general
4 statutes, municipalities, local housing authorities and organizations that
5 provide programs for emergency shelter services, transitional housing
6 services or on-site social services, shall develop a plan to respond to,
7 manage and deter temporary encampments by persons experiencing
8 homelessness upon any state highway right-of-way or real property
9 owned by the state and in the custody and control of the Department of
10 Transportation. Such plan shall, at a minimum, (1) establish procedures
11 for outreach and engagement by trained personnel that ensure respect
12 for the personal dignity and property of persons experiencing
13 homelessness, (2) require reasonable efforts to offer immediate
14 assistance and support for emergency shelters to such persons, and (3)
15 provide for coordination with the municipality where the temporary

Statement of Purpose:
To require the Commissioner of Transportation to develop a plan to

custody of the Department of Transportation.
