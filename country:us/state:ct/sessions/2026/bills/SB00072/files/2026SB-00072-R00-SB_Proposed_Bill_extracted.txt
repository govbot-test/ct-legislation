General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 72
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 98
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
AN ACT ELIMINATING STATE TAXES OR FEES FOR WHICH
COLLECTION COSTS EXCEED THE REVENUE RECEIVED.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to eliminate any state tax or fee
2 for which the cost of collection exceeds the revenue received by the state
3 from the imposition of such tax or fee.
Statement of Purpose:
To eliminate any state tax or fee for which the cost of collection exceeds
the revenue received by the state from the imposition of such tax or fee.
