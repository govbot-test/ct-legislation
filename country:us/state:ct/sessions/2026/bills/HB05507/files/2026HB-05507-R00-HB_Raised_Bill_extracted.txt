Statement of Purpose:
To (1) change statutory references to "accessory apartments" to
"accessory dwelling units", (2) repeal a provision allowing
municipalities to opt out of allowing accessory dwelling units on
residential lots, (3) prohibit the enforcement of certain residential land
use restrictions, and (4) require the Commissioner of Housing to
establish a program concerning preapproved accessory dwelling units.
