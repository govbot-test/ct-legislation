Section 1:
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 997
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. WILSON, 46th Dist.
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
REP. PEMBERTON, 139th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR A FIRE
SERVICE DISPATCH SYSTEM FOR THE CITY OF NORWICH.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate six
4 hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Emergency Services and Public Protection for the
6 purpose of providing a grant-in-aid to the city of Norwich for a fire
7 service dispatch system.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a fire dispatch system for the city of Norwich.
LCO No. 997 1 of 1
