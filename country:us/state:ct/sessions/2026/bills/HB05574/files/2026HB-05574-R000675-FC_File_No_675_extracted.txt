Explanation
The bill extends a sale deadline from a 2025 conveyance. There is
either no fiscal impact, or the impact described below, depending on the
timing of the sale.
To the extent the sale is completed under the original terms (i.e., prior
to the end of FY 26 or within a possible six month extension) or does not
happen under the extended timeframe, there is no fiscal impact.
To the extent that the sale happens in the extended time frame, the
bill has the following impact:
The bill conveys a specified 50.29 acre parcel of land from the
Department of Children and Families, via the Department of
Administrative Services, to the town of Hamden. The state would lose
use of an asset (most recently valued at $8,400,000) and gain revenue of
the specified purchase price ($1,500,000). The town of Hamden would
gain use of an asset and pay the purchase price plus administrative costs
