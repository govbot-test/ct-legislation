Referred to Committee on EDUCATION

AN ACT CONCERNING PUBLIC EDUCATION IN THE STATE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Department of Education shall
2 conduct a study of public education in Connecticut. Not later than May
3 26, 2026, the department shall submit a report of the results of such
4 study to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
5 cognizance of matters relating to education, in accordance with the
6 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 from passage New section
Statement of Purpose:
To require the Department of Education to conduct a study of public
education in Connecticut.
