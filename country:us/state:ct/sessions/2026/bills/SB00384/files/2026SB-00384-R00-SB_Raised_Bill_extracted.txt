Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 4-67o of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1, 2026):
3 As used in this section and sections 2-79e, 4-67p and 4-67z:
4 (1) "Data" means the final version of statistical or factual information
5 that: (A) Is reflected in a list, table, graph, chart or other non-narrative
6 form that can be digitally or nondigitally transmitted or processed; (B)
7 is regularly created or maintained by, or on behalf of, an executive
8 branch agency; and (C) records a measurement, transaction or
9 determination related to the mission of the agency or is provided to the
10 agency by third parties pursuant to law.
11 (2) "Executive branch agency" means any state agency, [listed in
12 section 4-38c, except the Board of Regents for Higher Education] as
13 defined in section 4d-1, except the constituent units of the state system
14 of higher education and the offices of the Secretary of the State,

36 with any and all applicable laws, rules, regulations, ordinances,
37 resolutions, policies or other restrictions, requirements or rights
38 associated with the data, including, but not limited to, contractual or
39 other legal restrictions, orders or requirements.
