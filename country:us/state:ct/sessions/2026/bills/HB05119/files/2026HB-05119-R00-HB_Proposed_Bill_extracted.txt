BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. GAIEWSKI, 40th Dist.
REP. NOLAN, 39th Dist.
SEN. MARX, 20th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE FOR THE LYMAN
ALLYN ART MUSEUM IN THE CITY OF NEW LONDON.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate six
4 hundred thousand dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the Lyman Allyn Art Museum in the city
7 of New London for a new roof.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for a new roof at the Lyman Allyn Art Museum and
ensure the valuable art of the museum is in a safe environment.
