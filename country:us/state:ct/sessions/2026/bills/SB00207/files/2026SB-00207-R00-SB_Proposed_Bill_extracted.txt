AN ACT EXEMPTING OVERTIME INCOME FROM THE PERSONAL
INCOME TAX.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to exempt from the personal
2 income tax the amount of any overtime income earned by an employee.

To exempt from the personal income tax the amount of any overtime
income earned by an employee.
