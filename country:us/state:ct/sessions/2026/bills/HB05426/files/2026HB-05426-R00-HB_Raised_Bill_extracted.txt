General Assembly Raised Bill No. 5426

Referred to Committee on HIGHER EDUCATION AND
EMPLOYMENT ADVANCEMENT
Introduced by:
(HED)
AN ACT REQUIRING THE AVAILABILITY OF AN ADVISOR DURING
DISCIPLINARY PROCEEDINGS AT INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER
EDUCATION.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Not later than August 1, 2026,
2 each institution of higher education in the state shall adopt a policy that
3 any student shall be entitled to be accompanied to any meeting relating
4 to a disciplinary proceeding concerning such student by an advisor or
5 support person of such student's choice, provided (1) the involvement
6 of such advisor or support person does not result in the postponement
7 or delay of such meeting as scheduled, and (2) for disciplinary
8 proceedings concerning sexual assault, stalking and intimate partner
9 violence, the policies adopted pursuant to the provisions of section 10a-
10 55m of the general statutes concerning a student's entitlement to be
11 accompanied by an advisor or support person shall take precedence
12 over the implementation of the policy adopted under this section to the
13 extent such policies differ.
