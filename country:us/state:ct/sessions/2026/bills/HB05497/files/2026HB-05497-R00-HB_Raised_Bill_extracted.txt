Introduced by:

AN ACT ESTABLISHING A TASK FORCE TO STUDY THE EFFECTS
OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE ON THE TRADES INDUSTRY AND
REQUIRING A STUDY OF A CERTIFIED NURSING ASSISTANT
TRAINING PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a task force
2 to study the effects of the use of artificial intelligence on the trades
3 industry. Such study shall include, but need not be limited to, an
4 examination of (1) job displacement of employees in the trades industry,
5 and (2) current coursework and curriculum of the Technical and
6 Education Career System regarding the use of artificial intelligence.
7 (b) The task force shall consist of the following members:
8 (1) Two appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives;
9 (2) Two appointed by the president pro tempore of the Senate;
10 (3) One appointed by the majority leader of the House of
11 Representatives;

22 (d) All initial appointments to the task force shall be made not later

26 tempore of the Senate shall select the chairpersons of the task force from
27 among the members of the task force. Such chairpersons shall schedule
28 the first meeting of the task force, which shall be held not later than sixty
29 days after the effective date of this section.
30 (f) The administrative staff of the joint standing committee of the
31 General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor and
32 public employees shall serve as administrative staff of the task force.
33 (g) Not later than January 1, 2027, the task force shall submit a report
34 on its findings and recommendations to the joint standing committee of
35 the General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to labor
36 and public employees, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-
37 4a of the general statutes. The task force shall terminate on the date that
38 it submits such report or January 1, 2027, whichever is later.

Statement of Purpose:
To establish a task force to study the effects of the use of artificial
intelligence on the trades industry and require the Commissioner of
Public Health to study the feasibility of establishing a certified nursing
assistant training program.
