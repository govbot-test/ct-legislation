2 Department of Transportation shall restore service on the Shore Line
3 East rail line to the same level of service that existed prior to the

5 the civil preparedness emergency declared by the Governor on March
6 10, 2020.

51 Sec. 4. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) The Department of Education
52 shall administer a grant program to provide grants to local and regional
53 boards of education for the purchase of passes for the use of state-owned
54 or state-controlled bus public transportation services and distribution of
55 such passes, without cost, to students who are enrolled in grades nine
56 to twelve, inclusive, of a public school under the jurisdiction of such
57 local or regional board of education. Applications for grants shall be
58 filed with the department at such time and in such manner as the
59 department prescribes. The department may develop guidelines and
60 grant criteria as it deems necessary to administer such grant program.
61 (b) Each local or regional board of education receiving a grant award
62 under this section shall submit, at such time and in such form as the
63 department prescribes, any reports and financial statements required by
64 the department. If the department finds that any grant awarded
65 pursuant to this section is being used for purposes that are not in
66 conformity with the purposes of this section, the department may
67 require the repayment of the grant to the state.
68 (c) Not later than July 1, 2027, and annually thereafter, the
69 Department of Education shall submit a report, in accordance with the
70 provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes, to the joint standing
71 committees of the General Assembly having cognizance of matters
72 relating to education and transportation. Such report shall include, but
73 need not be limited to, the amount of grants awarded during the prior
74 year and an assessment of the impact of the grant program on student
75 outcomes.

Statement of Purpose:
To (1) restore service on Shore Line East, (2) implement certain public
transportation projects and initiatives, (3) require the Department of
Transportation to provide notice of proposed fare increases, instead of
any fare change, for mass transportation by land, and (4) establish
programs for the purchase of bus passes for distribution to high school
students and veterans.
