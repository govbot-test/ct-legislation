General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 67
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3338
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF SEAN P.
SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Sean P.
2 McGuinness, Esquire, of Norwalk to be a Judge of the Superior Court,
3 to serve for a term of eight years from the date of confirmation by both
4 Houses, in succession to the Honorable Theodore R. Tyma, is approved
5 and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior Court for
6 said term.
