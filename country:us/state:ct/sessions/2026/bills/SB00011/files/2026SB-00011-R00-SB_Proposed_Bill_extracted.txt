General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 11

Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS

SEN. SOMERS, 18th Dist.
AN ACT CONCERNING FUNDING FOR THE PREAPPRENTICESHIP
GRANT PROGRAM.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the sum of eight hundred thousand dollars be appropriated to
2 the Department of Education, from the General Fund, for the fiscal year
3 ending June 30, 2027, for the preapprenticeship grant program,
4 established under section 10-21p of the general statutes.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding for the high school preapprenticeship grant
program.
