General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5243
February Session, 2026
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A WORKING GROUP TO DEVELOP AN
ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND TOURISM PLAN FOR THE
GREATER MYSTIC AREA.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a working
2 group to develop a plan relating to economic development and tourism
3 in the greater Mystic area. Such plan shall include, but need not be
4 limited to, an examination of (1) the potential federal, state or other
5 sources of funding for the restoration of any breakwater located in
6 Stonington Harbor, (2) transportation options to ease traffic congestion
7 and alleviate parking pressure, and (3) potential easements and
8 infrastructure improvements to enhance accessibility and coastal
9 resiliency. For the purposes of this subsection, "the greater Mystic area"
10 means the towns of Groton and Stonington.
11 (b) The working group shall consist of the following members: (1) The
12 chairpersons and ranking members of the joint standing committee of
13 the General Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to
14 commerce, or their designees; (2) the Commissioner of Economic and
15 Community Development, or the commissioner's designee; (3) the
16 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection, or the
17 commissioner's designee; (4) the Commissioner of Transportation, or
18 the commissioner's designee; (5) the Commissioner of Agriculture, or
