Referred to Committee on Aging

(AGE)
AN ACT ALLOWING TRAINED ASSISTED LIVING AIDES TO
ADMINISTER MEDICATION TO ASSISTED LIVING RESIDENTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "Administration" means the direct application of a medication by
3 means other than injection to the body of a person;
4 (2) "Assisted living aide" has the same meaning as provided in section
5 19-13-D105 of the regulations of Connecticut state agencies;
6 (3) "Assisted living services agency" has the same meaning as
7 provided in section 19a-490 of the general statutes;
8 (4) "Medication" means any medicinal preparation and includes any
9 controlled substances specifically designated in the regulations adopted
10 pursuant to subsection (d) of this section;
11 (5) "Registered nurse" means a registered nurse licensed pursuant to
12 chapter 378 of the general statutes; and
13 (6) "Trained assisted living aide" means an assisted living aide who
14 has successfully completed training for the administration of

31 (d) The Commissioner of Public Health shall adopt regulations, in
