General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 3
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1724
Referred to Committee on JUDICIARY
Introduced by:
(JUD)
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE CLAIMS COMMISSIONER'S
CLAIM AGAINST THE STATE FOR WRONGFUL INCARCERATION.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number 24394 of
2 said commissioner, that compensation in the amount of $16,500.00,
3 payable from the adjudicated claims account of the General Fund, be
4 awarded to Mark Stuart with respect to his claim against the state for
5 wrongful incarceration, is confirmed.
