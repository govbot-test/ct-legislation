General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5148
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING THE EXISTENCE OF AND METHODS OF
ADDRESSING RACIAL IMBALANCE IN PUBLIC SCHOOLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) (a) The Department of Education shall
2 study the effectiveness of the method of addressing racial imbalance
3 specified in sections 10-226a to 10-226e, inclusive, of the general statutes
4 and the regulations adopted pursuant to said sections. Such study shall
5 include, but not be limited to, (1) an examination of the effectiveness of
6 various strategies implemented to correct racial imbalances in the long
7 term and short term, (2) a cost benefit analysis of implementing such
8 strategies, (3) the impact on pupils of racial minorities, as described in
9 section 10-226a of the general statutes, as a result of such strategies, such
10 as access to neighborhood schools and educational outcomes, and (4)
11 suggestions for alternative methods for correcting racial imbalances.
12 (b) Not later than January 1, 2028, the Department of Education shall
13 submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the
14 general statutes, to the joint standing committee of the General
15 Assembly having cognizance of matters relating to education on the
16 findings of the study conducted pursuant to subsection (a) of this
17 section.

44 students causing said imbalance is fewer than five students at a school.

46 sections 10-226a to 10-226e, inclusive, shall include any proposed
