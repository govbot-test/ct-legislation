AN ACT CONCERNING VOCATIONAL EDUCATION AND
WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective July 1, 2026) The executive director of the
2 Technical Education and Career System shall study the feasibility of
3 expanding vocational education programs to students in grades
4 kindergarten to eight, inclusive. Not later than January 1, 2027, the
5 executive director shall submit a report on the results of such study, in
6 accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes,
7 to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
8 cognizance of matters relating to education.
9 Sec. 2. (Effective July 1, 2026) For the school year commencing July 1,
10 2026, the executive director of the Technical Education and Career
11 System shall establish a pilot program for the provision of one or more
12 courses to train students to become certified wastewater treatment
13 facility operators at technical education and career schools throughout
14 the state. Not later than January 1, 2027, the executive director shall
15 submit a report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the
