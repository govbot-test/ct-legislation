198 Sec. 4. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The Connecticut Housing
199 Finance Authority shall develop and administer a program of mortgage
200 assistance to police officers, as defined in section 7-294a of the general
201 statutes, and uniformed members of paid or volunteer fire departments
202 in the state. Such assistance shall be available to an eligible police officer
203 or firefighter, under guidelines adopted by the authority, for the
204 purchase of a home used as such police officer's or firefighter's principal
205 residence in the community served by such police officer or firefighter.
206 In making mortgage assistance available under the program, the
207 authority may utilize down payment assistance or any other
208 appropriate housing subsidies. The terms of any mortgage assistance
209 may allow the mortgagee to realize a reasonable portion of any equity
210 gain upon sale of the mortgaged property.
