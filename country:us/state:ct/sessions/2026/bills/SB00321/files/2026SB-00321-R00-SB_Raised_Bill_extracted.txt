AN ACT CONCERNING REQUIREMENTS FOR CERTAIN
EXCAVATION PROJECTS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 16-353 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 Except as provided in section 16-350, this chapter shall not be
4 construed to affect or impair local ordinances, charters or other
5 provisions of law requiring permits to be obtained before excavating in
6 a public highway or to demolish structures on private property, nor
7 shall it be construed to grant to any person or public agency any rights
8 not specifically provided by this chapter. A permit from a public agency
9 shall not relieve any person from responsibility for complying with the
10 provisions of this chapter. The failure of any person who has been
11 granted a permit to comply with the provisions of this chapter shall not
12 be deemed to impose any liability upon the public agency issuing the
13 permit. No person who obtains a permit to excavate in a public highway
14 shall be required to maintain such excavation in an open state for any
15 period of time after the completion of the excavation work unless
