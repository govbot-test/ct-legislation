COMMISSIONER TO DISMISS THE CLAIM AGAINST THE STATE OF
EDGAR CANTERBURY.

1 That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number 27205 of

3 of Edgar Canterbury, is vacated and the claim is remanded to the Claims
4 Commissioner for a hearing on the merits.
