General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 159
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1001
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
SEN. OSTEN, 19th Dist.
AN ACT AUTHORIZING BONDS OF THE STATE TO EXTEND THE
SPRAGUE STREETSCAPE PROJECT IN THE TOWN OF SPRAGUE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the State Bond Commission be empowered to authorize the
2 issuance of bonds of the state in accordance with section 3-20 of the
3 general statutes, in principal amounts not exceeding in the aggregate
4 one million dollars, the proceeds of which shall be used by the
5 Department of Economic and Community Development for the purpose
6 of providing a grant-in-aid to the town of Sprague to extend the Sprague
7 Streetscape Project to upgrade all three municipal playgrounds, finish
8 upgrading all streetlights to the same standard as the streetlights located
9 on Main Street in the village of Baltic by installing ten streetlights and
10 flashing lights for both schools and install an informational electronic
11 bulletin board.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide funding to the town of Sprague to extend the Sprague
Streetscape Project.
