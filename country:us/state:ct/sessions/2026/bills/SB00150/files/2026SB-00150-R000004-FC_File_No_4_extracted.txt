sSB 150
AN ACT CONCERNING THE LEGISLATIVE COMMISSIONERS'
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR TECHNICAL REVISIONS TO THE
HIGHER EDUCATION STATUTES.
SUMMARY
This bill eliminates a requirement that at least $100,000 of available
funding for the Roberta B. Willis Scholarship Program be for a Charter
Oak grant, which conforms to the elimination of the Charter Oak grant
as of July 1, 2026 (see CGS § 10a-173, as amended by PA 25-168, § 263).
The bill also makes technical changes.
EFFECTIVE DATE: July 1, 2026
