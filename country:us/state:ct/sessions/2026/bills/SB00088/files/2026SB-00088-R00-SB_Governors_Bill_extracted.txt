AN ACT REQUIRING INSURANCE COVERAGE FOR SCALP
COOLING.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective January 1, 2027) (a) For the purposes of this
2 section, "scalp cooling system" means any device designed and intended
3 for repeated medical use to cool the human scalp to prevent or reduce
4 hair loss as a result of chemotherapy.
5 (b) (1) Each individual health insurance policy providing coverage of
6 the type specified in subdivisions (1), (2), (4), (11), (12) and (13) of section
7 38a-469 of the general statutes delivered, issued for delivery, renewed,
8 amended or continued in this state on or after January 1, 2027, that
9 provides coverage for chemotherapy shall provide coverage for scalp
10 cooling systems used in connection with such chemotherapy that is at
11 least equivalent to such coverage provided under Medicare.
12 (2) No policy described in subdivision (1) of this subsection shall
13 impose a coinsurance, copayment, deductible or other out-of-pocket
14 expense for any such scalp cooling system that is more restrictive than
