General Assembly House Joint
Resolution No. 8
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 1222
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF HANSIKA
LENKALA OF EAST GRANBY TO BE A NONVOTING STUDENT
MEMBER OF THE STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Hansika
2 Lenkala of East Granby to be appointed a nonvoting student member of
3 the State Board of Education, in succession to Sophia Messina, to serve
4 a term ending June 30, 2026, or until a successor is appointed and has
5 qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
LCO No. 1222 1 of 1
