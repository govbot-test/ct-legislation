3 (a) For purposes of this section, the following terms have the

5 (1) "Mobile telephone" means a cellular, analog, wireless or digital
6 telephone capable of sending or receiving telephone communications

8 (2) "Using" or "use" means (A) holding a hand-held mobile telephone

11 operating the vehicle and properly restrained by such person's seat belt.

13 which a user engages in a call using at least one hand.

Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 14-222
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) include the playing of a video on a mobile electronic device that
is in full view to the person who is operating the vehicle as a form of
distracted driving, and (2) specify that distracted driving in a highway
work zone is a violation of the state's reckless driving statute.
