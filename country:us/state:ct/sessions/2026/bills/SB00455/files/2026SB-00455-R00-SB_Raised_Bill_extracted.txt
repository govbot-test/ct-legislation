General Assembly Raised Bill No. 455
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 2420
Referred to Committee on ENVIRONMENT
Introduced by:
(ENV)
AN ACT CONCERNING JURISDICTION OVER FARMING OCCURRING
ON CERTAIN STATE LANDS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective July 1, 2026) Notwithstanding any
2 provision of the general statutes, any farming that occurs on any state-
3 owned property, including, but not limited to, any property under the
4 care, custody or control of the Department of Energy and
5 Environmental Protection, shall be overseen by the Department of
6 Agriculture. Any lease agreement for any such farming shall, upon
7 renewal of such lease agreement, be entered into between the
8 Department of Agriculture and the respective lessor.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 July 1, 2026 New section
LCO No. 2420 1 of 2

Raised Bill No. 455
Statement of Purpose:
To transfer oversight of farming occurring on certain state lands from
the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection to the
Department of Agriculture.
