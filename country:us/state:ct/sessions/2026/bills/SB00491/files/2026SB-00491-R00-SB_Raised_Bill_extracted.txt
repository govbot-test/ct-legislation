Statement of Purpose:
To implement the Secretary of the State's recommendations for
implementation of no-excuse absentee voting, including provisions
regarding (1) removal of the statutory restrictions on absentee voting,
(2) distribution of absentee ballot applications, (3) use of a single
envelope and other aspects of returning absentee ballots, (4) ability to
cure absentee ballots in cases where voters have not signed the
statement on the return envelope, and (5) tracking of absentee ballots
and applications throughout the process.
