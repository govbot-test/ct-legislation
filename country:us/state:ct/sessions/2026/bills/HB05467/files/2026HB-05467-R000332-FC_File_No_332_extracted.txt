Explanation
The bill results in a cost of $109,500 in FY 27 and $146,100 in FY 28
and annually thereafter to the Department of Economic and Community
Development by requiring the agency to designate a department
employee to provide Connecticut small businesses concierge services.
The agency will require one full-time community development
specialist at an annualized cost of $103,000 in salary and $43,100 in
fringe benefits to provide these concierge services.
The Out Years
The annualized ongoing fiscal impact identified above would
continue into the future subject to inflation.

THE PURPOSE OF PROVIDING CONCIERGE SERVICES TO SMALL
BUSINESSES.
SUMMARY
This bill requires the Department of Economic and Community
Development (DECD) commissioner to designate a department
employee to give Connecticut small businesses concierge services,
including information and assistance about DECD-administered
economic or community development projects and programs.
