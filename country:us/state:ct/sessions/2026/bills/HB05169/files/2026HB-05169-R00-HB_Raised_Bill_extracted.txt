AN ACT REQUIRING THE ESTABLISHMENT OF AN ALERT IN THE
STATE-WIDE HEALTH INFORMATION EXCHANGE REGARDING
PATIENTS WITH A HISTORY OF VIOLENCE OR COMBATIVE
BEHAVIOR TOWARD A HEALTH CARE PROVIDER.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) Not later than January
2 1, 2027, the Department of Public Health shall develop a system (1) for
3 health care providers with an electronic health records system capable
4 of connecting to and participating in the State-wide Health Information
5 Exchange as specified in section 17b-59e of the general statutes to report
6 to said exchange incidences of patient violence or combative behavior
7 directed at a health care provider, and (2) that alerts a health care
8 provider with such an electronic health records system when the
9 provider accepts a new patient or has a scheduled visit with an existing
10 patient who has a documented history of any such incidence.
11 (b) On and after January 1, 2027, each health care provider with an
12 electronic health record system capable of connecting to and
13 participating in the State-wide Health Information Exchange as
14 specified in section 17b-59e of the general statutes shall document in its
