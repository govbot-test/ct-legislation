NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.

RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF THE HONORABLE
EUGENE CALISTRO, JR. OF GUILFORD TO BE A MEMBER OF THE
JUDICIAL REVIEW COUNCIL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Eugene Calistro, Jr. of Guilford to be a member of the Judicial
3 Review Council, as a judge of the Superior Court who is not also a judge
4 of the Supreme Court, to serve a term ending November 30, 2026, or
5 until a successor is appointed and has qualified, whichever is longer, in
6 succession to the Honorable Susan Quinn Cobb, is hereby approved and
7 confirmed.
