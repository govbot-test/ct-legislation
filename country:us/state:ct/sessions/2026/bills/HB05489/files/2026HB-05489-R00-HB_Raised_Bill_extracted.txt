AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF THE PROVISIONS OF THE
WORKERS' COMPENSATION ACT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) There is established a working group
2 to study the state's Workers' Compensation Act. Such study shall
3 include, but not be limited to, a review of provisions set forth in sections
4 31-275 to 31-355b, inclusive, of the general statutes for the purpose of
5 recommending needed changes to such provisions of the general
6 statutes. The working group shall consist of the following members,
7 who have familiarity with and expertise in the application and
8 interpretation of the state's Workers' Compensation Act: (1) One
9 appointed by the speaker of the House of Representatives; (2) one
10 appointed by the president pro tempore of the Senate; (3) one appointed
11 by the majority leader of the House of Representatives; (4) one
12 appointed by the majority leader of the Senate; (5) one appointed by the
13 minority leader of the House of Representatives; and (6) one appointed
14 by the minority leader of the Senate. Not later than January 1, 2027, the
15 working group shall submit a report on its findings and
16 recommendations to the joint standing committees of the General
