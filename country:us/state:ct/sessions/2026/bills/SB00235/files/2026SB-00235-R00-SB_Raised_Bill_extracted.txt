Raised Bill No. 235
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) permit a student to choose the gender of such student's driving
instructor prior to behind-the-wheel instruction, (2) require vehicles
used by a drivers' school to be equipped with an electronic monitoring
system and an audio or video monitoring system, and (3) provide
behind-the-wheel instruction in how to respond to a traffic stop by a law
enforcement officer.
