General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 69
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3097
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF GLENN POWELL
OF COLLINSVILLE TO BE REAPPOINTED A MEMBER OF THE STATE
DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION ARBITRATION PANEL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Glenn
2 Powell of Collinsville, to be reappointed a member of the State
3 Department of Education Arbitration Panel, as a neutral arbitrator, to
4 serve a term ending June 30, 2028, or until a successor is appointed and
5 has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
LCO No. 3097 1 of 1
