AN ACT CONCERNING A STUDY OF STATE REVENUE POLICIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) The Commissioner of Revenue
2 Services shall study the revenue policies of the state to identify
3 legislative changes that may be made to improve the business climate
4 and economic opportunities in the state. The commissioner may consult
5 with any individuals, businesses and state agencies the commissioner
6 deems necessary or appropriate to accomplish the purposes of the
7 study. Not later than January 1, 2027, the commissioner shall submit a
8 report, in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general
9 statutes, on the commissioner's findings and recommendations to the
10 joint standing committee of the General Assembly having cognizance of
11 matters relating to finance, revenue and bonding.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
