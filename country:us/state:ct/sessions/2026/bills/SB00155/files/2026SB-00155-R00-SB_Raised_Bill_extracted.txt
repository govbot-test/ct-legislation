Statement of Purpose:
To (1) consolidate existing Department of Children and Families
reporting requirements concerning the department's services,
compliance with statutory requirements, facilities operated by the
department, reports of animal harm, neglect or cruelty, the department's
family assessment response program, private providers that contract
with the department, foster care services and licensing and diversion of
children from the juvenile justice and court systems, (2) add a reporting
requirement concerning cost savings resulting from the implementation
of the Behavioral Health Partnership, (3) eliminate a reporting
requirement concerning children missing or abducted from the custody
of the department, and (4) make technical and conforming changes.
