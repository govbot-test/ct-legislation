Explanation
Section 1 through 4 allow taxpayers in Berlin, Lebanon, Middletown,
and West Hartford to receive tax exemptions that they would have
otherwise been eligible to receive if they had not missed the filing
deadline for such exemptions in certain years. Depending on whether
the payments have already been made, this results in a cost to these
towns to reimburse such taxpayers or a revenue loss. It is anticipated
that any impact would only occur in FY 27.
Section 5 allows municipalities to abate certain delinquent taxes. This
results in a revenue loss in FY 27 to the extent a municipality votes to
abate these taxes.
