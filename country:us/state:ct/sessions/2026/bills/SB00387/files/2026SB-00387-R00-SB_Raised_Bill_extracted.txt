1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) There is established a

187 Sec. 3. (NEW) (Effective January 1, 2027) A referred municipality
188 designated by the Municipal Election Accountability Board as a:
189 (1) Tier I municipality shall be subject to oversight by the board
190 consisting of (A) development by such municipality of a plan to mitigate
191 the concerns that warranted such designation by the board, which shall
192 be submitted to the board for approval and modification as required by
193 the board, (B) mandatory training of election officials in election
194 administration procedures and best practices, conducted by the office of
195 the Secretary of the State or by a certified moderator, town clerk or
196 registrar of voters, or a combination thereof, selected by the Secretary of
197 the State, and (C) distribution by said office to such election officials of
198 instruction or training manuals or other resources for promoting best
199 practices in election administration.
200 (2) Tier II municipality shall be subject to oversight by the board
201 consisting of (A) the measures provided under subdivision (1) of this
202 section for a Tier I municipality, and (B) regular contact with the
203 municipality to ensure (i) compliance with the developed mitigation
204 plan and timely achievement of the benchmarks set forth in such
205 mitigation plan, (ii) attendance of election officials at all mandatory
206 training, and (iii) distribution of manuals or resources in accordance
207 with the provisions of subparagraph (C) of subdivision (1) of this
208 section.
209 (3) Tier III municipality shall be subject to oversight by the board
