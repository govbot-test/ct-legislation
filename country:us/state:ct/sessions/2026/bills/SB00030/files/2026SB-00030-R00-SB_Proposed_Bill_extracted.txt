General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 30
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 86
Referred to Committee on APPROPRIATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. SAMPSON, 16th Dist.
AN ACT REDUCING NONEDUCATION AID TO THE CITY OF
HARTFORD.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to reduce noneducation aid to
2 the city of Hartford by an amount equal to the debt service paid by the
3 state on behalf of the city under a contract for financial assistance made
4 pursuant to section 7-576j of the general statutes.
Statement of Purpose:
To reverse the fiscal impact to the state of the debt service agreement
between the state and the city of Hartford.
