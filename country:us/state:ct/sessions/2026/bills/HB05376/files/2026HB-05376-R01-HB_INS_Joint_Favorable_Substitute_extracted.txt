General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5376
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING AFFORDABLE HOUSING APPLICATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 8-30g of the 2026 supplement to the general statutes
2 is amended by adding subsection (n) as follows (Effective October 1,
3 2026):
4 (NEW) (n) Notwithstanding any provision of subsection (g) of this
5 section, a commission may deny or return as incomplete any affordable
6 housing application that utilizes nonstandard, prefabricated or
7 proprietary construction assemblies if the applicant fails to provide a
8 preliminary life safety report. Such report shall be prepared by a
9 licensed professional engineer or fire protection engineer and shall
10 certify that the proposed design, including means of egress and fire-
11 rated separations, is capable of compliance with the State Building Code
12 and State Fire Safety Code without requiring a modification to the
13 proposed design. The commission may require a third-party peer
14 review of said report at the applicant's expense.
This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
