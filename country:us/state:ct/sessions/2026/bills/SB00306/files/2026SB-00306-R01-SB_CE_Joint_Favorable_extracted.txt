General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 306
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT AND
COMPREHENSIVE ENERGY STRATEGY.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 16a-3d of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (a) On or before October 1, 2020, and every four years thereafter, the
4 Commissioner of Energy and Environmental Protection, in consultation
5 with the Commissioner of Economic and Community Development,
6 shall prepare a Comprehensive Energy Strategy. Said strategy shall
7 reflect the legislative findings and policy stated in section 16a-35k,
8 provide any analysis and recommendations necessary to guide the
9 state's energy policy to meet greenhouse gas emission reduction
10 requirements, as established in section 22a-200a, in the most cost-
11 effective manner and incorporate (1) an assessment and plan for all
12 energy needs in the state, including, but not limited to, electricity,
13 heating, cooling, and transportation, (2) the findings of the Integrated
14 Resources Plan, (3) the findings of the plan for energy efficiency adopted
15 pursuant to section 16-245m, (4) the findings of the plan for renewable
16 energy adopted pursuant to section 16-245n, and (5) the Energy
17 Assurance Plan developed for the state of Connecticut pursuant to the
18 American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009, P.L. 111-5, or any
19 successor Energy Assurance Plan developed within a reasonable time
20 prior to the preparation of any Comprehensive Energy Strategy. Said

52 the department's Internet web site. Not later than fifteen days prior to
53 any such public meeting and not less than thirty days prior to any such
