AN ACT AMENDING A CONVEYANCE OF A PARCEL OF STATE
LAND TO THE CHESPROCOTT HEALTH DISTRICT.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Special act 22-18 is amended to read as follows (Effective
2 from passage):
3 (a) Notwithstanding any provision of the general statutes, the
4 Commissioner of Correction shall convey to the Chesprocott Health
5 District a parcel of land located in the town of Cheshire, at a cost equal
6 to the administrative costs of making such conveyance. Said parcel of
7 land has an area of approximately 2 acres and is identified as a portion
8 of the approximately 166.95 acre parcel containing the Cheshire
9 Correctional Institute, a portion of Block 57 on Town of Cheshire Tax
10 Assessor's Map 36, and adjoining the parcel conveyed pursuant to
11 section 2 of special act 14-23. The conveyance shall be subject to the
12 approval of the State Properties Review Board.
13 (b) The Chesprocott Health District shall use said parcel of land for
14 purposes of building a regional health district, including to house
