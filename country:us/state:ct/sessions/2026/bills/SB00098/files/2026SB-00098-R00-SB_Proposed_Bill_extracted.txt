AN ACT ESTABLISHING A CONNECTICUT DAIRY FARMER TAX
CREDIT.

Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to establish a dairy farmer tax
2 credit similar to the Massachusetts dairy farmer tax credit.

To establish a tax credit for dairy farmers in the state.
