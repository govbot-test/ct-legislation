General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 66
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3051
Referred to Committee on EXECUTIVE AND LEGISLATIVE
NOMINATIONS
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF RICHARD H.
STATE DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION ARBITRATION PANEL.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Richard H.
2 Kosinski of Oxford, to be reappointed a member of the State Department
3 of Education Arbitration Panel, as a neutral arbitrator, to serve a term
4 ending June 30, 2029, or until a successor is appointed and has qualified,
5 whichever is longer, is hereby approved and confirmed.
