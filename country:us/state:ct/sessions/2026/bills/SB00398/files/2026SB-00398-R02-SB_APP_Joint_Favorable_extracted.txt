AN ACT CONCERNING THE BOARD OF PARDONS AND PAROLES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Subsection (c) of section 54-124a of the general statutes is
2 repealed and the following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective July 1,
3 2026):
4 (c) Ten of the members of the board shall devote full time to the
5 performance of their duties under this section and shall be compensated
6 therefor in such amount as the Commissioner of Administrative
7 Services determines, subject to the provisions of section 4-40. The other
8 members of the board shall receive, [one hundred ten dollars for each
9 day spent in] within available appropriations, a per diem payment in
10 such amount as the Commissioner of Administrative Services
11 determines, subject to the provisions of section 4-40, for the performance
12 of their duties and additionally shall be reimbursed for necessary
13 expenses incurred in the performance of such duties. The chairperson
14 or, in the chairperson's absence or inability to act, a member designated
15 by the chairperson to serve temporarily as chairperson, shall be present
16 at all meetings of the board and participate in all decisions.
