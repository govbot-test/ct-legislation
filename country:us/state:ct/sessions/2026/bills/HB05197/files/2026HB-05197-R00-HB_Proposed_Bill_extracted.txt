General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5197
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 899
Referred to Committee on HUMAN SERVICES
Introduced by:
REP. DEMICCO, 21st Dist.
AN ACT EQUALIZING ADULT ACCESS TO MEDICAID.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 To amend title 17b of the general statutes to (1) set equivalent income
2 eligibility standards for adults age nineteen and over in the HUSKY A,
3 C and D programs, and (2) eliminate consideration of assets in eligibility
4 determinations.
Statement of Purpose:
To provide equal access to Medicaid-funded health care for older adults
and adults with disabilities.
