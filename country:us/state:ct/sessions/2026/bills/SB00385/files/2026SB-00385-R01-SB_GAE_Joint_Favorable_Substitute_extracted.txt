February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING THE TITLING OF BILLS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. Section 2-56 of the general statutes is repealed and the
2 following is substituted in lieu thereof (Effective October 1, 2026):
3 (a) The legislative commissioners shall:
4 [(a)] (1) Collect and compare the laws of this and other states

8 information relating to any matter which is the subject of proposed

16 disapproval;
