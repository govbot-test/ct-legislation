Statement of Purpose:
To (1) establish the crime of female genital mutilation, (2) allow for
testimony outside of the courtroom for victims twelve years of age or
younger, (3) prohibit a witness who is a victim of female genital
mutilation from being automatically adjudged incompetent due to age,
(4) authorize a civil action by a victim, (5) abrogate immunity between
a parent and child if the action is brought by the child against the parent,
and (6) allow such actions within thirty years from the victim attaining
the age of eighteen years.
