Resolution No. 29

SEN. WINFIELD, 10th Dist.
REP. STAFSTROM, 129th Dist.
EUGENE R. CALISTRO, JR. OF GUILFORD TO BE A JUDGE OF THE
SUPERIOR COURT.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of the
2 Honorable Eugene R. Calistro, Jr. of Guilford to be a Judge of the
3 Superior Court, to serve for a term of eight years from May 3, 2026, is
4 approved and confirmed, and he is appointed a Judge of the Superior
5 Court for said term.
