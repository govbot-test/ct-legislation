General Assembly Proposed Bill No. 5013
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 169
Referred to Committee on FINANCE, REVENUE AND
BONDING
Introduced by:
REP. NUCCIO, 53rd Dist.
AN ACT ESTABLISHING A FEE AND TAX RELATED TO ELECTRIC
VEHICLES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 That the general statutes be amended to (1) establish a registration fee
2 for electric vehicles and plug-in hybrid electric vehicles and a per-
3 kilowatt-hour tax on electricity purchased at public electric vehicle
4 charging stations, and (2) require the revenue generated from such tax
5 and fee to be deposited in the Special Transportation Fund.
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) establish a registration fee for electric vehicles and plug-in hybrid
electric vehicles and a per-kilowatt-hour tax on electricity purchased at
public electric vehicle charging stations, and (2) require the revenue
generated from such tax and fee to be deposited in the Special
Transportation Fund.
