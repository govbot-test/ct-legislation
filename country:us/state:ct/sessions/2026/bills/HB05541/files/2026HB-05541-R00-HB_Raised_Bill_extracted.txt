AN ACT CONCERNING COMMUNITY ACTION AGENCIES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (Effective from passage) As used in this section, "community
2 action agency" has the same meaning as provided in section 17b-885 of
3 the general statutes. Not later than October 1, 2026, the Commissioner
4 of Social Services shall file a status report on community action agencies,
5 in accordance with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes,
6 with the joint standing committee of the General Assembly having
7 cognizance of matters relating to human services. The report shall
8 include, but need not be limited to:
9 (1) The number of community action agencies serving the state
10 compared to the number of such agencies serving the state in calendar
11 year 2021;
12 (2) To the extent not exempt from disclosure pursuant to section 1-
13 210 of the general statutes, a summary of any investigation and findings
14 of such investigation into a community action agency by the
15 Department of Social Services in each of the last two calendar years;
