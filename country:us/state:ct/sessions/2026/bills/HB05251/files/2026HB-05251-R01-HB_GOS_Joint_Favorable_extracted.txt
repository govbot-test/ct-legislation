General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 5251
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING REPORTING ON IMPLEMENTATION OF
CERTAIN CONSULTANT REPORT RECOMMENDATIONS.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective from passage) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "Consultant" means any person who (A) (i) is a professional who
3 is registered or licensed to practice such profession in accordance with
4 the applicable provisions of the general statutes, or (ii) is a planner or an
5 environmental, management or financial specialist, and (B) provides
6 consultant services to a state agency pursuant to a contract with such
7 state agency, including, but not limited to, any architect, professional
8 engineer, accountant, planner or environmental, management or
9 financial specialist;
10 (2) "Consultant services" includes administrative, planning, analysis,
11 statistical or research services rendered by any consultant, as well as
12 incidental services that members of such consultant's profession and
13 those in their employ are authorized to perform, for purposes of
14 recommending a state agency course of action;
15 (3) "Firm" means any individual, partnership, corporation, joint
16 venture, association or other legal entity authorized by law to offer
17 consultant services; and
18 (4) "State agency" means any office, department, board, council,
