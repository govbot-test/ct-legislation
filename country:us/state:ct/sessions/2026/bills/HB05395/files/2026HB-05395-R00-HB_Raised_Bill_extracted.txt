AN ACT CONCERNING MODULAR OR PREFABRICATED HOMES.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) (a) As used in this section:
2 (1) "Modular or prefabricated home" means a completely assembled
3 and erected building or structure, including prefabricated unit service
4 equipment, of which the structural parts consist of prefabricated units
5 or prefabricated subassemblies. "Modular or prefabricated home" does
6 not include any mobile manufactured home, as defined in section 21-64
7 of the general statutes;
8 (2) "Prefabricated subassembly" means a built-up combination of
9 several structural elements designed and fabricated as an assembled
10 section of wall, ceiling, floor or roof to be incorporated into a building
11 or structure by on-site erection of two or more such subassemblies;
12 (3) "Prefabricated unit" means a built-up section forming an
13 individual structural element of a building or structure, such as a beam,
14 girder, plank, strut, column or truss, the integrated parts of which are
15 prefabricated, including the necessary means for erection and

This act shall take effect as follows and shall amend the following
sections:
Section 1 October 1, 2026 New section
Sec. 2 October 1, 2026 8-30g(l)(6)
Statement of Purpose:
To (1) require that zoning regulations allow the development of
modular or prefabricated homes as of right on any lot that allows for
use as a single-family home, and (2) award one-quarter housing unit-
equivalent point for each modular or prefabricated home developed as
of right.
