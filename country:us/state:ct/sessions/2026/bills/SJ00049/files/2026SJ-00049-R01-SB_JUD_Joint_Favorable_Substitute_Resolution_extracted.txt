General Assembly Substitute Senate Joint
Resolution No. 49
February Session, 2026
RESOLUTION VACATING THE DECISION OF THE CLAIMS
COMMISSIONER TO DENY THE CLAIM AGAINST THE STATE OF
WILHELMINA MCLAURIN, ADMINISTRATRIX OF THE ESTATE OF
LEONARD MCLAURIN AND GRANTING THE CLAIMANT PERMISSION
TO SUE THE STATE.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the decision of the Claims Commissioner, file number 26516 of
2 said commissioner, ordering the denial of the claim against the state of
3 Wilhelmina McLaurin, Administratrix of the Estate of Leonard
4 McLaurin, is vacated. In accordance with the provisions of subsections
5 (b) and (c) of section 4-159 of the general statutes, the General Assembly
6 grants the claimant permission to sue the state. The claimant's action
7 shall be brought not later than one year from the date of the final
8 adoption by the General Assembly of this resolution.
JUD Joint Favorable Subst.
