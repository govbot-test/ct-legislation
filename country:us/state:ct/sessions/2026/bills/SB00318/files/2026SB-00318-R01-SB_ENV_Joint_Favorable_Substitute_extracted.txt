General Assembly Substitute Bill No. 318
February Session, 2026
AN ACT CONCERNING ANIMAL WELFARE.
Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives in General
Assembly convened:
1 Section 1. (NEW) (Effective October 1, 2026) The Commissioner of
2 Agriculture, in consultation with the Office of Victim Services, shall
3 study the impact of the need for placement of companion animals on
4 victims of domestic violence. Such study shall include, but not be
5 limited to, the: (1) Existing resources available for victims of domestic
6 violence to find temporary or permanent placements for a companion
7 animal, (2) identification of one or more persons who may serve as a
8 contact person for victims of domestic violence in the state who need to
9 place their companion animal in the care of another person due to such
10 circumstance, and (3) feasibility of establishing a phone number or other
11 electronic communication line that is accessible twenty-four hours a day
12 for victims of domestic violence in the state who need to place their
13 companion animal in the care of another person due to such
14 circumstance. Not later than January 1, 2027, the commissioner shall
15 submit the findings and any associated recommendations concerning
16 such study to the joint standing committee of the General Assembly
17 having cognizance of matters related to the environment, in accordance
18 with the provisions of section 11-4a of the general statutes.
