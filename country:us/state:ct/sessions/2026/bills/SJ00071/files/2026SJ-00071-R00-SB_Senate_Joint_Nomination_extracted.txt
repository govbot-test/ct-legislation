General Assembly Senate Joint
Resolution No. 71
February Session, 2026 LCO No. 3149
Introduced by:
SEN. DUFF, 25th Dist.
REP. CONCEPCION, 4th Dist.
RESOLUTION CONFIRMING THE NOMINATION OF SHANNON
JACOVINO OF WEST HARTFORD TO BE DIRECTOR OF THE OFFICE
OF DEVELOPMENTAL SERVICES OMBUDSPERSON.
Resolved by this Assembly:
1 That the nomination by His Excellency, the Governor, of Shannon
2 Jacovino of West Hartford, to be reappointed Director of the Office of
3 Developmental Services Ombudsperson, to serve a term ending four
4 years from the date of confirmation, or until a successor is appointed
5 and has qualified, whichever is longer, is hereby approved and
6 confirmed.
LCO No. 3149 1 of 1
