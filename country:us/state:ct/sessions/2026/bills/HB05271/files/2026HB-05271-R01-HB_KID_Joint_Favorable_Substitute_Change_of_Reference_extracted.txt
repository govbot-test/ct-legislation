AN ACT REQUIRING THE OFFICE OF THE CHILD ADVOCATE TO
HIRE CERTAIN ADDITIONAL EMPLOYEES.

Assembly convened:

2 2027, the Office of the Child Advocate shall hire one or more full-time
3 employees to assist in the evaluation of the delivery of services to
